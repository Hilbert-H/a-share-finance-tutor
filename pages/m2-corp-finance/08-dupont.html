DuPont 分解DuPont Decomposition

"ROE = 30% 是怎么来的?" — DuPont 把 ROE 拆成三个 (或五个) 可识别的来源, 让你看到一家公司的盈利能力到底来自销售利润率、资产周转率、还是杠杆。两家 ROE 相同的公司可能有截然相反的经营本质 — DuPont 让你看出来。 "ROE = 30% — how?" DuPont decomposes ROE into 3 (or 5) identifiable sources: margin, turnover, leverage. Two firms with the same ROE can have opposite economic engines — DuPont reveals which.

学习目标Objectives
  • 能用三因子 DuPont (Net Margin × Asset Turnover × Equity Multiplier) 分解任意公司 ROE。
  • 能用五因子 DuPont 进一步分解, 把利息费用与税务效应单独剥离。
  • 能识别"高 ROE 但低质量"模式 (高杠杆驱动, ROIC 低)。
  • Decompose ROE via 3‑factor DuPont (margin × turnover × multiplier).
  • Apply 5‑factor DuPont to isolate interest and tax effects.
  • Identify "high ROE but low quality" patterns (leverage‑driven, low ROIC).

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition

上一章里, 老王和老李算 CCC 算到一半, 老李突然问了一个让老王愣住的问题。

In the last chapter Wang and Old Li were calculating CCC when Li tossed out a question that stopped Wang cold.

1. 老李的疑问。 "老王, 我跟你比一比 — 我餐馆出资人投了 50,000 元, 一年挣 10,000 元, 回报率 20%。你这面馆出资人 (你 + 小张) 投了 19,000 元, 一年挣 3,800 元, 回报率也是 20%。我们俩看起来生意一样好, 对吧?" 老王点头, 然后又摇头: "看起来一样, 可生意根本不像。" — 这就引出了本章核心: 同样的"出资人回报率", 内部可能是完全不同的生意。出资人投了多少钱、每年回来多少, 这个比率就叫 净资产回报率 (Return on Equity, ROE)。回核: 老王 ROE = ?, 老李 ROE = ?

1. Li's question. "Wang, compare us — my restaurant's owners put in ¥50,000, made ¥10,000 this year, that's 20%. Your stall's owners (you + Zhang) put in ¥19,000, made ¥3,800, also 20%. Same business, right?" Wang nods, then shakes his head: "Same on paper, but not the same business at all." That's the core of this chapter: identical "owner return" can hide completely different businesses inside. The ratio of net profit to what owners put in is called Return on Equity (ROE). Recheck: Wang's ROE = ? Li's ROE = ?

2. 老王先看自己。 一碗面卖 10 元, 牛肉面粉成本 3 元, 人工 + 房租 + 杂费 5 元, 每碗净赚 2 元 → 净利率 20%。一年卖出 ~1,900 碗, 营收 19,000 元, 净利 3,800 元。店里总资产 (现金 + 设备 + 存货) 大约 22,800 元 (出资 19,000 + 银行借款 3,800)。资产 22,800 元一年支撑了 19,000 元营收 → 资产周转率 ≈ 0.83。出资人投了 19,000, 资产 22,800 → 权益乘数 ≈ 1.2(轻微杠杆)。三个数乘起来: 20% × 0.83 × 1.2 ≈ 20%, 与 ROE 一致。回核: 三项相乘 → 是不是等于 ROE?

2. Wang first. A bowl sells for ¥10; beef and flour ¥3, labor + rent + misc ¥5, net ¥2 per bowl → net margin 20%. About 1,900 bowls a year, revenue ¥19,000, net profit ¥3,800. Total assets (cash + equipment + inventory) about ¥22,800 (¥19,000 equity + ¥3,800 bank loan). Revenue ¥19,000 on assets ¥22,800 → asset turnover ≈ 0.83. Equity ¥19,000 supporting assets ¥22,800 → equity multiplier ≈ 1.2 (light leverage). Multiply: 20% × 0.83 × 1.2 ≈ 20%, matching the ROE. Recheck: three pieces multiplied → does it equal ROE?

3. 老李拆自己的。 老李做的是套餐, 每份卖 50 元但成本 38 元 (食材、服务、装修分摊), 净赚 12 元 → 净利率 24%。但他翻台率高, 一年卖 ~830 份套餐, 营收 41,500 元, 净利 ~10,000 元。餐馆总资产 100,000 元 (出资 50,000 + 银行借款 50,000) → 资产周转率 ≈ 0.42(因为餐馆装修、桌椅、厨房设备占资产大头)。权益乘数 = 100,000/50,000 = 2.0 (高杠杆, 50% 是银行钱)。三项相乘: 24% × 0.42 × 2.0 ≈ 20%, 与老李的 ROE 一致。

3. Li breaks down his own. Li sells set menus at ¥50, cost ¥38 (ingredients, service, depreciation), net ¥12 → net margin 24%. High turnover: ~830 sets a year, revenue ¥41,500, net ~¥10,000. Total assets ¥100,000 (¥50,000 equity + ¥50,000 bank debt) → asset turnover ≈ 0.42 (restaurant gear is heavy). Equity multiplier = 100,000/50,000 = 2.0 (half debt). Multiply: 24% × 0.42 × 2.0 ≈ 20%, matching Li's ROE.

4. 两人把数字摆一起。

4. Numbers side by side.

  • 老王: 净利率 20%, 周转 0.83, 杠杆 1.2 → ROE 20%
  • 老李: 净利率 24%, 周转 0.42, 杠杆 2.0 → ROE 20%
  • Wang: margin 20%, turnover 0.83, leverage 1.2 → ROE 20%
  • Li: margin 24%, turnover 0.42, leverage 2.0 → ROE 20%

老王盯着这两行数字, 忽然明白: 自己不靠杠杆、靠快周转; 老李靠较高利润率 + 银行钱放大。同样的 ROE, 背后完全不是一个故事。这种"把一个总指标拆成三个独立来源"的做法, 后面叫 杜邦分解 (DuPont decomposition): ROE = 净利率 × 资产周转率 × 权益乘数。回核: 这三项里, 老王和老李哪一项相同? 哪一项截然不同?

Wang stares at those two rows and gets it: he runs on light leverage and fast turnover; Li runs on higher margin amplified by bank debt. Same ROE, completely different stories. This technique — decomposing a composite into three independent sources — is the DuPont decomposition: ROE = net margin × asset turnover × equity multiplier. Recheck: which of the three is similar between Wang and Li? Which is very different?

5. 风险对照。 老李补充: "我的杠杆 2.0 意味着, 如果生意一年下滑 20%, 银行还款不能少, 我的净利就翻倍下滑到 40%。你杠杆 1.2, 同样下滑只损失 24%。" 老王恍然 — 同样 ROE, 风险敞口完全不同。两家店看起来一样, 但碰上经济下行, 老王的店活得更稳, 老李的店压力大得多。回核: 同 ROE 不同杠杆 → 哪家在下行周期更脆弱?

5. Risk contrast. Li adds: "My 2.0 leverage means if business drops 20% in a year, the bank payment doesn't shrink — my profit collapses by 40%. Your 1.2 only loses 24% under the same hit." Wang gets it — same ROE, very different risk exposure. The two shops look identical, but in a downturn Wang's is far more resilient. Recheck: same ROE, different leverage → which is more fragile in a downturn?

6. 老李最后一招: 想象第三家店。 "假如我把套餐价从 50 降到 40, 但翻台率从 1 天 2 桌升到 1 天 4 桌呢? 净利率从 24% 跌到 10%, 但资产周转从 0.42 升到 1.0 → ROE 还能保住吗? 10% × 1.0 × 2.0 = 20%。一样!" 老王点头: 这就是沃尔玛模式 vs 茅台模式—— 用薄利多销和厚利慢销可以得到完全一样的 ROE。沃尔玛、京东这种零售商, 净利率只有 2-3% 但周转极快; 茅台、爱马仕这种品牌, 净利率 50% 但周转慢。"同 ROE 不同模式"的判断, 必须靠 DuPont 拆解才看得见。回核: 老李"涨周转 + 降利率"后, 三项相乘是不是仍等于 20%?

6. Li's last move: imagine a third shop. "What if I drop the set from ¥50 to ¥40 but turn each table from 2 to 4 times a day? Margin falls from 24% to 10%, but asset turnover rises from 0.42 to 1.0 → ROE? 10% × 1.0 × 2.0 = 20%. Same!" Wang nods: that's the Walmart vs Moutai split — thin‑margin high‑turnover and fat‑margin slow‑turnover deliver identical ROEs. Walmart and JD post 2–3% margins on extreme turnover; Moutai and Hermès post 50% margins on slow turnover. The "same ROE, different engine" call requires DuPont to see. Recheck: after Li's "turnover up, margin down" swap, does the product still hit 20%?

回看一下: 老王和老李从"我俩 ROE 都是 20%, 谁更优秀?"这个问题出发, 把同一个数字拆到了三个独立来源—— 净利率 (定价权 / 成本)、资产周转 (资源利用效率)、权益乘数 (杠杆放大)。这三项相乘永远等于 ROE, 但来源完全不同的两家公司, 抗风险能力、估值倍数、护城河都截然不同。Layer 2 把这条等式形式化为三因子和五因子版本, 引入 ROIC (杠杆中性的真实回报); Layer 3 用茅台 (32% ROE, 几乎全部来自利润率) vs 京东方 (3% ROE, 周期性) vs 招行 (16% ROE, 杠杆驱动) 三家 A 股, 验证: 看 ROE 必看分解。

Look back. Starting from "we both made 20% ROE — who's better?", Wang and Li have decomposed one number into three independent sources — net margin (pricing/cost), asset turnover (resource efficiency), and equity multiplier (leverage). Their product is always ROE, but two firms with very different sources differ dramatically in risk, valuation, and moat. Layer 2 formalises this into 3‑factor and 5‑factor versions and introduces ROIC (leverage‑neutral). Layer 3 verifies with three A‑share names: Moutai (32% ROE almost entirely from margin), BOE (3% ROE, cyclical), CMB (16% ROE, leverage‑driven). The rule: never read ROE without decomposing it.

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

Layer 1 用老王 vs 老李"同 ROE 不同来源"的对话, 直观说明了 ROE 不能单看。现在把这条直觉形式化为标准的三因子 / 五因子 DuPont, 并引入 ROIC 作为杠杆中性的真实回报指标。

Layer 1 used Wang vs Li's "same ROE, different sources" exchange to show why ROE can't stand alone. We now formalise this into the standard 3‑factor and 5‑factor DuPont decompositions, and introduce ROIC as the leverage‑neutral measure of real return.

① 三因子 DuPont

① 3‑factor DuPont

$$ \mathrm{ROE} = \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{NI}}{\mathrm{Revenue}}}_{\text{Net Margin}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{Revenue}}{\mathrm{Assets}}}_{\text{Asset Turnover}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{Assets}}{\mathrm{Equity}}}_{\text{Equity Multiplier (Leverage)}} $$(8.1)

代数恒等式: 直接乘开 → ROE = NI / Equity。每个因子有独立经济含义:

Algebraic identity. Each factor has independent meaning:

  • Net Margin: 每元营收产生多少净利 → 反映定价权 + 成本控制
  • Asset Turnover: 每元资产产生多少营收 → 反映资产使用效率
  • Equity Multiplier: 每元权益支撑多少资产 = 1 + D/E → 反映杠杆

Margin = pricing/cost power; turnover = asset efficiency; multiplier = leverage.

② 五因子 DuPont (扩展)

② 5‑factor DuPont

$$ \mathrm{ROE} = \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{EBIT}}{\mathrm{Revenue}}}_{\text{Op. Margin}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{Revenue}}{\mathrm{Assets}}}_{\text{Turnover}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{Assets}}{\mathrm{Equity}}}_{\text{Multiplier}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{EBT}}{\mathrm{EBIT}}}_{\text{Interest Burden}} \times \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{NI}}{\mathrm{EBT}}}_{\text{Tax Burden}} $$(8.2)

把"净利率"再拆为"经营利润率 × 利息负担 × 税收负担"。其中:

Splits margin into operating + interest + tax:

  • Interest Burden = EBT/EBIT: 利息消耗了多少经营利润. 1.0 = 无利息支出 (零杠杆); 越小 = 利息越重
  • Tax Burden = NI/EBT: 税务影响. 1.0 = 不交税; 中国 25% 税率下 ~0.75

Interest Burden 1.0 = no interest; lower = more interest. Tax Burden 1.0 = no tax; 0.75 = typical China 25% rate.

③ DuPont 与 ROIC 的关系

③ DuPont vs ROIC

ROE 受杠杆影响, 比较跨杠杆公司不公平. ROIC (return on invested capital) = NOPAT / Invested Capital = 经营利润 (税后) / (债务 + 权益 − 现金) — 与资本结构无关。

ROE leverages‑sensitive; ROIC = NOPAT / Invested Capital is leverage‑neutral, fairer cross‑firm.

用法: ROE - ROIC 的差距, 反映了杠杆对 ROE 的放大程度。若 ROE = 30%, ROIC = 15% → 一半的"高 ROE"来自杠杆放大, 而非真实经营效率。投资者应警惕这种"杠杆驱动型高 ROE"。

ROE − ROIC measures leverage amplification. ROE 30% with ROIC 15% means half the ROE is leverage, not operations. Beware.

Layer 3 · A 股案例A‑Share Case

三家对照Three‑way茅台 vs 京东方 vs 招商银行 — ROE 同 ~15-20%, 来源完全不同Moutai vs BOE vs CMB

把老王 vs 老李那张"同 20% ROE, 引擎不同"的对照表放大到 A 股: 老王那种"利润率高、杠杆低"对应的是茅台; 老李"利润率中、杠杆高"对应的是招行; 还有第三种"利润率薄、周转中、杠杆中"的周期型企业 — 京东方。三家 ROE 数字看似可比, DuPont 一拆, 三种完全不同的生意。

Take the Wang‑vs‑Li "same 20%, different engine" table and scale it to A‑shares: Wang's "high margin, low leverage" maps to Moutai; Li's "decent margin, high leverage" maps to a bank like CMB; and there's a third archetype — thin‑margin cyclical — represented by BOE. Three superficially comparable ROEs, three radically different businesses once DuPont strips them apart.

理解检验题Trap Questions

  1. Q1. 两家公司 A 与 B 的 ROE 都是 20%。A: 净利率 10%, 周转 1, 杠杆 2。B: 净利率 4%, 周转 2.5, 杠杆 2。商业模式差异何在?Q1. Two firms with ROE 20%. A: margin 10%, turnover 1, leverage 2. B: margin 4%, turnover 2.5, leverage 2. Business model difference?

    A — 高利润率模式 (10% 净利率): 品牌或专利驱动, 单笔交易利润大但周转慢。典型: 高端消费品、专科药、高端机床、奢侈品。护城河: 品牌/专利。风险: 品牌价值波动 / 新竞争者破坏定价权。

    B — 高周转模式 (2.5 周转): 规模 + 效率驱动, 单笔利润薄但快进快出。典型: 超市零售、快销品、低端制造、贸易公司。护城河: 规模 + 运营效率。风险: 价格战 / 新进入者扰乱周转。

    两者投资逻辑不同:

    • A 估值: 通常给高 P/E (品牌价值持续) — Moutai 30x, LVMH 25x
    • B 估值: 通常给低 P/E (周期 + 竞争压力) — Walmart 25x, 京东 15x
    • 同 ROE 不同质量

    诊断方法: 看十年 DuPont 三因子的稳定性 — A 模式的净利率稳定性强 (品牌持久); B 模式的周转率稳定性强 (规模效应)。任何一个因子异常波动 = 商业模式受到冲击。

    A = high‑margin model (brand/IP); B = high‑turnover model (scale/efficiency). Different moats, different risks, different P/Es. Same ROE, different quality.

  2. Q2. 一家公司 ROE 25% 但 ROIC 8%。意味着什么? 该公司在杠杆下行周期 (利率上升或信贷收紧) 中会如何?Q2. ROE 25%, ROIC 8%. Meaning? How will it fare in tightening cycle?

    差距分析: ROE 25% − ROIC 8% = 17 个百分点的杠杆放大。意味着真实资本回报只有 8%, 但通过举债"借了 17% 的便宜"。

    合理性测试: ROIC 8% 是否高于资本成本 WACC? 若 WACC = 7%, ROIC 仅 1% 超过 → 公司其实勉强在创造价值; 加杠杆后 ROE 25% 看似漂亮, 但 ROIC 与 WACC 的窄差说明经营本身不强。

    利率上升时:

    • $r_d$ 上升 → 利息成本上升 → 净利下降
    • WACC 上升 → 若 ROIC 仍 8%, 但 WACC 升到 10% → 公司毁灭价值
    • 杠杆约束 → 借不到那么多债 → ROE 直接下降
    • 困境成本 → 信用利差扩大, 加速恶化

    诊断框架:

    1. ROIC vs WACC: 是否真的创造价值?
    2. ROE − ROIC: 杠杆放大幅度
    3. 利率敏感性: 利率 + 100bp → 净利变化
    4. 信用评级: 评级一旦下调, $r_d$ 跳跃

    典型行业: 房地产 (ROE 高来自杠杆 + 周转, ROIC 弱) 2021+ 暴雷; 部分基建公司 ROE 高来自国资担保 + 杠杆。这种"低 ROIC + 高杠杆"的公司在顺周期看似优秀, 逆周期暴露脆弱。

    ROE − ROIC = 17pp leverage amplification. Real ROIC 8% may barely beat WACC; tightening cycle squeezes from multiple directions (interest cost up, leverage capacity down, credit spread widens). Real estate / certain infrastructure firms exemplify "low ROIC + high leverage → looks good in expansion, exposed in contraction."

  3. Q3. 五因子 DuPont 把"Interest Burden"单列出来。给出该指标在 0.5 vs 0.9 vs 1.0 三种情形下的解读。Q3. Interpret Interest Burden at 0.5, 0.9, 1.0.

    Interest Burden = EBT/EBIT:

    • 1.0: EBT = EBIT, 即无利息费用。零杠杆 (茅台、长江电力等). 完全没有债务压力
    • 0.9: 利息消耗了 10% 的经营利润. 健康的中等杠杆 (海康、美的等). 利息保障倍数高
    • 0.5: 利息消耗了 50% 的经营利润. 危险。即使 EBIT 还可观, 大部分被利息吞掉, 剩下给股东的极少. 高杠杆 + 高利率 + 信用恶化的复合特征。该指标接近 0 → 公司即将"利息保障倍数 < 1"→ 偿债压力濒临

    分析价值: Interest Burden 是顺周期信号 — 周期下行时 EBIT 下降 + 利息固定 → 指标快速恶化。监控其 8 个季度的趋势, 比看绝对值更敏感。

    1.0 = no interest (zero leverage); 0.9 = healthy (interest takes 10% of EBIT); 0.5 = danger (interest eats half). Approaching 0 → coverage < 1, distress. Monitor 8‑quarter trend.
