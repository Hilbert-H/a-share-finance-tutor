财务造假识别Detecting Financial Fraud

M1 教会你读财报, M2 前 9 章教会你用财报。本章是终极工具 — 用量化方法判断财报本身的可信度。Beneish M-Score 检测盈余操纵, Altman Z-Score 预测破产, Piotroski F-Score 筛选质量。康美、康得新、獐子岛在暴雷前 1-2 年这些指标都已亮红灯。 M1 taught how to read; M2 Ch.01–09 taught how to use. This chapter is the capstone — quantitative tests of the financial statements' own credibility. Beneish M for earnings manipulation, Altman Z for bankruptcy, Piotroski F for quality. Kangmei, Kangde Xin, Zhangzidao all flashed red 1–2 years before collapse.

学习目标Objectives
  • 能逐项计算 Beneish 八因子 M-Score, 知道 > −2.22 触发盈余操纵嫌疑。
  • 能计算 Altman Z-Score, 知道 < 1.81 触发破产警报区。
  • 能用 Piotroski F-Score 9 维度筛选高质量股票。
  • 能用应计盈余比 (Sloan 1996) 作为最简单的造假初筛。
  • Compute Beneish 8‑factor M‑Score; threshold > −2.22.
  • Compute Altman Z‑Score; distress zone < 1.81.
  • Apply Piotroski F‑Score (9 dimensions).
  • Use Sloan accruals ratio as a one‑number screen.

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition

上一章里, 老王让银行经理小赵 5 分钟读懂了自家店面的健康度。今天故事反过来 — 老王要去读别人的账本。隔壁巷子的老周想退休, 把他的小餐馆 (开了 8 年, 比老王店更大) 卖给老王, 开价 100,000 元。老周拍着胸脯说: "我账本一清二楚, 你随便看。" 老王翻开账本, 数字看上去都正—— 但他心里有点不踏实, 决定一项一项查。

In the last chapter Wang let bank manager Zhao read his stall's health in five minutes. Today the story flips — Wang has to read someone else's books. Old Zhou, who runs a slightly larger restaurant down the alley, wants to retire and is offering his 8‑year‑old shop to Wang for ¥100,000. "My books are clean," Zhou insists. "Look at anything you want." Wang opens the ledger. The numbers look reasonable — but something nags. He decides to check line by line.

1. 老王先看应收账款。 老周报告: 营收 80,000 元, 应收账款 16,000 元 — 大约 73 天的 DSO (16,000 / 80,000 × 365)。老王想了想: 老周这种小餐馆基本应该现结, 偶尔挂一两单, DSO 顶多 10-15 天才正常。73 天的应收异常得让人警觉。再翻三年前的账: 当时营收 50,000 元, 应收只有 2,000 元 — DSO 仅 15 天。应收账款相对营收的扩张速度, 远高于营收本身的增长。这是第一个红旗。老王问自己: 老周是不是为了把营收数字做漂亮, 把"还没收到钱"的销售也算进去了? 这种"应收异常增长"现象有个名字, 叫 应收/销售指数 (DSRI, Days Sales in Receivables Index) 异常。回核: 应收周转突然变慢 → 可能信号?

1. Wang starts with receivables. Zhou reports revenue ¥80,000, AR ¥16,000 — about 73 days of DSO (16,000 / 80,000 × 365). Wang thinks: a small restaurant like Zhou's mostly takes cash on the spot — DSO of 10–15 days at most. 73 days is alarming. Wang flips back three years: revenue ¥50,000, AR ¥2,000 — DSO just 15 days. AR is growing far faster than revenue itself. Red flag #1. Wang asks: is Zhou inflating revenue by booking sales that never collected? This pattern has a name: an abnormal DSRI (Days Sales in Receivables Index). Recheck: AR turnover suddenly slowing → what does it signal?

2. 老王再看毛利率。 三年前毛利率 35%, 今年突然跳到 52% — 同行业 (本地小餐馆) 平均 38%, 而且原材料这两年涨价。老周怎么逆势把毛利率拉这么高? 老王问老周: "你成本是不是少算了?" 老周笑笑: "我谈了好供应商。" 但老王知道这条巷子里的供应商就那么几家, 价格不可能差这么多。毛利率异常跳升常常意味着: 要么成本被刻意低估 (例如把原材料挂账到下年, 不计入当年成本), 要么收入被虚增 (营收涨得快, 但成本没同步涨)。这就是第二个红旗。回核: 毛利率跳升而行业不升 → 暗示什么?

2. Wang checks gross margin. Three years ago, gross margin was 35%; this year it jumps to 52% — local restaurant peers average 38%, and raw materials have inflated over those years. How did Zhou raise margins against the wind? Wang asks: "Are you understating costs?" Zhou smiles: "Good supplier deals." But Wang knows this alley — only a few suppliers, prices can't differ this much. An anomalous gross margin spike usually means: costs are quietly deferred (raw materials parked in next year's books) or revenue is inflated (sales up, costs not matching). Red flag #2. Recheck: gross margin jumping while industry doesn't → suggests what?

3. 关键的一步: 把利润对照现金流。 老王问老周: "今年净利 12,000 元, 那经营现金流大概多少?" 老周翻账: "经营现金流 ~2,000 元, 不太多。" — 老王心头一紧。净利 12,000 而现金只收回 2,000, 意味着 10,000 元利润是纸面的 (挂在应收、存货、预付的形式上, 还没变成现金)。这种"利润和现金背离"是所有造假最敏感的指标 (M1 第二个思维模型: accrual vs cash)。一个学者叫 Sloan 发现: 这种"高应计盈余比"的公司, 下一年股票回报普遍跑输市场 ~10%。老王在草纸上算: 应计盈余比 = (12,000 − 2,000) / 平均资产 80,000 = 12.5% — 极高, 异常。第三个红旗。回核: 净利 12,000, 现金流仅 2,000 → 利润是真的还是纸面的?

3. The key step: profit vs cash flow. Wang asks: "Net profit ¥12,000 — how much operating cash?" Zhou flips: "OCF about ¥2,000." Wang tightens. NI ¥12,000 but only ¥2,000 of cash — the other ¥10,000 of "profit" is paper (parked in AR, inventory, prepaids, never converting). This "profit vs cash divergence" is the single most sensitive fraud signal (M1's second mental model: accrual vs cash). A researcher named Sloan found that firms with high accruals underperform the market by ~10% the following year. Wang scribbles: accruals ratio = (12,000 − 2,000) / avg assets 80,000 = 12.5% — extremely high, anomalous. Red flag #3. Recheck: NI ¥12,000 but OCF ¥2,000 → real or paper profit?

4. 老王翻到资产负债表底部, 检查"其他资产"。 三年前老周账上没有"长期待摊费用"项, 今年这一项突然挂了 8,000 元。老王问: "这 8,000 元是什么?" 老周: "店面装修费, 我分 5 年摊销。"老王想了想: 一个 80,000 元营收的小餐馆, 装修一次摊 8,000 元 → 摊销期延 5 年, 每年只扣 1,600 元成本。如果一次扣完, 今年净利会立刻从 12,000 跌到 4,400。老周把"应该当年扣的成本"资本化成了一项长期资产, 把当年利润垫高了。这种"激进资本化"的做法, 学术上叫资产质量指数 (AQI) 异常。第四个红旗。回核: 不该资本化的成本被资本化 → 当年利润是高估还是低估?

4. Wang flips to "other assets." Three years ago Zhou had no "long‑term deferred expense" line; this year ¥8,000 sits there. Wang: "What's this?" Zhou: "Renovation, amortising over 5 years." Wang thinks: an ¥80,000‑revenue stall amortising ¥8,000 over 5 years → only ¥1,600 hits expense each year. If expensed in full, net profit would drop from ¥12,000 to ¥4,400 today. Zhou has capitalised what should have been a current expense, inflating this year's profit. This pattern of aggressive capitalisation is the abnormal Asset Quality Index (AQI). Red flag #4. Recheck: cost that shouldn't be capitalised gets capitalised → current profit overstated or understated?

5. 最后一项: 现金真假。 老周账上"货币资金"30,000 元, 老王说要看银行流水。老周犹豫了一下: "流水有点麻烦, 我手抄给你看吧。" — 老王立刻警觉。任何不愿意提供银行流水原件的卖方, 都可能在虚增现金。真现金是最难造假的科目 (因为银行流水第三方可验证), 但 A 股历史上多次出现"账上 100 亿现金, 实际银行账户空空"的暴雷 — 康得新 122 亿存款"无法使用"、康美 200+ 亿虚增货币资金都是典型。第五个红旗 — 而且是最致命的。回核: 卖方不愿出示银行流水 → 信任度该往哪个方向走?

5. Last item: is the cash real? Zhou's ledger shows ¥30,000 of "cash and equivalents." Wang asks to see bank statements. Zhou hesitates: "Originals are inconvenient — I'll copy them by hand." Wang's alarm sounds. Any seller unwilling to provide raw bank statements may be inflating cash. Real cash is the hardest line to fake (third‑party verifiable), yet A‑share history has plenty of "¥10bn on the books, empty in the bank" cases — Kangde Xin's ¥12.2bn "unusable deposits," Kangmei's ¥20bn+ of fictitious cash. Red flag #5 — and the most lethal. Recheck: seller won't show original bank statements → which way does trust go?

6. 五个红旗加起来。 老王在草纸上列出来:

Five red flags add up. Wang lists them:

  • 应收账款相对营收暴涨 (DSRI 异常) — 收入可能虚增
  • 毛利率反常上跳 (GMI 异常) — 成本可能低估
  • 净利 12,000 但 OCF 仅 2,000 (高应计) — 利润是纸面的
  • 激进资本化 (AQI 异常) — 当年费用塞到资产里
  • 不愿出示银行流水 — 现金可能是假的
  • AR jumps vs revenue (DSRI anomaly) — revenue likely inflated
  • Gross margin spikes against industry (GMI anomaly) — cost likely understated
  • NI ¥12,000 vs OCF ¥2,000 (high accruals) — paper profit
  • Aggressive capitalisation (AQI anomaly) — expenses parked in assets
  • Won't show bank statements — cash possibly fake

老王把账本合上, 摇头: "老周, 你这账本我读出来五处不对, 100,000 元我不敢接。要不你拿真账本来, 价咱再谈?" — 这就是多信号融合 (multi‑signal aggregation) 的精髓: 单一红旗可能是误报, 五个红旗同时亮 = 几乎不可能误报。把这五条 (再加几条) 加权打分, 就是学术界的 Beneish M-Score (盈余操纵); 把"是否破产"相关的五项财务指标加权打分, 就是 Altman Z-Score; 把"基本面质量"九项二元指标加总, 就是 Piotroski F-Score。Layer 2 给出三个分数的完整公式与阈值; Layer 3 用康美、康得新、獐子岛三个真实暴雷案例验证: 这些指标在暴雷前 1-2 年都已经亮了红灯。

Wang closes the ledger and shakes his head: "Zhou, five things don't add up. I can't pay ¥100,000. Show me the real books and we'll talk price." That's multi‑signal aggregation in a nutshell: one red flag can be noise; five at once is almost never noise. Weight these five (plus a few more) and you get the academic Beneish M‑Score (earnings manipulation); weight five bankruptcy‑related ratios and you get Altman Z‑Score; sum nine binary quality checks and you get Piotroski F‑Score. Layer 2 gives the full formulas and thresholds; Layer 3 verifies with three real A‑share collapses — Kangmei, Kangde Xin, and Zhangzidao — all of which flashed red 1–2 years before the blow‑up.

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

Layer 1 用老王查老周账本时发现的五个红旗, 直观展示了"多信号融合"的逻辑。现在把这种思想形式化为三个学术评分: Beneish M-Score (盈余操纵)、Altman Z-Score (破产困境)、Piotroski F-Score (质量), 再加上一个最简的 Sloan 应计盈余比。

Layer 1 used Wang's five red flags while inspecting Zhou's books to show the "multi‑signal aggregation" logic. We now formalise this into three academic scores — Beneish M (earnings manipulation), Altman Z (bankruptcy distress), Piotroski F (quality) — plus the simplest one‑number Sloan accruals ratio.

① Beneish M-Score (8 因子)

① Beneish M‑Score (8 factors)

$$ M = -4.84 + 0.92\,\mathrm{DSRI} + 0.528\,\mathrm{GMI} + 0.404\,\mathrm{AQI} + 0.892\,\mathrm{SGI} + 0.115\,\mathrm{DEPI} - 0.172\,\mathrm{SGAI} + 4.679\,\mathrm{TATA} - 0.327\,\mathrm{LVGI} $$(10.1)

阈值: $M > -2.22$ → 存在盈余操纵嫌疑 (Beneish 实证: 操纵公司平均 M = −1.78, 干净公司 M = −2.84)

Threshold: $M > -2.22$ flags manipulation risk. Manipulators average −1.78; clean firms −2.84.

Beneish, M.D. (1999) "The Detection of Earnings Manipulation," Financial Analysts Journal

② Altman Z-Score

② Altman Z‑Score

$$ Z = 1.2 X_1 + 1.4 X_2 + 3.3 X_3 + 0.6 X_4 + 1.0 X_5 $$(10.2)

变量:

  • $X_1$ = 营运资本 / 总资产 (流动性)
  • $X_2$ = 留存收益 / 总资产 (积累的盈利能力)
  • $X_3$ = EBIT / 总资产 (资产经营回报)
  • $X_4$ = 股权市值 / 总负债 (市场估值杠杆)
  • $X_5$ = 营收 / 总资产 (资产周转)

阈值:

  • $Z > 2.99$: 安全区
  • $1.81 < Z < 2.99$: 灰色区
  • $Z < 1.81$: 困境区 (2 年内破产概率 > 50%)

$Z > 2.99$ safe; 1.81–2.99 grey; $< 1.81$ distress (50%+ bankruptcy in 2y).

Altman, E.I. (1968) "Financial Ratios, Discriminant Analysis and the Prediction of Corporate Bankruptcy"

Altman Z' for private firms (book equity replaces market):

$$ Z' = 0.717 X_1 + 0.847 X_2 + 3.107 X_3 + 0.42 X_4' + 0.998 X_5 $$(10.3)

阈值: > 2.9 安全, 1.23-2.9 灰色, < 1.23 困境.

③ Piotroski F-Score (9 因子, 0-9 分)

③ Piotroski F‑Score (9 binary factors)

每个因子满足得 1 分, 否则 0 分。总分 0-9:

Each factor: 1 if satisfied, 0 otherwise. Total 0–9:

  1. 当年 ROA > 0
  2. 当年 OCF > 0
  3. 当年 ROA > 上一年
  4. OCF > NI (应计质量好)
  5. 当年长期债务 / 总资产 < 上一年 (去杠杆)
  6. 当年流动比率 > 上一年
  7. 没有发行新股 (无股东稀释)
  8. 当年毛利率 > 上一年
  9. 当年总资产周转率 > 上一年

9 binary criteria: ROA > 0; OCF > 0; ROA rising; OCF > NI; LT‑debt/Assets falling; Current ratio rising; no equity issuance; gross margin rising; asset turnover rising.

用法: 在低 P/B 价值股中筛选 F > 7 的"高质量价值"组合, Piotroski (2000) 证明该组合 5 年累计回报远超随机选低 P/B 股。

Use: within low‑P/B value stocks, pick F > 7. Piotroski (2000) showed 5y returns far exceed random low‑P/B picks.

Piotroski, J.D. (2000) "Value Investing: The Use of Historical Financial Statement Information"

④ Sloan 应计盈余比 (最简版本)

④ Sloan accruals ratio (simplest)

$$ \mathrm{Accruals\,Ratio} = \frac{\mathrm{NI} - \mathrm{OCF}}{\mathrm{Avg\,Total\,Assets}} $$(10.4)

解读: 高应计 = NI 远高于 OCF = "纸面利润". Sloan (1996) 实证: 高应计公司未来 1 年 -10% α (相对低应计公司). 这就是著名的"应计盈余异象"。

High accruals = NI >> OCF = paper profit. Sloan (1996): high‑accrual firms underperform by ~−10% next year — the accruals anomaly.

Sloan, R.G. (1996) "Do Stock Prices Fully Reflect Information in Accruals and Cash Flows About Future Earnings?"

SZ·002069獐子岛 — "扇贝跑了"Zhangzidao — "scallops swam away"应计盈余的极端案例Extreme accruals

獐子岛 2014, 2017, 2019 三次公告"扇贝大量死亡 / 跑了 / 莫名消失", 每次计提巨额存货跌价损失。这是典型的应计盈余操纵: 把"存货"作为利润蓄水池, 好年份不计提虚增利润, 差年份集中计提"清零", 形成 big bath。

Zhangzidao announced "scallops died/escaped/vanished" in 2014, 2017, 2019 — each time massive inventory write‑downs. Classic accruals manipulation: use inventory as a profit reservoir, defer impairments in good years, lump them as big baths in bad years.

Sloan 应计盈余比 2013-2014 年间从 +5% (正常偏高) 飙升到 +15% (极端高) → 强烈预警。投资者使用 (10.4) 简单公式即可在 2014 暴雷前发现异常。

Sloan accruals ratio jumped from +5% (somewhat high) to +15% (extreme) in 2013–14 — strong pre‑collapse warning available via the simple (10.4) formula.

  1. Q1. 一家公司 M-Score = −1.8 (高于 −2.22 阈值)。这必然是造假公司吗? 该如何使用 M-Score 作为投资工具?Q1. M‑Score = −1.8 (above −2.22). Must this firm be a fraud? How should one use M‑Score?

    不必然。Beneish (1999) 模型在样本内分类准确率 ~76%, 即:

    • 真正操纵的公司中, ~76% 被正确识别为操纵 (M > −2.22)
    • 但未操纵的公司中也有 ~17% 被误判为操纵 (假阳性)
    • 反之, 真正操纵公司中有 ~24% 被漏判 (假阴性)

    误判来源: 真实高速成长公司 (营收暴涨 + 应收账款扩张 + 资产增长) 会被模型"误伤" — 看起来像操纵。例如早期快速增长的零售商、SaaS 公司。

    正确用法:

    1. 筛选不是结论: M-Score 高仅是"开始调查"的信号, 不是"必然有问题"的判决
    2. 结合定性: 看附注关联方交易、审计意见、问询函历史、行业地位 等定性证据
    3. 多年趋势: 连续 3 年 M-Score 上升 比单年高更可靠
    4. 组合多指标: M-Score + Z-Score + F-Score + 现金流性格 (Ch.M1.08) 综合判断 — 任一指标异常单独不足, 多指标同向异常才高度可信
    5. 反向使用: 用 M-Score < −2.84 作为排除可疑的高质量筛, 而非"M > −2.22 必造假"的肯定式判断

    实务: 量化对冲基金会用 M-Score 作为做空候选库的初筛, 再做深度尽调; 长期价值投资者会用 M-Score 作为避雷工具, 排除高 M 公司。

    No. Beneish 1999 in‑sample accuracy ~76% — 17% false‑positives (fast‑growing firms with rising AR/assets), 24% false‑negatives. Use as a screen, not a verdict. Combine with notes, audit opinions, inquiry history, industry context. Trend (3y) more reliable than single year. Combined with Z and F scores plus cash flow personality, multi‑indicator alignment gives high confidence. Practical: quant short‑bias use it as candidate screen; long‑term value investors use it to avoid.

  2. Q2. Altman Z-Score 在银行业和互联网行业都几乎无意义。给出原因。Q2. Altman Z is nearly useless for banks and internet firms. Why?

    Altman 1968 原始模型基于制造业上市公司样本(美国, 33 家破产 + 33 家健康对照). 模型权重反映制造业的破产驱动机制 — 营运资本 + 资产周转 + 经营杠杆.

    银行业失效:

    • $X_1$ (营运资本/资产) — 银行无传统"营运资本"概念 (Ch.M2.07 讨论 CCC 在房地产同理). 公式直接套用得到接近 0 或极小值
    • $X_4$ (市值/负债) — 银行业天然高杠杆 (D/E 10+), $X_4$ 极小, 但这是行业结构而非困境信号
    • $X_5$ (周转) — 银行营收/资产比 ~3-5%, 远低于制造业 50-100%
    • 结果: 健康银行 Z 也会落入"困境区"

    互联网/科技失效:

    • $X_2$ (留存收益/资产) — 早期科技公司大量烧钱, 累计亏损 → $X_2$ 为负或极小
    • $X_3$ (EBIT/资产) — 未盈利期 $X_3$ 为负
    • $X_5$ (周转) — 重资产 (服务器、研发) + 营收尚未爆发 → 低周转
    • 但这些公司可能融资充足 + 商业模式有效, 实际破产风险低

    正确替代:

    • 银行: 看监管资本充足率 (CET1, AT1, Total Capital), 不良贷款率, 拨备覆盖率. Z-Score 不适用
    • 科技初创: 看"现金跑道" (cash runway = cash / monthly burn), 融资节奏, 单位经济效益 (LTV/CAC). Z-Score 也不适用
    • Altman 2002 Z'' 修订版: 针对非制造业, 但仍不适合银行 / 重监管行业

    启示: 任何统计模型都有其训练数据的特征局限. 跨行业 / 跨时代直接套用会产生系统性偏差. 应该用同行业 同结构的对照基准。

    Altman 1968 trained on US manufacturers. $X_1$ irrelevant for banks (no "working capital" concept); $X_4$ low by industry structure; $X_5$ low. Healthy banks fall into distress zone. Tech: $X_2$ negative (accumulated losses), $X_3$ negative pre‑profit, $X_5$ low. Use instead: bank capital adequacy / NPL / coverage; tech cash runway / burn / LTV‑CAC. Any statistical model bears training‑sample limits; cross‑industry transfer needs caution.

  3. Q3. 公司 NI = 100, OCF = 30, 总资产平均 = 1000. 算 Sloan 应计盈余比. 用 Sloan (1996) 实证结论判断该股票下一年预期回报相对市场如何?Q3. NI=100, OCF=30, Assets=1000. Sloan ratio? Expected next‑year return vs market per Sloan (1996)?

    $\mathrm{Accruals\,Ratio} = (NI - OCF)/A = (100-30)/1000 = 7\%$

    解读: 应计盈余比 7% 处于极高区间 (典型公司在 0-3%, 高质量公司 OCF > NI 时为负)。NI 远高于 OCF 说明大量利润是"账面"的, 没有变成现金。

    Sloan (1996) 实证: 把美股按应计盈余比五分位排序, 最高 quintile (类似本题) 下一年股票回报低于最低 quintile 平均 ~10 个百分点 (年化超额回报 −10% α). 这就是"应计盈余异象" (accruals anomaly):

    Accruals ratio = 7%, in the top quintile. Sloan (1996): top quintile underperforms bottom by ~10% next year — the accruals anomaly.

    原因解读:

    1. 盈利质量低: 应计盈余的可持续性远低于现金盈余. 投资者初期高估了该利润, 未来实际现金不足以维持估值
    2. 反转: 高应计公司常常未来 1-2 年内出现"业绩低于预期" → 股价下跌
    3. 异常持续性: Sloan 之后 30 年大量研究确认此异象在美国、欧洲、日本、中国都存在 — 是少数能跨时间跨地区稳健存在的因子

    A 股应用: 中国市场对应计盈余的反应"滞后"更明显 (散户主导 + 信息不对称). 量化基金把"应计盈余比"作为多因子模型的负向因子(高应计 → 减分)。

    实务陷阱: 应计盈余比受会计政策影响 (折旧加速 / 减缓 / 应收账款准备等). 单年数据可能噪音大, 用 3 年平均更可靠。

    Ratio = 7% — top quintile. Sloan: top quintile underperforms by ~10% in following year. Mechanism: low earnings quality; future earnings disappointments; mean reversion. Anomaly persists across geographies and decades — one of finance's most robust factor results. A‑share: even stronger lag due to retail dominance + asymmetry. Use 3‑year average to reduce noise.
