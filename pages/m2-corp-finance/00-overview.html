公司财务 — 模块导览Corporate Finance — Module Overview

如果说会计学是描述已发生的经济实质, 公司财务就是决定未来要做什么 — 投资什么项目, 用什么资本结构, 派多少股利。所有决策的"客观函数"是股东价值最大化, 所有约束是风险、信息不对称、代理冲突。本模块用代数化的方式把这些权衡讲清楚。 If accounting describes what has happened, corporate finance decides what to do — which projects to take, how to finance them, how much to pay out. The objective is shareholder value; the constraints are risk, information asymmetry, and agency conflicts. This module makes the tradeoffs algebraic.

学习目标Objectives
  • 用时间价值、风险-收益 tradeoff、无套利三条原理把所有公司财务结论推导出来 (而不是死记)。
  • 能用 WACC 评估任意 A 股公司的资本成本; 能用 NPV/IRR 判断投资项目可行性。
  • 能用 DuPont 三层/五层分解任意公司的 ROE, 识别其经营效率的真实来源。
  • 能用 Beneish M-Score / Altman Z-Score / 应计质量等量化指标识别财务造假与暴雷风险。
  • Derive all corporate‑finance results from three principles: time value, risk‑return tradeoff, no arbitrage.
  • Compute WACC for any A‑share firm; evaluate projects with NPV/IRR.
  • Decompose ROE via 3‑step and 5‑step DuPont to locate the real source of operating efficiency.
  • Use Beneish M‑Score / Altman Z‑Score / accruals quality to detect fraud and distress risk.

First-Principles 思维模型First‑Principles Mental Models

i

时间价值Time value of money

$1$ 元今天 $\neq$ $1$ 元明天。所有现金流必须折回同一时点才能比较。物理类比: 不同惯性系下的时间, 需要 Lorentz 变换才能比较 — 这里的"Lorentz 变换"就是折现率。

¥1 today $\neq$ ¥1 tomorrow. All cash flows must be discounted to a common date to compare. Physics analogy: time in different inertial frames; the Lorentz transform is the discount rate.

ii

机会成本Opportunity cost

任何决策的真正成本不是花了多少钱, 而是放弃了的最佳替代。这是 WACC、hurdle rate、所有资本预算的基础。

The true cost of any decision is the foregone next‑best alternative, not the cash outlay. Foundation of WACC, hurdle rate, all capital budgeting.

iii

风险-收益 tradeoffRisk‑return tradeoff

在均衡市场, 任何超额收益都伴随超额风险。"没有 free lunch"是 CAPM、APT、Black-Scholes 的核心约束。

In equilibrium, excess return implies excess risk. "No free lunch" anchors CAPM, APT, Black‑Scholes.

iv

价值守恒 / Value additivityValue additivity

两个独立项目的总价值 = 各自价值之和。这是 MM 定理的根 — 切割同一块蛋糕的方式不改变蛋糕大小。

The total value of two independent projects equals the sum of their values. The root of MM — slicing the pie differently doesn't change its size.

v

无套利No arbitrage

如果两种现金流相同的资产价格不同, 套利会瞬间消除差异。这是所有资产定价 (CAPM, APT, B-S) 的强约束。

If two cash‑flow‑equivalent assets have different prices, arbitrage closes the gap instantly. The strong constraint behind all asset pricing.

vi

代理成本 (Agency cost)Agency cost

管理层与股东的利益不完全一致, 这种偏离的代价就是 agency cost。它解释了股利政策、资本结构、薪酬设计的许多现实选择。

Manager‑shareholder interest misalignment imposes a cost. Explains many real‑world choices in dividends, capital structure, compensation.

vii

实物期权 (Real option)Real option

不确定性下, "等待"、"扩张"、"放弃"本身有价值。静态 NPV 通常低估有期权特性项目的价值。

Under uncertainty, the ability to wait / expand / abandon has option value. Static NPV typically underestimates projects with optionality.

viii

资本成本 = 边际折现率Cost of capital = marginal discount rate

公司接受新项目的最低门槛 = 资本提供方在同等风险下能拿到的收益率。WACC 就是这条门槛的具体计算方式。

The minimum hurdle rate = the return capital providers can earn elsewhere at the same risk. WACC operationalizes this hurdle.

ix

信号 / 信息不对称Signaling / information asymmetry

管理层与外部投资者拥有的信息不同。可观察的行动(分红、回购、增发) 传递管理层不可观察的信念。Akerlof 柠檬市场 / Spence 信号理论是这套框架的根。

Insiders and outsiders have different information. Observable actions (dividends, buybacks, issuance) signal unobservable beliefs. Rooted in Akerlof's lemons market and Spence's signaling.

资本结构无关性 (假设下)Capital structure irrelevance (under assumptions)

无税 + 无破产成本 + 无信息不对称 + 完美市场, 公司价值不依赖资本结构。无论债务还是权益融资, 公司总价值相同。引入税收后, 有税盾价值, 全杠杆最优。

With no taxes, no bankruptcy costs, no information asymmetry, perfect markets — firm value is independent of capital structure. With taxes, the tax shield favors high leverage.

trade-off / pecking order 派Trade-off / pecking-order school
实际杠杆有最优区间Optimal leverage exists

税盾收益 vs 财务困境成本 (破产成本 + 信号 + 代理) → 存在内部最优。Pecking order (Myers 1984): 公司偏好内部融资 → 债务 → 股权, 因为信息不对称下股权发行被市场解读为悲观信号。

Tax shield benefit vs distress costs (bankruptcy + signaling + agency) → interior optimum. Pecking order (Myers 1984): internal cash → debt → equity, because equity issuance signals pessimism.

现状State MM 是教学起点 (告诉你"假设下"的中性参照), 实务采用 trade-off + pecking order 解释行业差异。中国 A 股大量公司处于非最优杠杆状态: 国企倾向高杠杆 (政府隐性担保), 民企倾向低杠杆 (融资约束)。 MM is the pedagogical baseline; trade‑off + pecking order explain reality. A‑share state firms run higher leverage (implicit guarantee); private firms lower (financing constraints).
分歧DisputeDCF vs 相对估值的可靠性DCF vs relative valuation
DCF 派 (Buffett, Damodaran)school (Buffett, Damodaran)
内在价值优先Intrinsic value first

DCF 反映"这家公司本身能产生多少现金", 是估值的第一性原理。相对估值依赖"市场对可比公司的定价是对的"假设 — 在市场系统性高估或低估时全错。

DCF reflects "how much cash this firm itself can generate" — first‑principles valuation. Relative valuation assumes "the market prices comparables right" — fails when the whole sector is mis‑priced.

相对估值派 (实务券商)Relative valuation (practitioners)
可比是检验真理的市场标准Comparables anchor reality

DCF 的参数 (永续增长率、折现率、终值倍数) 全部主观, 极易"算出任意价格"。相对估值 (PE/PB/EV-EBITDA) 用市场可观察价格作锚, 更接近"市场愿意付的"。

DCF inputs (terminal growth, discount rate, exit multiple) are all subjective — you can "arrive at any price." Multiples anchor to market‑observable prices and reflect "what the market is willing to pay."

现状State Damodaran 自己说"用两套各算一遍", 然后对比差异理解市场预期。M3 Ch.05-06 详述。 Damodaran himself recommends "do both, then compare to read the market's implicit assumptions." M3 Ch.05‑06 develops both.
分歧Dispute回购 vs 派息Buyback vs dividend
MM 1961 无关性irrelevance
理论上等价Theoretically equivalent

完美市场下, 派息 vs 回购对股东价值无差别。投资者可以自己"造分红"(卖股票) 或"造回购"(再投资分红)。

In a perfect market, dividends and buybacks are equivalent. Investors can replicate either via personal trading.

税收 / 信号派Tax / signaling school
现实中两者不同Not equivalent in practice

税收: 美国回购税负低于分红 → 美国公司偏好回购。中国: 分红税 (20%) vs 资本利得税 (个人持股>1年 0%) → 长期股东偏好回购。
信号: 持续分红 = 稳定盈利能力; 突然加大回购 = 管理层认为股价被低估。两者传递的信号不同。

Taxes: US buybacks taxed lighter than dividends → US firms prefer buybacks. China: dividend tax 20% vs LT capital gains 0% (>1y) → long‑term holders prefer buybacks too. Signaling: steady dividends signal stable earnings power; sudden buybacks signal undervaluation.

现状State 美国 2010+ 回购规模超过分红; 中国 2018 后回购规模迅速上升 (回购新规修订后), 但长期股东偏好分红的"传统"仍在。 US buybacks have exceeded dividends since 2010+. China's buybacks have grown rapidly post‑2018 reform, but dividend culture persists.

用 5 年月度数据回归个股收益率对市场收益率, 得到 β。简单, 但依赖"过去 β = 未来 β"假设。

Regress 5y monthly stock returns on market. Simple, assumes past β = future β.

Bottom-up β (Damodaran)Bottom-up β
从同业资产 β 重塑Rebuild from peer asset βs

取同业可比公司的 β → 用 Hamada 公式去杠杆得到资产 β → 用目标公司的 D/E 重新杠杆化。优势: 对短上市公司、并购后公司、资本结构变化的公司更稳健。

Take peer βs → unlever via Hamada → relever at target firm's D/E. Better for short‑listed, post‑M&A, or capital‑structure‑changing firms.

现状State 学术与实务两者都用; Damodaran 在新版教材中越来越偏向 bottom-up, 因 A 股大量科创板/创业板公司缺乏长历史数据。 Both used in practice; Damodaran increasingly favors bottom‑up, especially for STAR/ChiNext firms with short trading histories.

隐含 ERP (Damodaran)Implied ERP
从当前估值反推Back‑solve from valuation

用当前指数水平 + 分析师未来 5 年盈利预测 + 假设永续增长 → 反推使 PV = 当前价的隐含 r → 隐含 ERP。优势: 实时, 反映市场当前情绪与基本面。

Solve for the discount rate that equates DCF on current consensus earnings to current index level → implied ERP. Live, reflects sentiment + fundamentals.

现状State Damodaran 每月发布美国隐含 ERP (1.5%-6% 波动), 实务越来越多采用。中国学术界与实务正在向类似方法过渡。 Damodaran updates US implied ERP monthly (1.5–6% range). Chinese practice is moving in that direction.
