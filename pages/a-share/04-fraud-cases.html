Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · 老王复盘 5 起 A 股造假, 每起换一个识别角度 · Wang re-traces five A‑share frauds, each through a different detection lens

老王退休后在家里挂了一块白板, 上面写着五个名字: 康得新、康美、瑞幸、辉山、华锐。他每晚泡一壶茶, 把这五份年报和退市公告挨个翻一遍, 不是为了"听故事", 是为了把每一笔造假动作还原成"如果当时是我, 我能不能从报表里看出来"。每一个案子他只问一个问题, 因为每个案子都对应一种典型的取证手法。今晚的茶, 喝了五种味道。

After retiring, Old Wang put a whiteboard on his wall with five names: Kangde Xin, Kangmei, Luckin, Huishan, Sinovel. Every evening, with a pot of tea, he goes through these five annual reports and delisting notices one by one — not to hear the stories but to replay each manipulation as: "If I were on the other side of this filing, could I have caught it?" One question per case, because each case anchors a different forensic procedure. Tonight's tea has five flavours.

1. 康得新 002450 — 122 亿"现金"对应不上 → 银行存款核对漏洞. 老王翻 2018 年中报: 货币资金 154 亿。看着这个数他先问一个最朴素的问题 — "这 154 亿到底在哪些银行的哪些账户上?" 附注列了北京银行账户、招商银行账户、农业银行账户……乍看没问题。但康得新和北京银行西单支行签了一份"现金管理协议" — 上市公司的钱"归集"到控股股东康得集团那边的母账户。形式上钱仍在上市公司账户名下、银行函证回函也显示"账上有钱", 但支配权已经被划走 — 控股股东说取就取, 上市公司却动不了。审计师那一年寄了银行函证, 银行回函显示余额无误, 于是审计意见无保留。识别准则: 看年报附注的"受限货币资金"披露; 同时去年报附注里找"关联方资金归集 / 现金管理协议"字眼 — 这是 2018 年前 A 股造假最隐蔽的入口。如果当时你看到 "现金管理协议" 四个字, 能识别出来吗? 大多数散户不会. 老王第一次读 2017 年报时也没看出来 — 他这次是回头读, 才发现这条线索就明明白白写在附注里。

1. Kangde Xin 002450 — ¥12.2bn of "cash" that doesn't reconcile → the bank-confirmation hole. Wang opens the 2018 interim: cash & equivalents ¥15.4bn. He starts with the bluntest question — "which bank, which accounts, exactly?" The footnote lists Bank of Beijing accounts, China Merchants Bank accounts, ABC accounts… nothing jumps. But Kangde Xin signed a "cash-management agreement" with the Bank of Beijing Xidan branch — the listco's funds were swept into a master account held by parent Kangde Group. Nominally the money still sat in the listco's name; the auditor's bank confirmation duly showed the balance; the auditor issued an unqualified opinion. But control had been transferred — the parent could draw at will; the listco could not. Detection rule: always read the "restricted cash" disclosure; then grep the footnotes for "related-party fund sweep" or "cash-management agreement." Pre-2018, this was the most-hidden A‑share fraud entry point. If you had seen those eight characters back then, would you have flagged it? Most retail wouldn't. Wang didn't on first read either — only on this replay did he see the clue was plainly there in the footnotes.

2. 康美药业 600518 — 300 亿现金虚增 + 利息倒推算法不一致 → 用报表自己揭报表的丑. 老王翻康美 2017 年报: 货币资金 341 亿, 利息收入 4.7 亿。他拿铅笔在纸上写了一道小学算术: 4.7 / 341 ≈ 1.4%。然后他翻自己家里的工行存款回单 — 三个月定期 2.6%, 一年期 3%。他停了一下: "就算全是活期 (0.3-0.5%), 一家公司也至少会买点通知存款或短期理财, 加权 1.5-2% 是底线。1.4% 这个数, 要么是公司把现金都搁着不打理 (不可能, 没有 CFO 这么不爱钱), 要么是账上没那么多钱." 倒推一下: 利息收入 4.7 / 合理收益率 2% ≈ 真实货币资金 235 亿, 比披露的 341 亿少 100+ 亿。后续证监会查实, 实际虚增货币资金 887 亿, 单 2017 年末就有 300 亿造假, 比老王倒推的还多。这是 A 股造假识别的最经济算法 — 识别准则: 任何公司年报, 用"利息收入 / 货币资金"算一下加权收益率, 低于 1.5% 就深度怀疑。如果当时你看到了 1.4% 这个数, 能识别出来吗? 用一行小学算术就够了, 但前提是知道要算它. 老王后来给所有他研究的公司都加了这一列.

2. Kangmei 600518 — ¥30bn cash inflation + interest-income inversion → use the filing to indict itself. Wang opens Kangmei 2017: cash ¥34.1bn, interest income ¥0.47bn. Pencil arithmetic: 0.47 / 34.1 ≈ 1.4%. He pulls his own ICBC deposit slip — 3-month term 2.6%, 1-year 3%. Pause: "Even if every yuan sat in a no-interest current account (0.3–0.5%), any CFO would route part of ¥34bn into notice deposits or short-term wealth products; a blended rate of 1.5–2% is the floor. 1.4% means either this CFO doesn't care about money (impossible) or the cash isn't really there." Inversion: 0.47 / 2% ≈ ¥23.5bn implied true cash, ¥10bn short of the booked ¥34.1bn. The CSRC's later finding: cumulative cash inflation ¥88.7bn; 2017 single-year ¥30bn — even worse than Wang's back-of-envelope. Detection rule: for every A‑share annual you read, compute interest-income / cash; below 1.5%, suspect. If you'd seen 1.4% back then, would you have caught it? It's one line of grade-school arithmetic — provided you know to run it. Wang now adds this column to every name he studies.

3. 瑞幸咖啡 LKNCY (中概 ADR) — 杠杆做空报告 (浑水) 揭露虚增订单 → 实地调研 + 数据反算双引擎. 2020 年 1 月 31 日, 浑水 (Muddy Waters) 发布一份匿名做空报告, 89 页, 引用了 11,260 小时门店监控视频 + 25,843 张交易小票, 算出瑞幸 2019 年 Q3 单店日均订单 ~263 单, 但财报披露 ~444 单 — 虚增 69%。报告还交叉验证: 第三方支付通道数据 (支付宝小程序订单) 比公司披露的低 50%。瑞幸最初否认, 4 月 2 日自查公告承认虚增 22 亿元 (3.1 亿美元) 销售, 股价单日跌 75%, 6 月 SEC 摘牌。老王问: 浑水这份报告, 一个散户能复刻吗? 大头不能 (1.1 万小时监控录像 + 25,843 张小票, 单人是雇 1500 人月才有的工作量), 但方法论可以学: 报表内部数据如果和外部公开数据 (支付平台、税收、行业统计) 对不上, 是最强信号。识别准则: 把财报的"单位经济"(per-store, per-customer, per-unit) 拿出来对比同行 + 用外部数据 (商场客流、外卖平台、社交媒体讨论度) 交叉验证。如果当时你看到 2019 年瑞幸披露的单店订单 444 单远超星巴克成熟门店的 200 单, 能怀疑吗? 反过来想想: 一家从 0 开到 4500 家店、营销靠 18 元优惠券的新公司, 单店日均比星巴克成熟门店多一倍 — 这本身就是反逻辑的, 不需要 11000 小时监控录像。

3. Luckin Coffee LKNCY (China-concept ADR) — short-seller report by Muddy Waters exposes inflated orders → field work + data inversion, twin engines. Jan 31 2020, Muddy Waters published an anonymous 89-page short, citing 11,260 hours of in-store CCTV + 25,843 sales receipts collected by ~1,500 part-time field workers, computing 2019 Q3 average daily orders per store ≈ 263 vs the company's disclosed ≈ 444 — a 69% inflation. Cross-check: third-party payment channel data (Alipay mini-program orders) ran ~50% below disclosure. Luckin denied, then on Apr 2 self-disclosed ¥2.2bn (~$310m) fabricated sales; the stock fell 75% in one day; SEC delisted in June. Wang asks: can a retail investor reproduce a Muddy Waters report? Mostly not (11k hours of CCTV + 25,843 receipts ≈ 1,500 person-months). But the methodology is teachable: when filed unit economics diverge from public outside data (payment platforms, tax, industry stats), it is the strongest possible signal. Detection rule: pull per-store / per-customer / per-unit metrics out of the filing and cross-check against peer benchmarks and outside data (mall foot traffic, delivery platforms, social-media chatter). If in 2019 you'd seen Luckin's 444 daily orders per store vs Starbucks-mature-store 200, would you have suspected? Reverse: a 0-to-4500-store newcomer running on ¥18 coupon promos out-orders mature Starbucks 2-to-1 — that is itself counter-logical; you don't need 11,000 hours of CCTV.

4. 辉山乳业 6863.HK (港股) — 现金流连续多年负, 净利润持续正 → 经营现金 vs 净利 长期背离. 辉山是东北最大的奶牛养殖 + 乳制品公司, 2013 年港交所上市。老王翻它 2013-2016 四年财报: 净利润累计 +63 亿港币, 看着不错; 但同期经营现金流累计 −12 亿港币。赚的"利润"和经营现金流方向相反, 持续四年。这种结构在 M3 Ch.08 的 Sloan accruals anomaly 里就是"极高应计" — 报表利润几乎全是应计, 没有现金支撑。2016 年 12 月浑水又出手, 发布做空报告指控辉山虚报奶牛存栏数 (实际比披露低 30%+), 加之大股东 70% 股权质押。2017 年 3 月 24 日股价 90 分钟内闪崩 85% (从 2.81 港币跌到 0.42), 当日停牌至今。识别准则: 3 年滚动累计 OCF / 3 年滚动累计 NI 是核心比率。如果连续 3 年 OCF / NI < 0.5, 即使每年看都"还行", 滚动一看就是结构性虚假。如果当时你算了这个滚动比率, 能识别出来吗? 这是单变量 + 三年时间窗的最简检验, 计算量比 Beneish M-score 还小. 老王说他每只长期持仓都要做这个三年滚动检验.

4. Huishan Dairy 6863.HK — operating cash flow negative for years, net income positive throughout → multi-year cash-NI divergence. Huishan was the largest cow-dairy producer in northeast China, HK-listed in 2013. Wang lays out 2013–16: cumulative NI +HK$6.3bn — pretty; cumulative OCF −HK$1.2bn. Earnings and operating cash flow moved in opposite directions for four straight years. Under M3 Ch.08's Sloan accruals anomaly, this is "extreme accruals" — earnings without cash. Dec 2016, Muddy Waters struck again, alleging Huishan overstated dairy-cow inventory by 30%+ and noting 70% promoter share pledges. Mar 24 2017 the stock crashed 85% in 90 minutes (HK$2.81 → HK$0.42), trading halted ever since. Detection rule: the core ratio is 3-year cumulative OCF / 3-year cumulative NI. If < 0.5 over a rolling 3-year window, even if each annual reading looks "fine," the rolling read flags structural fakery. If you'd computed this rolling ratio back then, would you have caught it? It's the simplest possible single-variable, three-year-window test — less arithmetic than the Beneish M-score. Wang says he runs this rolling check on every long-term holding.

5. 华锐风电 601558 — 提前确认发货 (主管事后承认) → 收入确认时点造假. 华锐风电 2011 年 1 月上市, 发行价 90 元, 是 A 股当时最贵 IPO 之一。2012 年报披露净利润 −5.8 亿, 比 2011 年的 +7.7 亿急剧反转。2013 年 5 月公司发公告承认: 2011 年报虚增收入 24.3 亿, 净利润少 1.7 亿(把 2011 NI 从 7.7 调到 6.0)。手法很简单: 销售部门把发往海外港口的风机, 即使没有完成安装、客户没有验收, 也在 2011 年 12 月份就确认了收入。M1 Ch.05 收入确认五步法里, 第四步"履约义务被满足"是关键 — 风机要等客户在工地组装、调试、并网发电后才算履约。华锐的销售把"发货上船"等同于"履约完成", 提前 6-12 个月确认了收入。2013 年自查暴露后, 时任董事长韩俊良 2017 年因违规披露被判 11 个月有期徒刑。识别准则: 看年报"合同负债 vs 应收账款"的比例与同行业对比。重大工程类公司 (风机、核电设备、船舶) 客户通常是"分阶段付款", 应收占比偏高且账龄长是正常; 但如果应收账款占营收比 突然升高 + 合同负债占营收比 突然下降, 是提前确认的典型信号。如果当时你看到华锐 2011 年应收占收入比从 2010 年的 32% 跳到 49%, 能怀疑吗? 老王自己当年就是没看附注的应收账龄结构, 后悔了好几年。

5. Sinovel Wind 601558 — premature recognition on shipped-but-uninstalled turbines (admitted post-hoc by management) → timing-of-recognition fraud. Sinovel IPO'd in Jan 2011 at ¥90, one of the priciest A‑share IPOs of that era. 2012 NI swung to −¥580m from +¥770m the year before. May 2013 the company self-disclosed: 2011 revenue inflated ¥2.43bn, NI ¥170m (restated 2011 NI from ¥770m to ¥600m). Mechanism: sales recognized revenue on overseas-port-shipped turbines that had not been installed nor accepted by customers. Under M1 Ch.05 (5-step revenue recognition), step 4 — "performance obligation satisfied" — is the gate; for a wind turbine, satisfaction is customer-site assembly + commissioning + grid-connection, not ship-out. Sinovel sales equated "loaded onto vessel" with "obligation satisfied," accelerating recognition by 6–12 months. CEO Han Junliang was sentenced to 11 months for misdisclosure in 2017. Detection rule: for any heavy-equipment / EPC firm (turbines, nuclear, shipbuilding), watch AR/Revenue and Contract Liability/Revenue against peers. AR-heavy is normal (staged-payment customers), but a sudden spike in AR/Rev combined with a drop in CL/Rev is the classic early-recognition signature. If you'd seen Sinovel's AR/Rev jump from 32% (2010) to 49% (2011), would you have suspected? Wang missed this exact ratio at the time — by his own admission, "I just didn't read the aging schedule, and I regretted it for years."

傍晚总结. 老王看着白板上的五个名字, 喝完最后一口茶。他说: 这五起案子, 没有一起需要内幕信息才能识别 — 所有线索都明明白白写在公开年报里。康得新的"现金管理协议"是附注里的一句话; 康美的"利息收入 / 货币资金"是小学算术; 瑞幸的"单店日均订单 444 单"是公司自己披露的; 辉山的"3 年累计 OCF / NI" 用计算器 20 秒; 华锐的"应收占收入比从 32 跳到 49" 是同一张表上下两行的差。问题不是信息不公开, 是大多数投资者不知道该读哪一行、该算哪个比. Wrap-up. Wang looks at the five names on the board, finishes his tea. The point: none of these five cases required insider information — every clue sat openly in the public annual report. Kangde Xin's "cash-management agreement" is a sentence in the footnote; Kangmei's interest-income-over-cash ratio is grade-school division; Luckin's "444 daily orders per store" is the company's own disclosure; Huishan's 3-year cumulative OCF/NI is 20 seconds on a calculator; Sinovel's AR/Revenue spike (32 → 49) is two rows of the same table. The problem isn't disclosure; it's that most investors don't know which row to read and which ratio to compute. 关联方循环交易识别

Related-party round-tripping

康得新、乐视、获取多个 A 股造假案的共同套路: 控股股东 ←→ 上市公司 之间设置看似独立的交易, 实质是同一口袋里的钱倒来倒去, 把现金搬到大股东账户, 把"收入"留在上市公司报表。识别要点:

A common A‑share pattern (Kangde Xin, Leshi, dozens more): the listco and the controlling shareholder set up nominally independent transactions that are economically money-circulating-within-one-pocket — cash flows to the parent, "revenue" stays on the listco. Detection:

  1. 定向看附注 "关联方交易明细": 销售给关联方 / 采购自关联方 / 提供劳务给关联方 / 担保给关联方 — 单笔大额或合计 > 净资产 10% 都需深查.Targeted read of "Related-Party Transactions" footnote: sales to, purchases from, services for, guarantees to a related party — any single large entry or aggregate > 10% of equity demands deep-dive.
  2. "其他应收款"明细: 关联方占款最常挂在"其他应收款", 因为它不参与营业利润计算, 容易藏匿. 看明细前 5 名是否包含关联方."Other receivables" breakdown: related-party fund extractions most often sit in Other Receivables (not flowing through operating profit, easy to hide). Check whether the top-5 entries include related parties.
  3. "受限货币资金"披露: 抵押、冻结、保证金、对外担保保证金 — 都会显示资金"账上在但用不了". 受限比例 > 30% 是强信号."Restricted cash" disclosure: pledged, frozen, collateral, guarantee-margin — cash "on the books but unusable." Restricted > 30% of cash is a strong signal.
  4. 担保附注: 上市公司对关联方提供担保 — 即使没爆雷, 也是或有负债. 担保金额 / 净资产 > 50% 是 A 股监管硬指标 (2020 证监会 36 号文).Guarantees footnote: listco guarantees to related parties — contingent liability even if no default. Guarantee / equity > 50% triggers a hard CSRC regulatory line (2020 Decision 36).

识别 vs 起诉的差异: Layer 2 的所有工具都是识别工具 — 用于决策"要不要持仓"。"起诉造假"是证监会的事, 需要更高证据标准。投资者的目标不是"判定是否造假", 而是"是否足以怀疑造假而退出"。前者需要 100% 证据, 后者只需要 60% 把握。不对称损失的逻辑 (M3 Ch.08 Q4 反复出现): 错过 −5%, 踩雷 −80%, 当然该把怀疑阈值压低。

  1. Q1. 一家上市公司账面货币资金 200 亿, 当年利息收入 1.8 亿。同时账面短期借款 80 亿, 当年利息费用 5.2 亿。算"利息收入率"和"融资成本率", 解读这两个数。还有什么额外的反逻辑?Q1.     3. 治理讨论 (10 页) 是动机说明: 解释"为什么这家公司有造假动机" (VIE 结构 + 质押率), 让前两段的发现逻辑闭环。投资者读完会想 "这种结构本来就容易造假, 现在被人抓到了, 合理".

    启示给散户的方法论: 三段缺一不可. 你自己研究公司, 不需要 1500 人雇佣的实地调研, 但需要:

    • (财务分析) 用 4.1-4.2 的工具算 Beneish + CCS + 利息倒推
    • (第一手 / 公开数据) 用第三方公开数据 (Wind 数据 + 行业报告 + 同行单位经济) 交叉
    • (治理) 看公司治理结构 (大股东质押、关联方、VIE / 红筹结构、审计师变更)

    三段都跑过, 再决定持仓。结论: 浑水方法论是三段合议, 不是单段判刑. 这与 Q3 的"多证据合议"是同一道理.

    Surface answer: the 60 pages of first-hand data (CCTV + 25,843 receipts). But this isn't quite right.

    More accurate: three-section contribution.

    1. Financial analysis (20 pages) is the trigger: without it, CCTV data is "street rumour"; it translates "263 vs 444 daily orders" into "¥2.2bn revenue inflated" — the concrete number institutions need. Institutions read the finance section, retail reads CCTV.
    2. First-hand data (60 pages) is the evidentiary floor: without it, finance analysis is "model guess" and the company can rebut assumptions. CCTV is uncontestable — footage is footage.
    3. Governance (10 pages) is the motive narrative: explains why this firm had incentive to commit (VIE + pledging), closing the logical loop. Investors think "this structure was already conducive to fraud; the finding fits."

    Retail methodology transfer: all three sections matter. You can't hire 1,500 field workers, but you can: (financial) compute Beneish + CCS + interest inversion (eqs 4.1, 4.2); (first-hand / public) cross-check with Wind / industry reports / peer unit economics; (governance) read pledge, related-party, VIE structure, auditor changes. Run all three before deciding. Bottom line: Muddy Waters' method is three-section deliberation, not single-section conviction — same principle as Q3's multi-metric deliberation.

  5. Q5. 假设 A 股 2024 年新出一家明星公司, 财务指标全绿: OCF/NI = 1.2 (5y avg), Beneish M = −2.5 (远低于阈值), AR/Rev 同行可比, 大股东无质押, 审计师"标准无保留", PCAOB 美国上市同步审计。仍然可能有什么剩余风险使你不投资? 给出至少 3 种。Q5. Imagine a 2024 A‑share star: every financial indicator green — OCF/NI = 1.2 (5y avg), Beneish M = −2.5, AR/Rev peer-comparable, no promoter pledge, unqualified audit, PCAOB-audited US-listed parallel. What residual risks would still keep you out? Name three.

    这道题是反过来问 — 即使"看起来很干净"也要保持怀疑. 几种剩余风险:

    1. 未来财务造假 ≠ 历史财务造假: Beneish / Sloan / 五盏灯都是历史数据. 一家公司过去 5 年干净, 不能保证未来 3 年干净, 特别是:
      • (a) 业绩压力发生质变 (例如刚 IPO 高估值需要"对得起市场")
      • (b) 控股权变更 (新大股东动机不同)
      • (c) 行业进入下行 (压力增加)
    2. 未触发的"非财务造假": 财务报表干净 ≠ 业务本身合规. 可能存在: (a) 反垄断风险 (互联网巨头 2020-2022); (b) 数据安全 / 出海风险 (滴滴退市); (c) ESG / 环保违规; (d) 同业竞争 / 关联交易未披露; (e) 国家安全审查 (军工或科技敏感行业).
    3. 商业模式风险: Beneish 不识别"商业模式落后". 一家公司账可能干净, 但产品 / 技术正在被颠覆 (例如 2015 的诺基亚仍然财务干净). 投资亏损同样大 (M3 Ch.08 的 "价值陷阱").
    4. 估值风险: 干净 + 估值贵 = 时间问题. 茅指数 2021 顶部所有公司财务都很干净, 但 P/E 60-100 倍后续连续两年下行. 这是 M3 Ch.08 三角的"估值缺"维度.
    5. 系统性 / 宏观风险: 即使个股完美, 整个市场 / 行业 / 国家面临系统性风险 (例如 2022 中概股集体退市风险, 与单个公司质量无关).
    6. "清白本身"作为信号: 一家公司财务过于完美, 同时所有指标都精确踩在监管/学术阈值上方一点点 — 这种"完美"本身可能是精心调校的结果. 真正干净的公司不会让所有指标都"刚好绿色"; 它会自然分布. 这是高阶 Sloan 检测的逆向变体: 看波动 / 噪音是否过低.

    核心启示: Layer 1-3 教的所有工具都是必要条件而非充分条件. 通过这套筛选 = "已被剔除的风险类别"; 不通过 ≠ "已识别所有风险类别". 投资的安全边际 (Graham 1949) 是多重防御 — 财务质量 + 商业模式 + 治理 + 估值 + 仓位分散, 任何一道单独都不够.

    The question flips the framing — even "looks clean" still demands skepticism. Residual risk categories:

    1. Future fraud ≠ historical fraud: Beneish / Sloan / five lights are historical. Clean past 5 years doesn't guarantee clean next 3, especially when (a) earnings pressure changes (post-IPO valuation pressure); (b) control changes; (c) industry rolls into downcycle.
    2. Untriggered "non-financial fraud": clean filings ≠ compliant business. Could face: antitrust (internet 2020–22), data security / overseas listing (DiDi), ESG / environmental, undisclosed competition, national security (defense / sensitive tech).
    3. Business-model risk: Beneish doesn't detect obsolete models. Books can be clean while product/tech is being disrupted (Nokia 2015 was financially clean). Loss magnitude similar (M3 Ch.08's "value trap").
    4. Valuation risk: clean + expensive = a time problem. Moutai-index peak 2021 was financially clean across the board, but P/E 60–100 led to two-year drawdown. M3 Ch.08's "valuation-leg-missing" dimension.
    5. Systemic / macro: single name perfect, market/sector/country faces systemic risk (2022 China-concept ADR delisting risk, unrelated to individual company quality).
    6. "Too clean" as itself a signal: a firm whose metrics all precisely sit a hair above every regulatory/academic threshold — "perfection" can be engineered. Truly clean firms have natural variance; uniformly-just-green is suspicious. An advanced Sloan-style inverse test: look for under-noise.

    Core lesson: Layer 1–3 tools are necessary, not sufficient. Passing them = "ruled-out risk categories"; failing them ≠ "ruled in all risk categories." Graham's margin of safety (1949) is multi-layer defense — financial quality + business model + governance + valuation + position diversification. No single layer suffices.
