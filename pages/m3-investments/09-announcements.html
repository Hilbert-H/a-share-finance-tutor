A 股公告解读Reading A‑Share Filings

业绩预告 / 定增 / 回购 / 限售解禁 / ST 退市 / 问询函 / 监管函 — A 股的"公告流"是日常波动的主要来源. 学会读, 看出市场反应是否合理, 比看年报更高频。Earnings preannouncement, placement, buyback, lock‑up expiry, ST/delisting, inquiry letters, regulator letters — A‑share's filing stream drives daily volatility. Reading them makes everyday market reactions interpretable.

学习目标Objectives
  • 识别 7 类高频公告 + 各自市场反应模式
  • 用 event study 方法 (估计窗 + 事件窗 + abnormal return + CAR) 形式化"公告反应"
  • 理解 PEAD (post-earnings announcement drift) 现象: 信息慢慢被消化
  • 区分"市场过度反应"(机会) 与"信息含量真正大"(不要逆行)
  • Identify 7 common filing types and typical reactions.
  • Formalize "announcement reaction" via event study (estimation + event window + abnormal return + CAR).
  • Understand PEAD: information is absorbed gradually.
  • Tell over-reaction (opportunity) from genuinely large information (don't fight).

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · 老王复盘 2023 年五个公告事件 · Wang reviews five 2023 filing events

老王最近翻自己 2023 年的交易记录, 发现一件事: 自己持有的股票里, 有几只在公告日当天 ±10% 跳, 自己当时全靠"凭感觉"反应 — 有一次果断卖了, 后来涨回去; 有一次没卖, 后来还在跌。他想搞清楚: 公告日的市场反应到底合理不合理? 反应快的能不能利用? 反应慢的能不能等? 他翻出 5 个有印象的事件, 一个一个复盘。

Sorting through his 2023 trades, Wang notices: a handful of his holdings gapped ±10% on filing days. He reacted on gut — once sold and watched it recover, once held and watched it slide further. He wants to figure out: are filing-day moves rational? When does over-reaction create opportunity? When is the move genuinely warranted? He pulls five events from memory and walks through them.

1. 茅台 (600519) 价格上调公告 → 当日 +6%。 2023 年 11 月某天傍晚, 茅台公告飞天 53° 出厂价从 969 元上调到 1,169 元, 涨幅 20%。第二天开盘股价 +6%, 收盘 +5.8%。老王问自己: 这 +6% 合理吗?简单算一下: 飞天年销量约 4 万吨左右, 出厂价提 200 元/瓶 ≈ 4,800 万元/年, 相当于 NI 多 250 亿/年, 占当前 NI 750 亿的 ~33%。但实际涨价不会立即全部传导 (经销商提价节奏 + 消费者接受度需要时间), 第一年传导率假设 50%, 即 NI 多 125 亿。若 P/E 不变, 股价应该 +17%。当日 +6% 反而是不足反应 — 后续 3 个月还会继续涨。这就是 PEAD。老王检查: 茅台 2023 年 11 月公告后 3 个月内涨幅 +12%, 与他的估算吻合。

1. Moutai (600519) ex-factory price hike → +6% next day. One evening in November 2023, Moutai announced ex-factory price on its flagship 53° Feitian moved from ¥969 to ¥1,169 (+20%). Next morning gap +6%, close +5.8%. Wang asks: is +6% rational? Quick math: annual Feitian volume ~40k tonnes, +¥200/bottle ≈ +¥48m/y per channel, scaled → NI +¥25bn/y, or ~33% of current ¥75bn NI. First-year pass-through ~50% (distributor and consumer absorption takes time) → NI +¥12.5bn. Holding P/E constant: price should rise ~17%. +6% is under-reaction — the rest leaks out over 3 months. That's PEAD. Wang checks: cumulative +12% over the next 3 months, consistent with his estimate.

2. 某仿制药企集采落选 → −15%。 老王清晰记得这件事: 2023 年 3 月某天, 国家集采第八批 (糖尿病用药) 结果公布, 某中型仿制药企的核心产品全部落选。当天股价跌停 −10%, 第二天再 −5%。老王盯着想: 集采落选意味着这家公司未来该产品的销售额归零。如果该产品占公司营收 60% (常见集采核心产品占比), 那 NI 直接腰斩。若 P/E 不变, 股价应该 −50%。−15% 实际是大幅不足反应 — 老王查了后续: 该股 3 个月内继续 −20%, 6 个月内总跌幅 −40%。如果老王当天就懂"集采落选 → 营收归零"的传导, 第二天就该全部卖出, 而不是看到 −10% 就以为"已经跌够了"。

2. A generic-pharma firm fails centralized procurement → −15%. Vividly remembered: in March 2023, the 8th centralized procurement round (diabetes drugs) results dropped. A mid-cap generic firm's core SKUs all failed. Daily limit −10% day 1, then −5% day 2. Wang reasons: failing centralized procurement means future revenue of that product line ≈ zero. If that product is 60% of company revenue (typical for "core" SKUs), NI halves. P/E constant: stock should drop −50%. −15% is severe under-reaction. Wang checks: −20% over next 3 months, −40% cumulative over 6. If he'd grasped the "fail centralized → revenue → zero" transmission on day 1, he should have sold the day after, not assumed "−10% is enough."

3. 某新能源 业绩预增 +50% → 当日 +8% 但当日就回吐到 +2%。 2023 年 7 月某天, 一家二线新能源公司公告半年报预增 NI +50%。开盘高开 +8%, 但当天就一路回吐, 收盘只剩 +2%。老王仔细看公告: 预增的主要来源是"处置某子公司"产生 8 亿一次性投资收益, 而经营性净利润同比反而 −10%。当天高开 +8% 是市场看到"+50%"标题的本能反应, 但读懂公告的投资者一上午就把它卖回去了。这就是第五盏红绿灯 (Ch.08): 非经常性损益 / 净利比的实战版。教训: 别只看预增百分比, 要点开公告看预增来源。

3. A second-tier new-energy firm preannounces NI +50% → gap +8% but fades to +2% intraday. July 2023, the H1 preannouncement. Gap +8%, faded all day, closed +2%. Detail in the filing: the +50% is almost entirely from a one-off ¥800m gain on disposing a subsidiary; operating NI actually fell −10% YoY. The morning gap was reflexive "+50% headline" reaction; investors who read the filing sold by lunch. This is Ch.08 Light #5 (non-recurring / NI) live: don't trust the preannouncement %, click into the source.

4. 重大重组公告 → 复牌涨停 +10% (但老王没参与)。 2023 年某重组案: A 公司停牌 3 个月, 复牌公告"拟以 35 亿收购 B 公司 100% 股权 (B 公司是 A 公司主营业务的国内龙头补充)"。复牌当日 +10% 涨停, 之后连续 3 个涨停。老王没买 (因为他在停牌前根本没持仓), 但事后复盘他问: 三个涨停后追不追? 这是事件驱动 (event-driven) 策略的典型场景。判断的关键: (a) 重组的协同价值是否真实? (b) 收购对价 35 亿是否合理 (B 公司 NI 与同行业 EV/EBITDA)? (c) 监管批准的概率? 老王查了, 收购对价对应 B 公司 P/E ~25 (同行业 18-22) — 略贵, 但有产业协同。三个涨停后追入风险 / 回报已经不对称, 老王决定不追。后续: 完成 3 个涨停后还涨了 ~15%, 但波动加大, 半年后涨幅缩到 +20%。"错过"在事件驱动里是常态, 不追未必错。

4. Major M&A announcement → +10% limit-up on resumption (Wang didn't participate). An A‑share suspended for 3 months, resumes with "acquiring B for ¥3.5bn, B is the domestic complementary leader." Day-1 +10% limit-up, then three consecutive limit-ups. Wang wasn't holding pre-suspension. Post-hoc question: chase after three limit-ups? Classic event-driven setup. Tests: (a) is synergy real? (b) is ¥3.5bn fair given B's NI and peer EV/EBITDA? (c) regulator approval odds? Wang found: ¥3.5bn implies P/E ~25 for B vs peer 18–22 — slightly rich but synergy plausible. Post three limit-ups, risk/reward asymmetric, he doesn't chase. Outcome: another +15% over six weeks, then volatility took it back to +20% net. Missing is the norm in event-driven; not chasing isn't wrong.

5. 某实控人公告 拟减持 3% → 当周 −10%。 2023 年 9 月某天, 老王持有的一只中盘股突然公告"实际控制人拟在未来 6 个月内通过集中竞价方式减持不超过 3%"。当周股价 −10%。老王问自己: 这反应合理吗?简单算: 实控人持股原 25%, 减 3% 后 22%, 仍然绝对控股 (不会引发治理变化)。3% 流通量按当前股价折 ~8 亿, 相对于当周成交量 ~4 亿是 200%, 短期供需冲击大但有限。但市场解读更深: "实控人都要减, 一定是知道未来不好" — 这是信号效应 (signaling), 与减持金额本身无关。是不是过度反应? 取决于其他信号 (公司是否有其他红灯、行业是否进入下行)。老王检查: 公司基本面无变化, 无红灯 — 判定过度反应, 适合逆向小仓位加仓。后续: 6 个月后, 减持执行完成且股价恢复到公告前水平 (即 −10% 跌幅几乎完全回补)。Mr. Market 又一次过度反应。

5. Controlling shareholder announces 3% reduction → −10% in a week. September 2023, one of Wang's mid-caps announces controlling shareholder plans to reduce up to 3% over the next 6 months via secondary trades. Week-1 −10%. Reasonable? Quick math: insider stake from 25% to 22%, still absolute control (no governance change). 3% × current price ≈ ¥800m, vs weekly turnover ¥400m — short-term supply shock 200%. But market reads deeper: "insider sells = bad news ahead" — pure signaling effect, independent of size. Over-reaction? Depends on other signals (red flags elsewhere? sector decline?). Wang checks: no red lights, no sector turn. Verdict: over-reaction, contrarian small add. Outcome: 6 months later, reduction completed and price back to pre-announcement level. Mr. Market over-reacted again.

傍晚总结。 五个事件, 复盘后老王发现一个规律: 市场对结构性 / 慢传导信息(集采、价格上调)反应不足, 对情绪性 / 信号性信息(减持、业绩预增的标题数字)反应过度。这不是市场"低效", 而是市场消化信息的速度不同。慢传导信息需要 3-6 月 PEAD 慢慢落地; 情绪信息当日 / 当周就回吐。老王把这个规律写在小本子上: "慢消息可以慢追, 快消息别追快"。Layer 2 把这个直觉用 event study 方法论形式化 (估计窗 + 事件窗 + abnormal return + CAR + PEAD)。Layer 3 用蚂蚁集团 IPO 暂停 (2020-11-03) 和滴滴中概监管 (2021-07) 两个传导事件验证。

Wrap-up. Five events. Pattern: market under-reacts to structural / slow-transmission news (centralized procurement, price hikes), over-reacts to emotional / signaling news (insider reductions, headline preannouncement numbers). Not "inefficiency" — different absorption speeds. Slow news leaks out over 3–6 months as PEAD; emotional news fades the same day or week. Wang writes a rule: "chase slow news slowly, don't chase fast news fast." Layer 2 below formalizes this with event study methodology (estimation window + event window + abnormal return + CAR + PEAD). Layer 3 validates with Ant IPO suspension (2020-11-03) and Didi regulatory event (2021-07).

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

Layer 1 用 5 个公告事件画出了"慢消息反应不足 + 快消息反应过度"的双轨规律。现在我们把这套规律用事件研究 (event study) 方法论形式化 — 这是金融实证研究的标准工具, 由 Fama-Fisher-Jensen-Roll 1969 论文引入, 后经 Brown-Warner 1985 改进, 至今仍是检验"市场对事件的反应"的标准做法。

Layer 1 sketched the dual rule: slow news under-reacts, fast news over-reacts. Layer 2 formalizes with the event study framework — the standard tool, introduced in Fama-Fisher-Jensen-Roll (1969) and refined by Brown-Warner (1985), still used today to test "market reaction to events."

Event study 方法论

Event study methodology

Event study 的标准流程 (Brown-Warner 1985 框架):

Standard event-study flow (Brown-Warner 1985):

  1. 事件日定义 $t = 0$: 公告日。注意分清"事件首次公开日"与"事件发生日"—— 在 A 股, 内幕信息提前泄漏常见, 真正 $t=0$ 应取第一次价格异动日而非公告日。
  2. 估计窗 (estimation window): 事件日前 [−250, −30] 交易日, 用来估计"正常收益率"参数 (CAPM 的 α 与 β)。
  3. 事件窗 (event window): [−5, +30] 交易日 (短期) 或 [−5, +120] (长期 PEAD)。
  4. 异常收益 (abnormal return, AR): 实际收益减去预期收益。
  5. 累计异常收益 (CAR): AR 在事件窗内累加。
  6. 统计检验: CAR 显著不为零 → 事件含信息。
  1. Event date $t=0$: announcement. In A‑shares insider leakage is common — true $t=0$ is the first abnormal price-move day, not the formal announcement day.
  2. Estimation window: [−250, −30] trading days, used to estimate "normal return" parameters (CAPM α and β).
  3. Event window: [−5, +30] (short-term) or [−5, +120] (long-term, PEAD).
  4. Abnormal return (AR): realized return minus expected return.
  5. Cumulative abnormal return (CAR): AR summed across the event window.
  6. Significance test: CAR significantly ≠ 0 ⇒ event has information content.
Abnormal ReturnAbnormal return
$$ \mathrm{AR}_{i,t} \;=\; R_{i,t} \;-\; E[R_{i,t} \mid \mathrm{est. window}] $$(9.1)

$R_{i,t}$ = 股票 $i$ 在事件日 $t$ 的实际收益率; $E[\cdot]$ 通常用 CAPM: $E[R_{i,t}] = \alpha_i + \beta_i \cdot R_{m,t}$, 其中 $\alpha, \beta$ 在估计窗回归得到。

$R_{i,t}$ = realized return on stock $i$ at event time $t$; $E[\cdot]$ usually from CAPM: $E[R_{i,t}] = \alpha_i + \beta_i \cdot R_{m,t}$, with $\alpha, \beta$ fit on the estimation window.

Fama, Fisher, Jensen, & Roll (1969). "The Adjustment of Stock Prices to New Information." International Economic Review, 10(1). · Brown & Warner (1985). "Using Daily Stock Returns: The Case of Event Studies." JFE, 14.
Cumulative Abnormal ReturnCumulative abnormal return
$$ \mathrm{CAR}_i(t_1, t_2) \;=\; \sum_{t=t_1}^{t_2} \mathrm{AR}_{i,t} $$(9.2)

事件窗 $[t_1, t_2]$ 内 AR 累加。例如 $\mathrm{CAR}(-1, +1) = \mathrm{AR}_{-1} + \mathrm{AR}_0 + \mathrm{AR}_{+1}$ 代表"公告前后 3 天的总异常收益"。

Sum of AR over $[t_1, t_2]$. E.g. $\mathrm{CAR}(-1, +1)$ captures the 3-day window straddling the announcement.

PEAD — Post-Earnings Announcement Drift

PEAD — Post-Earnings Announcement Drift

PEAD 现象: Bernard & Thomas (1989, 1990) 发现 — 公司业绩超预期 (SUE > 0) 后, 股价不是当日完全反映, 而是持续 3-9 个月慢慢漂移上行; 业绩不及预期同样漂移下行。这是 EMH 半强式假说最坚固的反例之一。

PEAD: Bernard & Thomas (1989, 1990) — after a positive earnings surprise (SUE > 0), the price doesn't fully reflect on day 0; it drifts upward over 3–9 months. Negative surprises drift downward. One of the most robust anomalies and the toughest counter-evidence to semi-strong EMH.

SUESUE
$$ \mathrm{SUE} \;=\; \frac{\mathrm{EPS}_{\text{actual}} - \mathrm{EPS}_{\text{forecast}}}{\sigma(\mathrm{EPS}_{\text{forecast}})} $$(9.3)

标准化未预期盈余。Bernard-Thomas 把 SUE 分 10 档, 最高档与最低档之间 60 天 CAR 差异约 7-8% (美国数据, 1974-1986)。中国 A 股 PEAD 幅度更大 (~10-15%) — 因散户占比高、信息处理慢。

Standardized Unexpected Earnings. Bernard-Thomas decile 10 vs decile 1, 60-day CAR gap ~7–8% (US 1974–86). A‑share PEAD even larger (~10–15%) — high retail share, slower information processing.

Bernard & Thomas (1989). "Post‑Earnings Announcement Drift: Delayed Price Response or Risk Premium?" JAR, 27. · Bernard & Thomas (1990). "Evidence That Stock Prices Do Not Fully Reflect the Implications of Current Earnings for Future Earnings." JAE, 13.

为什么 PEAD 存在 30+ 年仍然 robust?

  1. 注意力有限 (Hirshleifer-Teoh 2003): 散户和机构都来不及消化所有公告信息, 信息逐渐渗透到价格里。
  2. 锚定与缓慢更新 (Barberis-Shleifer-Vishny 1998): 投资者对历史预期"锚定", 新信息一次只更新一部分。
  3. 套利成本 (Mendenhall 2004): PEAD 在小盘股 / 高分析师分歧股 上更显著, 这些恰是套利成本最高的股 — limits to arbitrage 直接限制套利资本。
  1. Limited attention (Hirshleifer-Teoh 2003): retail and institutional investors can't digest all filings; info seeps into prices.
  2. Anchoring + slow updating (Barberis-Shleifer-Vishny 1998): investors anchor on prior expectations and update partially each step.
  3. Limits to arbitrage (Mendenhall 2004): PEAD strongest in small-caps and high-analyst-disagreement stocks — precisely where arbitrage cost is highest.

读公告的第一性 5 问

Five first-principle questions

  1. 这是已被市场预期的吗? 已预期的信息已反映在价格 (EMH 半强). 反应大小反映"超预期的程度", 即 SUE 的方向与幅度
  2. 是暂时还是结构性? 周期性业绩波动 vs 行业系统性恶化, 估值含义完全不同 (Ch.07 价值陷阱判断)
  3. 对未来现金流长期影响? 把短期信号代入 DCF / 估值模型, 反推内在价值变化, 与价格变化对比 (Ch.05 + Ch.07)
  4. 是否触发护城河 / 能力圈边界变化? (Ch.07) 例如海康受美国制裁触发"海外护城河缩窄"
  5. 市场反应是否合理? 用 (9.1)(9.2) 估算合理 CAR, 与实际比对: 实际 > 合理 = 过度反应 (机会逆向); 实际 < 合理 = 不足反应 (顺势 PEAD)
  1. Already expected? Priced-in info reflects in price (semi-strong EMH). Reaction magnitude reflects SUE.
  2. Temporary or structural? Different valuation implications (Ch.07 value-trap distinction).
  3. Long-run cash-flow impact? Plug into DCF, derive change in IV, compare to price change (Ch.05 + Ch.07).
  4. Moat or competence-boundary shift? (Ch.07) — e.g., US sanctions narrowed Hikvision's overseas moat.
  5. Is the reaction proportionate? Use (9.1)(9.2) to compute fair CAR; vs realized: over-react → contrarian opportunity; under-react → PEAD ride.

对照 Brealey 与 Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe: Brealey Principles of Corporate Finance Ch.13 把 event study 作为检验半强式 EMH 的标准工具; Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe Corporate Finance Ch.14 把 PEAD 与定增反应作为 EMH 的"两个最大例外"。前者结论: 市场大体有效, PEAD 是边缘; 后者结论: 市场有效但非完美, 异象保留套利空间。两本经典教材在中国 MBA / MSF 课程通用, 立场不完全一致, 反映 EMH 仍是开放问题。

Brealey vs Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe: Brealey Principles of Corporate Finance Ch.13 uses event study as the standard test for semi-strong EMH; Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe Corporate Finance Ch.14 treats PEAD and private-placement reactions as the "two biggest exceptions" to EMH. Brealey concludes markets are "broadly efficient with edge cases"; RWJ concludes "efficient but imperfect, anomalies leave arbitrage space." Both standard texts in Chinese MBA / MSF programs — they disagree, reflecting that EMH remains an open question.

把 5 问当 checklist: 老王每看一个公告, 在小本子上回答 5 个问题。如果 5 个回答全清楚且一致 (例如"已被预期 + 周期性 + 长期无影响 + 不触及护城河 + 反应合理"), 那他不做任何动作。任何一个问题的答案与价格反应不一致(例如"信息很大但价格只动 2%"), 就是研究入口。这就是"NO 比 YES 重要"在事件驱动场景的落地。

Use the 5 questions as a checklist: for every filing, Wang writes one-line answers. If all five are consistent (e.g., "expected + cyclical + no long-run impact + moat intact + proportionate"), no action. Any inconsistency between an answer and the price move (e.g., "huge info but +2%") is a research entry. The event-driven realization of "NO matters more than YES."

理解检验题Trap Questions

每题至少一个反直觉点。Each question has at least one counterintuitive twist.

  1. Q1. 公司宣布业绩预增 200%, 开盘高开 9%. 你买入吗?Q1. 200% earnings preannouncement, +9% gap. Buy?

    看预期是否已被定价。Layer 2 的 5 问 + 一个特别问题:

    1. 预增是否已被分析师 consensus 预期? 若已预期 200%, 9% 高开不够反应, 后续可能继续涨 (PEAD)。若 consensus 只预期 150%, 实际 200% 多出 50%, SUE > 0 大, PEAD 概率高。
    2. 预增来源? 经营好转 (持续) 还是非经常 (一次性, 投资收益 / 处置 / 政府补助)?
    3. 下一年预增是否可持续? 周期顶部 vs 结构性改善?
    4. 市场过度反应(常见 +15-20%) 还是不足反应?
    5. 结合估值: 9% 高开后 P/E 仍合理吗?

    三种典型情境:

    • 经营持续改善 + 估值合理 + 未被充分预期 → 后续 3-6 月 PEAD, 可买入
    • 非经常性 + 估值已贵 → 不追高, 当日内可能就回吐 (Layer 1 第 3 个事件)
    • 已被充分预期 → 9% 高开就是终点, 后续可能下跌 (Buy the rumor, sell the news)

    操作建议: 不在公告日当日做决定。公告日波动大、情绪噪音高, 等 1-2 天看市场是否冷静下来再做。Buffett 名言: "Lethargy bordering on sloth remains the cornerstone of our investment style."

    Five-question check + one extra: was the 200% already in consensus? If consensus 150%, actual 200% delivers SUE > 0 large; PEAD likely. If consensus was 200%, +9% is the terminus, may fade. Source: operating sustained vs non-recurring? Sustainable next year? Over-/under-reaction? Post-gap valuation?

    Three scenarios: sustained operating + reasonable valuation + under-reaction → PEAD ride; one-off + expensive → don't chase; fully expected → +9% is terminus, may fall ("buy the rumor, sell the news"). Practical rule: don't decide on announcement day. Wait 1–2 days for sentiment to cool. Buffett: "Lethargy bordering on sloth remains the cornerstone of our investment style."

  2. Q2. 大股东大规模限售解禁前一周, 股价已跌 15%. 解禁日如何反应?Q2. Major lock‑up expiry approaches; stock down 15% in prior week. Day‑of reaction?

    EMH 半强: 解禁日是已知事件 (上市时已披露解禁日期), 抛压已被提前定价。解禁日实际反应通常温和 (±2%), 因市场已消化。跌 15% 已经反映了预期抛压。

    三种可能的解禁日反应:

    • 大股东没卖 (公告"承诺不减持" 或 后续披露增持) → 正面反弹 +3-5% — "insider confidence signal"
    • 大股东小幅减持 (远低于解禁额) → 温和反应, 或继续慢跌
    • 大股东大幅减持 (接近解禁额) → 继续 −3-5%, 但因已大跌 15%, 后续反弹概率高

    逆向机会: 散户在解禁周恐慌抛售是常见非理性 (Mr. Market 抑郁期)。若公司基本面良好 (Ch.08 五盏灯 + Ch.07 四把尺子全过), 解禁恐慌期是逆向小仓位买入机会。福耀玻璃 600660、招行 600036 多次解禁周表现出"先恐慌后回升"的模式 — 6-12 月内 CAR > 0。

    风险点: 不要套用所有股。基本面恶化中的公司 (Ch.08 红灯) 解禁日是真正抛压释放的开始, 不是机会; 反而是更深下跌的起点。

    Semi-strong EMH: priced in; day-of typically ±2%. The 15% prior drop has absorbed expected pressure.

    Three possibilities: (a) insider doesn't sell (commitment or buy-back) → +3–5% rebound, "insider confidence signal"; (b) small reduction (well below cap) → mild; (c) large reduction (near cap) → −3–5% but rebound likely given 15% drop already.

    Contrarian opportunity: retail panic on lock-up week is common irrationality (Mr. Market depressed). For fundamentally sound names (clear five-light + four-filter pass), it's a small-add contrarian moment — Fuyao 600660, CMB 600036 historically show "panic then recover" with positive 6–12m CAR.

    Risk: don't generalize. For deteriorating names (red lights), the lock-up day is the real selling-pressure release, not opportunity — start of deeper drop, not bottom.

  3. Q3. 用 event study 方法验证"公告日内幕信息提前泄漏"假设: 如果泄漏存在, 你预期估计窗与事件窗的交界处 (例如 $t = -5$ 到 $t = -1$) 会有什么数据特征?Q3. Use event study to test "insider leakage before announcement." If leakage exists, what data feature do you expect near the estimation/event boundary ($t = -5$ to $t = -1$)?

    泄漏的数据特征: 在正式公告日前的几天 (典型 $t=-5$ 到 $t=-1$) 出现:

    1. 显著非零 AR: $|\mathrm{AR}_{t<0}|$ 大于估计窗 $\sigma$ 的 2 倍以上 (即 t 统计量 > 2)
    2. AR 方向与公告内容一致: 公告若是利好, 提前 AR 显著为正; 利空则为负
    3. 成交量异常放大: $t=-5$ 到 $t=-1$ 成交量 / 估计窗均值 > 1.5
    4. 价格变化在公告日反而减弱: 因为很大一部分已经在 $t=-5$ 到 $t=-1$ 中定价了。CAR(-5, +1) 显著, 但 CAR(0, +1) 单独看可能小于无泄漏情境

    实证发现 (A 股): 多份学术研究 (例如清华、上财、北大光华的论文) 发现 A 股重大公告前 5 天 CAR 平均显著, 利好公告前 ≈ +2-3%, 利空公告前 ≈ −3-5%。利空的提前泄漏比利好更显著, 因"知情人"在利空公告前止损动机强。这与监管反复打击内幕交易但难以完全消除的现实吻合。

    方法论修正: 在 A 股 event study 中, 真正的"事件日"应取第一次显著异动日而非正式公告日。这就是 Layer 2 提到的"$t = 0$ 修正"。Fama 等美国研究者也类似处理 SEC 投诉 / 内幕调查 case。

    Leakage features near $t \in [-5, -1]$:

    1. Significant non-zero AR: $|\mathrm{AR}_{t<0}| > 2\sigma$ (t-stat > 2).
    2. AR direction matches announcement: positive for good news pre-event, negative for bad.
    3. Abnormal volume: volume(t<0) / estimation-window mean > 1.5.
    4. Day-0 price move smaller than no-leakage counterfactual — much already priced in $t \in [-5, -1]$. CAR(-5, +1) large but CAR(0, +1) alone smaller.

    Empirical (A‑share): Tsinghua, SUFE, PKU studies find significant pre-event CAR over 5 days — positive announcements ~+2–3%, negative ~−3–5%. Negative leakage stronger because insiders' stop-loss incentive on bad news is stronger. Consistent with regulators' inability to fully suppress insider trading despite repeated enforcement.

    Methodological fix: in A‑share event studies, true $t=0$ should be the first significant move, not the formal announcement day. Same convention applies in US studies of SEC investigations and insider cases.

  4. Q4. PEAD 在公开发表 30 年后仍存在。如果你是一名个人投资者, 想用 PEAD 套利, 列出至少四个实际操作障碍。Q4. PEAD has been public for 30 years yet persists. As a retail investor wanting to arbitrage PEAD, list four practical obstacles.
    1. 识别"SUE 大"需要快速 + 准确的数据: 你需要在公告日内拿到 (a) 公告 EPS; (b) 分析师 consensus; (c) 历史预测分散度 — 这些数据散户难以快速拿到, 通常分析师 consensus 在 Wind / Bloomberg 等付费终端。当你算出 SUE 时, 机构早已动手。
    2. 交易成本: A 股股票佣金 + 印花税合计 ~0.05-0.1% 单边, 频繁交易侵蚀 PEAD 的 5-10% 年化收益。如果只持有 60-90 天再换股, 一年换手 4-6 次, 成本可能占 alpha 的 1/3。
    3. 分散化要求: PEAD 在组合层面稳定, 单只股票仍有 30-50% 概率方向错。你需要持有 30-50 只股的组合才能实现期望收益。8 万元资金分散到 30 只, 每只 2,700 元, 在 A 股最小交易单位 (100 股) 下基本不可行 — 小盘股每股几十元一手就要几千元。
    4. 心理障碍: PEAD 平均收益 60 天 ~5-8%, 但波动大 (年化 σ 25%+), 中间会经历多次回撤。散户在回撤 10% 时往往止损, 错过反弹 — 这是行为偏差对策略的"无形税"。
    5. 样本偏差与生存者偏差: 历史 PEAD 数据基于"当时仍存活的公司"。一些"超预期"公司后来被退市 (康得新型), 历史回测忽略了这种极端尾部损失。实际操作中, 即使捕获 PEAD 平均收益, 偶尔踩到一只退市股的 −80% 足以抵消十次盈利。
    6. 规避方法不充分: 即使叠加 Ch.08 五盏灯过滤掉造假股, 仍有"基本面正常但 PEAD 不显著"的失效样本 — 任何策略在 A 股都有 30-40% 失效率, 散户对失效率的耐受性低。

    实务建议: 散户用 PEAD 套利的修正版是: 在 Ch.07 + Ch.08 已经筛选出的 30 只优质池子内, 关注公告超预期且方向与基本面一致的样本, 小仓位顺势加仓。这不是纯 PEAD 套利, 是价值投资 + PEAD 信号的复合策略, 实际可行性更高。

    Obstacles: (1) SUE detection needs fast and accurate data — analyst consensus typically on Wind/Bloomberg; by the time retail computes, institutionals are already in. (2) Transaction costs — A‑share commission + stamp duty ~0.05–0.1% per side; 4–6 turnovers per year can eat 1/3 of alpha. (3) Diversification requirement — PEAD stable at portfolio level; single stock has 30–50% wrong-direction probability. Need 30–50 names; ¥80k split is ~¥2.7k each, infeasible given A‑share 100-share lots. (4) Psychological obstacle — average +5–8% over 60 days but σ ~25%; mid-window drawdowns trigger retail stop-losses, missing rebounds — "invisible tax" on the strategy. (5) Survivor bias — historical PEAD samples are "firms still listed." Some "surprise-positive" firms later delisted (Kangde Xin type); back-test ignores tail loss. (6) Imperfect filters — even with Ch.08 five-light overlay, ~30–40% of A‑share strategies fail; retail tolerance for failure rate is low.

    Practical: retail PEAD is best as a signal overlaid on the Ch.07 + Ch.08 quality pool — within that 30-name pool, monitor announcements for "surprise that aligns with fundamentals" and add positions on those. Hybrid value-investing + PEAD strategy, more feasible than pure PEAD arbitrage.

  5. Q5. 2020-11-03 蚂蚁 IPO 暂停后, 港股阿里 60 天 CAR ≈ −25%, A 股茅台 60 天 CAR ≈ −8% (即使两者基本面无关)。茅台的 −8% 算"不合理"吗? 用 event study 方法分解。Q5. Post-Ant 2020-11-03, HK Alibaba 60-day CAR ≈ −25%, A‑share Moutai 60-day CAR ≈ −8% (despite no business overlap). Is Moutai's −8% "irrational"? Decompose via event study.

    "看似不合理"的 −8% 实际可分解为三部分:

    1. 市场 beta 传导: 蚂蚁事件冲击了沪深 300, 沪深 300 60 天 −3%。茅台 β ≈ 0.95, 这部分应贡献 −3% × 0.95 ≈ −2.8%。
    2. 板块情绪传导 (sentiment spillover): 监管事件之后, 投资者对"行业头部公司"的监管溢价 (regulatory premium)普遍提高。茅台作为消费龙头, 一直有"政策风险" (反腐反三公消费历史阴影 + 高端消费的政策属性), 这部分溢价被重估, 贡献 −2% 到 −3%。
    3. 残差: 真正不合理部分: −8% − (−2.8%) − (−2.5%) ≈ −2.7%, 这是抢踩踏+风险偏好下降的非理性溢出。

    判定: −8% 中约 65% (5.3 个百分点) 是合理的 (beta + 监管溢价上调); 约 35% (2.7 个百分点) 是非理性(踩踏)。后者是逆向小仓位加仓的机会, 前者不是。

    历史验证: 茅台在 2020-11-03 后 60 天 −8%, 但 90-180 天恢复到事件前水平甚至超过, 反映那 2.7% 非理性部分被市场自我修正。这一现象与 Layer 1 老王观察到的"实控人减持 −10% 后 6 月恢复"模式一致 — 都是情绪性反应被基本面修正。

    方法论提醒: 把 CAR 分解为 (beta + sector + residual) 是 event study 的标准做法 (Fama-French 三因子模型扩展)。残差 (residual abnormal return) 才是真正"个股层面的反应", 这才是判断"过度反应 / 不足反应"的正确指标。直接看 CAR 不分解会得出错误结论。

    The −8% decomposes into three parts:

    1. Market beta transmission: Ant event hit CSI 300 by −3% over 60 days. Moutai β ≈ 0.95 → contributes −3% × 0.95 ≈ −2.8%.
    2. Sector sentiment spillover: regulatory events raise investors' regulatory premium on all leading firms. Moutai, a consumer leader, has historical policy-risk overhangs (anti-corruption / "three-publics-consumption" history + high-end consumer political sensitivity). Re-priced premium contributes −2% to −3%.
    3. Residual: truly irrational part: −8% − (−2.8%) − (−2.5%) ≈ −2.7%; rush-to-exit + risk-aversion non-rational spillover.

    Verdict: ~65% of the −8% is rational (beta + regulatory-premium repricing); ~35% (≈2.7%) is irrational (panic). The latter is a contrarian small-add opportunity; the former isn't.

    Historical validation: Moutai 60-day CAR was −8%, but at 90–180 days fully recovered or exceeded pre-event — the 2.7% non-rational portion self-corrected. Same pattern as Layer-1 insider-reduction case: emotional reaction → fundamental correction.

    Methodological note: decomposing CAR into (beta + sector + residual) is the standard refinement (Fama-French extension). The residual abnormal return is the true "stock-level reaction" and the correct metric for over/under-reaction. Reading raw CAR without decomposition leads to wrong conclusions.
