证券投资学 — 模块导览Investments — Module Overview

M1 教读财报, M2 教用财报做公司财务决策, M3 教给资产定价。三大支柱: 现代组合理论 (MPT) + 资产定价 (CAPM/APT/FF) + 估值 (DCF/相对估值/价值投资)。再加 EMH 与行为金融的张力 — 这是所有 A 股投资判断的理论基础。 M1 reads, M2 uses, M3 prices. Three pillars: MPT + asset pricing + valuation. Plus EMH vs behavioral tension. The theoretical foundation for any A‑share decision.

Objectives
  • 构建均值-方差最优组合并理解有效前沿
  • 用 CAPM / Fama-French 估算任意 A 股 $r_e$
  • 用 DCF 和相对估值评估任意 A 股内在价值
  • 用价值投资框架 (安全边际 / 护城河 / 能力圈) 做选股
  • 解读 A 股公告 (业绩预告 / 定增 / 回购 / 限售解禁 / 问询函)
  • Build mean‑variance portfolios; understand the efficient frontier
  • Estimate $r_e$ via CAPM / Fama‑French
  • Value firms via DCF and multiples
  • Apply value investing (margin of safety / moat / circle of competence)
  • Read A‑share filings (earnings preannouncements, placements, buybacks, lock‑ups, inquiry letters)

First-Principles Mental Models

i

复利Compounding

指数增长反直觉。长期持有 + 红利再投资 = 价值投资的数学基础。

Exponential growth is counterintuitive. Long holding + reinvestment = math of value investing.

ii

能力圈Circle of competence

Buffett: 只投自己能理解的业务。不在能力圈内的投资本质是赌博。

Only invest in what you can understand. Outside it = gambling.

iii

Mr. Market

Graham: 市场是个情绪化的合伙人, 每天报价但你可以选择不交易。情绪是机会, 不是指引。

Graham: Mr. Market quotes daily but you choose to act. Emotions are opportunities, not guidance.

iv

安全边际Margin of safety

Graham 核心: 只在价格显著低于内在价值时买入. 防止估值错误的工程冗余设计。

Buy only when price is significantly below intrinsic value — engineering redundancy against valuation errors.

v

护城河Moat

五种类型: 品牌 / 规模 / 转换成本 / 网络效应 / 特许经营. 反映长期超额回报的可持续性。

Five types: brand / scale / switching costs / network effects / franchise. Drives sustained excess returns.

vi

回归均值Mean reversion

极端不持续。任何指标偏离长期均值, 终将回归。逆向投资的数学基础。

Extremes don't persist. Any metric far from its long‑run mean reverts. Math of contrarian investing.

vii

反身性Reflexivity

Soros: 价格影响基本面 (融资能力 / 信心 / 客户行为). 因果不是单向的。泡沫与崩溃的核心机制。

Soros: prices shape fundamentals. Two‑way causality. Core of bubbles and crashes.

专家最激烈的分歧Where Experts Disagree

EMHEMH 是否成立Does EMH hold?
Fama (Nobel 2013)
市场有效Markets efficient

所有可得信息瞬间反映在价格。主动投资长期跑不赢被动指数 (扣费后).

All info reflected instantly. Active loses to passive net of fees.

Shiller (Nobel 2013)
市场显著无效Markets persistently inefficient

"非理性繁荣". 股价波动远超基本面波动. 行为偏差产生可预测的回报模式。

"Irrational exuberance." Volatility far exceeds fundamentals.

2013 Nobel 同时颁给两人 — 学界至今没有定论. 实务上用"弱有效"判断短期, "半强不完全有效"判断中期, "理性长期回归"判断长期.
价值 vs 成长Value vs Growth
Fama-French (value premium)
价值股长期跑赢Value premium

低 P/B 股票 1927-2007 年化跑赢高 P/B ~5%. HML 因子被多次实证.

Low P/B stocks beat high P/B by ~5% annualized (1927–2007). HML factor.

Lynch / FAANG 投资者
伟大成长股不可替代Growth dominates

2010-2021 FAANG 大幅跑赢价值股. 在科技革命周期, 成长股是新常态。

2010–21 FAANG crushed value. In tech revolutions, growth dominates.

主动 vs 被动Active vs Passive
Bogle (Vanguard)
被动指数最优Passive wins

主动基金扣费后 70%+ 跑输指数. 长期持有低成本指数 = 普通投资者最优策略.

70%+ active funds lose to index net of fees. Low‑cost index = optimal default.

Buffett / 对冲基金派hedge funds
少数主动者长期跑赢Selected active wins

Buffett 1965-2023 年化 ~20%, 远超 S&P 500 ~10%. Renaissance Medallion Fund 30 年 ~40% 年化. 少数策略持续创造 alpha.

Buffett 1965–2023 ~20% vs S&P 10%. Medallion ~40% for 30y. A few strategies persistently outperform.
