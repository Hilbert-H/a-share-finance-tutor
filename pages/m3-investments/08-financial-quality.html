Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · 老王盯着五份年报, 五个红绿灯 · Wang stares at five filings, five traffic lights

老王上周从证券公司客户经理那里听到一句话: "看年报最重要的不是看净利润, 是看净利润真不真"。回家以后他把手机里关注的 5 只 A 股年报全打开, 准备用 5 个"红绿灯"问题逐一检验。每只他只问 5 件事, 任何一盏亮红灯就直接出局, 不勉强。

Last week Wang's broker said something that stuck: "What matters most in an annual report isn't the NI number — it's whether the NI is real." He opens five filings on his phone and runs each through five traffic lights. Any red — out, no excuses.

1. 第一只: 某创业板新能源公司, 净利润 +120%, 老王查"红绿灯一: 经营现金流 / 净利润"。年报里 NI = 8 亿, 但经营现金流 (OCF) 只有 1.5 亿。OCF/NI = 0.19, 远低于 1。老王琢磨: 公司说自己赚了 8 亿, 但银行账上实际只到账 1.5 亿, 剩下 6.5 亿在哪里?——挂在应收账款里。即"客户答应给我 8 亿, 但今年只到账了 1.5 亿, 剩下 6.5 亿欠着, 算我的利润"。这种"利润已认、钱没到手"的状态叫 纸面利润 (paper profit)。红灯。老王放弃这只。

1. First name: a ChiNext new-energy firm, NI up 120%. Wang checks light #1: OCF/NI. Report shows NI = ¥800m, but operating cash flow only ¥150m. OCF/NI = 0.19, far below 1. Translation: the firm claims it earned ¥800m, but only ¥150m hit the bank — the other ¥650m sits in accounts receivable. "Customers promised ¥800m, paid ¥150m, the rest is on credit, I'm calling it profit." That's paper profit. Red. Wang passes.

2. 第二只: 某医疗设备公司, 营收 +60%, 应收账款 +210%。这是 红绿灯二: 应收账款增速 vs 收入增速。营收增 60%, 但欠老王的钱 (应收) 涨了 210%。这意味着: 要么客户付款速度显著变慢 (信用质量下降), 要么更可能—— 一些"收入"是放宽信用压货给经销商挂出来的 (Channel stuffing, 渠道压货)。老王想起 Beneish 那个论文里的"DSRI 指标" — 这是经典的虚增收入预警。红灯。出局。

2. Second name: a medical-device firm, revenue +60%, AR +210%. Light #2: AR growth vs revenue growth. Revenue +60%, money customers owe +210%. Either customers are paying drastically slower (credit quality eroded), or — more likely — some "revenue" is channel-stuffed onto distributors via loosened credit terms. Beneish's DSRI ratio flags exactly this. Red. Out.

3. 第三只: 某重工设备厂商, 净利润 +30%, 但翻附注发现"折旧政策从 8 年延长到 12 年"。这是 红绿灯三: 折旧 / 摊销政策变更。同样 1 亿的设备, 之前 8 年折旧每年 1,250 万, 现在 12 年折旧每年 833 万 — 每年少计 417 万折旧, 净利就凭空多出 417 万 (税后 ≈ 350 万)。如果设备规模是 50 亿, 这一个动作就让 NI 凭空多 17.5 亿。老王看了同行业的折旧年限: 三一重工 6-10 年, 中联重科 8-10 年, 这家突然延到 12 年 — 红灯。出局。会计上没违规, 但管理层的动机暴露无遗。

3. Third name: a heavy-equipment maker, NI +30%. Footnote: depreciation life extended from 8 years to 12 years. Light #3: depreciation policy change. Same ¥100m equipment — 8-year life = ¥12.5m/y; 12-year = ¥8.33m/y; saves ¥4.17m/y per ¥100m of fixed assets. Scale to ¥5bn of assets → NI inflated by ¥1.75bn from this one move. Peer life: Sany 6–10y, Zoomlion 8–10y; this firm jumped to 12y. Red. Out. Not illegal — but management's motive is fully exposed.

4. 第四只: 某地方化工公司, 净利润 +80%, 翻附注发现"关联方往来"高达净资产 30%。这是 红绿灯四: 关联方交易 / 关联方占款。老王盯着附注看 — "公司向控股股东 XX 集团销售 5 亿、采购 3 亿、其他应收 12 亿"。控股股东和公司在同一个口袋里把钱倒来倒去, 5 亿"销售"很可能是把仓库里的货搬到关联方账上, 财报上变成"已确认收入"。这是 A 股造假最经典的套路: 通过关联方虚增收入 + 关联方占款隐藏现金流出。红灯。出局。规则: 关联方占款 (其他应收 + 其他应付里关联方部分) / 净资产 > 10% 直接放弃。

4. Fourth name: a local chemicals firm, NI +80%. Footnote: related-party balances ~30% of net assets. Light #4: related-party transactions / extractions. Notes say: "Sales to controlling shareholder XX Group ¥500m; purchases ¥300m; other receivables ¥1.2bn." Money churns between parent and listco within one pocket — "sales" are likely just inventory moved onto parent's books and booked as revenue. The classic A‑share fraud pattern. Red. Out. Rule: related-party "other receivables/payables" > 10% of equity → automatic disqualify.

5. 第五只: 某医药公司, 净利润 +150%, 但仔细看构成: 净利 5 亿里有 4 亿是"出售子公司股权"的一次性投资收益。这是 红绿灯五: 非经常性损益占净利比。"经常性"利润 (主营业务赚的) 只有 1 亿; 真正的 NI 是 4 亿一次性 + 1 亿经常。明年还能卖一次子公司吗?不能。所以明年合理的 NI 预测是 1 亿, 不是 5 亿。但当下市场如果按 5 亿乘以 P/E 30 给估值 → 严重高估。黄灯 (不一定红, 但需要剥离非经常性损益重新估值)。老王把它放在观察清单, 等下一年看主营 NI 是否真的能撑住。

5. Fifth name: a pharma firm, NI +150%. Drill in: of ¥500m NI, ¥400m is a one-off gain on disposing a subsidiary. Light #5: non-recurring items / NI. Recurring (operations) NI = ¥100m. True forward NI estimate is ¥100m, not ¥500m. If the market still values it at P/E 30 × ¥500m, it's massively overpriced. Yellow. Move to watch list — see if next year's operating NI holds up without the one-off.

傍晚总结。 老王今天 5 只样本, 4 红 1 黄, 0 通过。这正是 Buffett 说的 "看 100 家公司, 99 家都该 NO"。这五盏红绿灯不是要"诬陷"上市公司, 而是把那些用各种会计技巧粉饰利润的样本从你的关注池里筛掉, 让剩下的、真正干干净净赚钱的公司浮出水面。后面 Layer 2 会把这五个红绿灯背后的统计学 (Sloan accruals anomaly, Beneish M-score) 写清楚, Layer 3 会用 A 股最经典的造假案例 (康得新 002450, 康美药业 600518) 验证, 并用茅台 (600519) 作为"对照组" — 这五盏灯在一家真正高质量公司身上长什么样。

Wrap-up. Wang's day: 5 samples, 4 red + 1 yellow, 0 pass. Exactly Buffett's "99 of 100 should be NO." The five lights aren't meant to slander listed firms — they sift out cosmetically-dressed earnings so the genuinely clean cash-makers float to the top. Layer 2 below gives the statistics (Sloan accruals anomaly, Beneish M-score); Layer 3 validates with two classic A‑share fraud cases (Kangde Xin 002450, Kangmei 600518) and uses Moutai as the control — what these five lights look like on a truly high-quality firm.

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

Layer 1 用 5 盏红绿灯把 4 只 A 股筛掉、留下 1 只观察。现在把每盏灯背后的代数与文献写出来。中心思想由 Richard Sloan 1996 年那篇 The Accounting Review 经典论文奠定: 净利润可以拆成现金部分和应计部分, 应计部分的持续性远低于现金部分。其余四盏灯都是这条 first principle 的不同侧面。

Layer 1 ran five lights against five names. Layer 2 writes the algebra and lineage. The keystone: Sloan 1996 (The Accounting Review) — NI decomposes into a cash component and an accrual component; the accrual component is far less persistent than the cash component. The other four lights are facets of this single first principle.

质量检验的根基: NI = Cash + Accruals

The keystone: NI = Cash + Accruals

利润分解Earnings decomposition
$$ \mathrm{NI}_t \;=\; \mathrm{CFO}_t \;+\; \mathrm{Accruals}_t $$(8.1)
$$ \mathrm{Accruals}_t \;=\; \Delta\mathrm{WC}_t \;-\; \mathrm{D\&A}_t \;+\; \text{Other non-cash adj.} $$(8.2)

CFO = 经营性现金流; ΔWC = 营运资金净增 (应收 + 存货 − 应付); D&A = 折旧摊销。这一拆与 M1 Ch.09 间接法现金流量表数学上完全一致。

CFO = cash from operations; ΔWC = increase in working capital; D&A = depreciation & amortization. Mathematically identical to the indirect-method cash-flow statement (M1 Ch.09).

Sloan, R. (1996). "Do Stock Prices Fully Reflect Information in Accruals and Cash Flows about Future Earnings?" The Accounting Review, 71(3), 289–315.

Sloan 1996 的实证结论: 把美国市场所有公司按 Accruals / 总资产 排序, 取最低 10% (低应计组) 和最高 10% (高应计组), 一年后看股价表现 — 低应计组年化超额收益约 +5%, 高应计组约 −5%, 差异 ~10%。这个现象后来被称为 accruals anomaly (应计异象), 在多个市场重复验证, 是行为金融最稳健的发现之一。机制: 应计利润不持续, 市场短期不区分, 长期被迫修正 — 高应计公司报表"好看"但下期 NI 多半回落, 股价跟着掉。

Sloan 1996 empirical: sort all US firms by Accruals / Total Assets; low-accrual decile +5% annual abnormal return, high-accrual decile −5%, ~10% spread. The accruals anomaly has replicated across markets and is one of the most robust behavioral findings. Mechanism: accrual earnings don't persist; markets ignore the distinction short-run and correct long-run. "Pretty" high-accrual firms see NI revert next period; price follows.

红绿灯一: OCF / NI 比率

Light #1: OCF / NI

$$ \mathrm{Cash\,Quality} \;=\; \frac{\mathrm{CFO}}{\mathrm{NI}} $$(8.3)

阈值: 5 年平均 > 1 是高质量信号 (经营赚到的现金 ≥ 报表利润, 多出来的部分主要是折旧加回); 5 年平均 < 0.7 是预警; < 0.5 是严重警告。注意单年波动正常 — 重资本支出年、应收账款周期变化都会造成单年异常, 应用 3-5 年滚动平均。

Thresholds: 5-year average > 1 = high quality (cash ≥ reported, surplus mostly D&A add-back); < 0.7 = warning; < 0.5 = severe. Single-year noise is normal — use 3–5 year rolling.

红绿灯二: AR 增速 vs 收入增速 (Beneish DSRI 的基础)

Light #2: AR growth vs revenue growth (Beneish DSRI core)

$$ \mathrm{DSRI}_t \;=\; \frac{(\mathrm{AR}_t / \mathrm{Sales}_t)}{(\mathrm{AR}_{t-1} / \mathrm{Sales}_{t-1})} $$(8.4)

DSRI > 1.31 是 Beneish (1999) 给出的"造假" 警戒阈值。直觉: 销售应收比从 0.2 跳到 0.3 (即 DSRI = 1.5), 意味着"赚的钱里更多是赊账", 这是放宽信用、虚增收入、渠道压货的共同财务表现。

Beneish (1999): DSRI > 1.31 is the manipulation threshold. Intuition: AR/Sales ratio jumping from 0.2 to 0.3 (DSRI = 1.5) means more of "earnings" is on credit — the joint signature of credit loosening, revenue inflation, and channel stuffing.

红绿灯三 + 四 + 五: 折旧政策 / 关联方 / 非经常性损益

Lights #3 #4 #5

Beneish 检测能力: 1999 原始样本对 74 例已知造假公司, M-score 在事前能识别 ~76%。最著名的事后应用: 安然 (Enron) 在 1998 年 M-score 已经 > −1.78, 距离 2001 年破产还有 3 年。在 A 股, 康得新 (002450) 在 2017 年 M-score 已超阈值, 距离 2019 年东窗事发尚有 18 个月。

Beneish detection power: on 1999's 74-fraud sample, M-score flagged ~76% pre-event. Famous retro: Enron's M-score crossed −1.78 in 1998, three years before the 2001 collapse. In A‑shares, Kangde Xin (002450) crossed in 2017, 18 months before exposure in 2019.

配套指标: Altman Z-score + Piotroski F-score

Companions: Altman Z + Piotroski F

Beneish 检测造假风险; Altman Z (1968) 检测破产风险; Piotroski F (2000) 检测财务健康改善方向。三者并用形成"质量三角":

Beneish flags manipulation; Altman Z (1968) flags bankruptcy; Piotroski F (2000) flags direction of financial health. Together: the "quality triangle."

$$ Z = 1.2\,X_1 + 1.4\,X_2 + 3.3\,X_3 + 0.6\,X_4 + 1.0\,X_5 $$(8.6)

$X_1$ 营运资本/总资产, $X_2$ 留存收益/总资产, $X_3$ EBIT/总资产, $X_4$ 市值/总负债, $X_5$ 销售/总资产。Z > 2.99 = 健康; 1.81-2.99 = 灰色区; < 1.81 = 高破产风险。

$X_1$ working capital/TA, $X_2$ retained earnings/TA, $X_3$ EBIT/TA, $X_4$ market cap/TL, $X_5$ sales/TA. Z > 2.99 healthy; 1.81–2.99 grey; < 1.81 high bankruptcy risk.

Piotroski F-score (2000, JAR): 9 个二元指标 (盈利、杠杆/流动性、营运效率), 每个达标得 1 分。F ≥ 7 = 高质量; F ≤ 3 = 低质量。Piotroski 原文实证: 在低 P/B 池里, F > 7 的子集年化超额 +23%, F < 3 的子集年化超额 −9%, 差异 30%+ — 比 magic formula 在低估值池里的表现更强。

Piotroski F-score (2000, JAR): 9 binary indicators (profitability, leverage/liquidity, operating efficiency); each met = 1 point. F ≥ 7 high quality; F ≤ 3 low. Piotroski's evidence: within the low-P/B pool, F>7 subset delivers +23% annual alpha vs F<3 at −9% — 30% spread, stronger than magic formula in low-valuation pools.

Altman, E. (1968). "Financial Ratios, Discriminant Analysis and the Prediction of Corporate Bankruptcy." Journal of Finance, 23(4). · Piotroski, J. (2000). "Value Investing: The Use of Historical Financial Statement Information to Separate Winners from Losers." Journal of Accounting Research, 38, 1–41.

三角的逻辑: 三个维度缺一不可。质量缺 → 雷 (康得新型造假); 估值缺 → 漂亮但贵, 长期回报塌 (2021 茅指数高位); 成长缺 → 价值陷阱 (传统出版、煤炭某些子板块结构性衰退)。

Triangle logic: all three are required. Missing quality → landmine (Kangde Xin); missing valuation → "pretty but expensive," long-run return collapses (2021 Moutai herd); missing growth → value trap (legacy publishing, parts of coal in structural decline).

文献交叉: 这个三角和 Joel Greenblatt magic formula (Ch.07) 的关系是: magic formula 把"质量"压成 ROC、把"估值"压成 EBIT/EV, 两个一起排, 简化成可以等权组合的统计套利。三角框架是 magic formula 的"详细版" — 慢、需要读年报, 但能识别 magic formula 看不到的造假与陷阱。两者互补, 不是对立。

Cross-lineage: Greenblatt's magic formula (Ch.07) compresses quality → ROC and valuation → EBIT/EV into a two-rank statistical arbitrage. The triangle is the "verbose version" — slower, requires reading filings, but catches frauds and traps invisible to the magic formula. Complementary, not opposed.

会计与投资的桥: Layer 2 的所有指标都直接基于 M1 学过的财报科目。OCF/NI 来自现金流量表; Accruals 是间接法的反推; 关联方占款在附注; 折旧政策在附注; 非经常性损益是 A 股财报必披的附表。"会计学不是学会做账, 而是学会反过来读账" — 这是 Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe Corporate Finance 教材中财报章节的核心立意。

The bridge from accounting to investing: every Layer 2 metric maps back to M1 line items. OCF/NI from the cash-flow statement; accruals from the indirect-method inversion; related-party in notes; depreciation policy in notes; non-recurring items in an A‑share mandated supplementary schedule. Ross-Westerfield-Jaffe's core message in the financials chapter: "accounting isn't about learning to keep books — it's about learning to read books backwards."

Layer 3 · A 股案例A‑Share Cases

  1. Q1. 三角框架: 质量高 + 估值便宜 + 成长低 = 投资 or 不投资?Q1. High quality + cheap valuation + low growth — invest?

    • 若低增长是暂时 (周期下行) + 公司在能力圈 → 可投, 等周期反转
    • 若低增长是结构性 (行业衰退, 例如某些传统出版、煤炭子板块) → 价值陷阱, 不投
    • 核心问: 公司未来 5-10 年还能产生现金吗? 若能, 高股息率 + 回购 = 长期 OK

    用 (8.3) OCF/NI 5 年均值判断: 若过去 5 年 OCF/NI > 1 且持续, 即使 NI 不增长, 现金流也稳定 — 这种是债性资产 (类似长江电力)。若 OCF/NI 持续下行, 说明现金生成能力在塌, 即"质量"维度其实也在恶化, 三角实际是"质量下降 + 估值便宜 + 成长低", 应直接否决。

    Case-by-case. Often a value trap; sometimes deep value. Distinguish cyclical vs structural decline; if cyclical and within competence, can be long-run play. Key question: does the firm still generate cash in 5–10 years? If yes, high dividend + buybacks → long-run OK (bond-like asset, akin to Yangtze Power).

    Use 5-year OCF/NI (8.3): if > 1 and stable, cash flow is stable even without growth — bond-like. If OCF/NI is itself declining, "quality" leg is also breaking; the real triangle is "quality down + cheap + low growth" — fail.

  3. Q3. 一家公司账面货币资金 50 亿, 年度利息收入 0.5 亿。这说明什么? 用同一份年报内部数据反算, 真实货币资金可能是多少?Q3. A firm reports ¥5bn cash and annual interest income ¥50m. What's wrong? Reconcile within the filing to estimate true cash.

    反算: 利息收入 / 货币资金 = 0.5 / 50 = 1% — 远低于活期 (~0.3% 但要乘 12 个月平均) + 定期 (2-3%) 的合理范围。一般公司账面现金加权平均年化收益应在 1.5-3% (混合活期 + 通知存款 + 短期理财)。

    若实际加权年化收益 2%, 反算真实"货币资金" ≈ 0.5 / 2% = 25 亿。账面 50 亿 - 真实 25 亿 = 差 25 亿。

    解释这 25 亿的去向:

    • (a) 被关联方占用 (康得新型: 122 亿被集团"现金管理")
    • (b) 虚增 (康美型: 直接造假)
    • (c) 限制性使用 (作为抵押 / 保证金 / 关联方融资担保)
    • (d) 被冻结 (诉讼 / 监管查封)

    公司必须在附注披露 (c)(d), 若附注未披露 → 高度怀疑 (a) 或 (b)。无论哪种, 投资者的合理结论都是"账面货币资金不可信, 真实流动性 50% 折算"。这一手法在著名做空报告中反复使用。

    Ratio 0.5/50 = 1% — far below the 1.5–3% blended yield on a mixed current + notice + short-term wealth portfolio. At 2% blended, implied true cash ≈ 0.5/0.02 = ¥2.5bn. Gap ¥2.5bn. Possible explanations: (a) extracted by related parties (Kangde Xin pattern); (b) outright inflated (Kangmei pattern); (c) restricted (collateral / guarantee); (d) frozen (litigation / regulator). (c) and (d) must be disclosed in notes; absence → suspect (a) or (b). Either way, sane investor conclusion: "book cash unreliable, true liquidity ≈ 50% haircut." Used repeatedly in famous short reports.

  4. Q4. 五盏红绿灯都设置在"高造假风险预警"侧。这种"高误警 (false alarm) + 低误漏 (miss)"的设计是不是太保守, 会让你错过大量好公司?Q4. The five lights err on "high false-alarm + low miss-rate." Isn't this so conservative it misses many great firms?

    是设计选择, 不是失误。投资中的损失非对称: 踩雷 −80% 到 −100% (康得新、康美、暴风、瑞幸), 错过好公司只是机会成本 (年化少 5-10%)。Kahneman 损失厌恶 (2.5x): 大多数投资者宁可少赚 10% 也不愿亏 80%。所以五盏灯设计成"宁可错过 99 家好公司, 也不踩 1 家雷"。

    统计学解释: 把它当作分类器, 设计目标是高 recall (识别所有造假, 误漏低) 而非高 precision (识别的都是造假)。投资中, 一次踩雷的损失远大于 99 次误警的机会成本。Buffett 1996: "Rule No. 1: Never lose money. Rule No. 2: Never forget Rule No. 1."

    错过的代价多大? A 股 5,300 只里, 五盏灯严苛筛选后剩 ~1,000 只。即使丢掉的 4,300 只里有 1,000 只是"被冤枉的好公司", 剩下的 1,000 池子仍然足够大 — 老王一年能深入研究 20-30 只就到极限了, 池子大小不是约束。所以"错过好公司"的边际成本极低, 而"踩雷"的边际成本极高。这就是非对称损益分布下的最优筛选策略。

    It's a design choice, not a defect. Investing losses are asymmetric: stepping on a fraud is −80% to −100% (Kangde Xin, Kangmei, Storm, Luckin); missing a good firm is only opportunity cost (~5–10% p.a.). Kahneman's loss aversion (2.5×): most investors prefer missing 10% gains to taking 80% losses. So design: "rather miss 99 great firms than touch 1 fraud."

    Statistically: the five-light classifier targets high recall (catch all frauds), not high precision. A single fraud hit dwarfs the opportunity cost of 99 false alarms. Buffett 1996: "Rule No. 1: Never lose money. Rule No. 2: Never forget Rule No. 1."

    How costly is missing? After strict filtering, ~1,000 names remain from 5,300 A‑shares. Even if 1,000 of the dropped 4,300 are "wrongly accused good firms," the remaining 1,000 is still far larger than Wang can deeply research (~20–30 per year). Pool size isn't the binding constraint. Marginal cost of missing ≈ 0; marginal cost of touching a fraud ≈ ∞. This is the optimal filter under asymmetric loss.

  5. Q5. Sloan 1996 accruals anomaly 在 1996 年发表, 至今近 30 年。理论上, 公开发表的赚钱的异象应该被套利掉。它为什么仍然存在?Q5. Sloan 1996 has been public for nearly 30 years. Anomalies that pay should be arbitraged away. Why does it persist?

    这是金融学界一个长期问题, 至少四种解释:

    1. 套利成本 (limits to arbitrage, Shleifer-Vishny 1997): 做空高应计公司风险大 — 短期可能继续涨, 借券成本高, 强制平仓风险。专业投资者面对客户赎回压力, 无法承受长期等待。所以即使知道异象存在, 也没多少资金去消除它。
    2. 行为偏差持续 (Kahneman-Tversky 1979): 投资者对"过去高增长"过度乐观, 对"过去现金质量差"麻木 — 这是大脑层面的偏差, 论文发表不能改变它。理性"信息已传播"假设在行为层面不成立。
    3. 样本扩展: Sloan 原样本是美国 1962-1991。后续研究 (Lewellen 2010, Hirshleifer et al 2012) 在全球各市场重复验证, 异象幅度有缓慢下降但仍显著存在, 中国 A 股 2010-2020 测算约 4-6% (低于美国 10% 但仍 positive)。
    4. 异象的结构性本质: 应计 vs 现金的差异本身是会计制度造成的 — accrual accounting 必然产生这种张力, 只要制度不变, 异象的生成机制就不变。这与 P/E 异象不同 — P/E 异象可以被套利平掉一部分, 但应计异象的"源头"是会计制度本身。

    Buffett 的观察 (1989 致股东信, 间接表达): "If past history was all there was to the game, the richest people would be librarians." 知道异象 ≠ 能赚到。能赚到需要: 套利资本 + 心理素质 + 时间。前两者大多数投资者都缺。

    A long-running puzzle, four explanations:

    1. Limits to arbitrage (Shleifer-Vishny 1997): shorting high-accrual firms is risky — they can rally further, borrow costs, forced cover. Professionals face redemption pressure and can't ride it out. So even with the anomaly public, arbitrage capital is too thin to eliminate it.
    2. Persistent behavioral bias (Kahneman-Tversky 1979): investors over-extrapolate past growth and ignore past cash quality — brain-level bias, publication doesn't fix.
    3. Out-of-sample replication: Sloan's original sample was US 1962–91. Lewellen 2010, Hirshleifer et al 2012 replicate globally; spread has slowly compressed but remains; A‑share 2010–20 ~4–6% (vs US ~10%, still positive).
    4. Structural source: the accrual-cash gap is created by accrual accounting itself. As long as the institution stands, the generation mechanism stands. Different from P/E anomaly (partly arbitrageable) — accrual anomaly's source is the accounting system.

    Buffett 1989 (paraphrased): "If past history was all there was to the game, the richest people would be librarians." Knowing the anomaly ≠ harvesting it. Harvesting needs arbitrage capital + psychological stamina + time — most investors lack the first two.
