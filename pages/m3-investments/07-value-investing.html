价值投资框架Value Investing Framework

Graham → Buffett → Munger 的投资哲学. 核心: 内在价值 + 安全边际 + 能力圈 + 护城河 + Mr. Market. 这是估值模型 (Ch.05-06) 之上的选股决策框架 — 估值告诉你"值多少", 价值投资告诉你"该不该买"。Graham → Buffett → Munger philosophy. Five pillars: intrinsic value, margin of safety, circle of competence, moat, Mr. Market. The decision framework above valuation models — Ch.05–06 tell you "what it's worth," value investing tells you "whether to buy."

学习目标Objectives
  • 把"价格 vs 价值"这件事从直觉变成可计算的安全边际 (MOS) 公式
  • 识别 Buffett 的四把尺子: 看得懂的业务 / 护城河 / 老实的管理层 / 显著低价
  • 用 Graham number、owner earnings、Greenblatt magic formula 三种估值工具交叉验证
  • 把"护城河"五种类型 (品牌、规模、转换成本、网络效应、特许经营) 与 A 股标的对号入座
  • Translate "price vs value" into a computable margin-of-safety formula.
  • Identify Buffett's four filters: understandable business, moat, honest management, significantly low price.
  • Cross-check via Graham number, owner earnings, Greenblatt magic formula.
  • Map five moat types to specific A‑share franchises.

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · 老王手里 12 万, 想用 Buffett 的方法挑 A 股 · ¥120k of free cash, picking A‑shares the Buffett way

老王最近读完了一本巴菲特股东信合订本, 也读了那本黄色封皮的《聪明的投资者》。他手里有 12 万块的闲钱, 想拿 8 万试试"价值投资", 4 万先放着。但他不知道从哪里下手 — 沪深两市加起来 5,300 多家公司, 总不能一家家翻年报。他把 Buffett 的话总结成四把尺子, 决定一只一只筛过去, 任何一把过不去就直接放过, 不勉强。

Old Wang just finished a bound volume of Buffett's shareholder letters and the yellow-jacket "Intelligent Investor." He has ¥120k of free cash, plans to commit ¥80k to "value investing" and park the rest. He doesn't know where to start — Shanghai and Shenzhen list 5,300+ companies; he can't read every annual report. So he condenses Buffett into four filters: any name that fails any one filter is silently passed over, no excuses.

1. 早上, 老王盯着自己的手机, 想从"看得懂的业务"开始筛。 他打开第一只: 某基因测序公司, 招股书里写满"NGS 平台"、"多组学"、"液体活检"。老王盯着看了十分钟, 还是不能用一句话向他媳妇解释这家公司怎么赚钱。他问自己: 我能不能预测它三年后会比今年更赚钱?——不能, 我连它的产品长什么样都不清楚。这只直接划掉。这一动作就是 Buffett 反复讲的 能力圈 (circle of competence): 不在我能力圈里的, 哪怕涨得再凶, 都跟我无关。

1. Morning: Wang opens his app and starts with "businesses I can explain." First name up — a genomics firm, prospectus stuffed with "NGS platform," "multi-omics," "liquid biopsy." Ten minutes in, he still can't tell his wife in one sentence how it makes money. Question: can I predict whether it earns more in three years than today? No — I don't even know what the product looks like. Cross it out. This move is what Buffett calls staying inside your circle of competence: things outside it don't exist, no matter how hot they run.

2. 中午, 他切到一家自己熟悉的: 茅台 (600519)。 业务一句话: 把高粱酒装瓶卖出去, 一瓶飞天 1,499 元出厂价, 经销商抢着拿货还要预付。这老王懂 — 他自己婚宴上买过, 朋友升职买过, 春节送礼买过。他再问第二把尺子: 它有护城河吗?——有。第一, 品牌: "茅台"两个字在中国白酒里就是质量信号; 第二, 产地: 必须用赤水河的水, 别人造不出来; 第三, 时间: 基酒要存五年, 谁也没法弯道超车。这三条加起来, 就是 Buffett 笔下的"护城河 (economic moat)": 别人想抢你的生意, 得先填掉一条又宽又深的护城河, 而护城河上还有鳄鱼。老王把茅台留下, 进下一步。

2. Noon: he switches to one he actually knows — Moutai (600519). Business in one sentence: bottle sorghum liquor, ship at ¥1,499 per flagship bottle ex-factory, distributors fight to prepay. Wang gets this — he bought it for his own wedding banquet, for a friend's promotion, for Spring Festival gifts. Second filter: does it have a moat? Three answers. Brand: in Chinese baijiu, "Moutai" is a quality signal by itself. Geography: it must use water from the Chishui river — nobody else can produce it. Time: base liquor ages five years; you can't shortcut. Together, that's a wide and deep moat with crocodiles in it (Buffett's phrase). Wang keeps Moutai, moves on.

3. 下午, 老王翻到福耀玻璃 (600660)。 这家他也懂 — 老王家车的前挡风玻璃就是福耀的, 4S 店换玻璃几乎只用这家。业务一句话: 给全球车厂做汽车玻璃, 全球份额 30% 以上。护城河呢?第一, 规模: 一条汽车玻璃产线烧的钱够买几十套茅台基酒, 后来者投不起。第二, 客户绑定: 福耀和大众、丰田、特斯拉的合作都是十几年的车型周期, 一旦定型谁都不会随便换。第三, 工艺: 曹德旺干这行 40 年, 设备和良品率别人短期赶不上。三条都成立, 老王想起 Charlie Munger 在 2003 年伯克希尔股东大会上专门提过曹德旺 — 一个会做实业、不乱花钱的老板。第三把尺子是 管理层是不是"老实人": 福耀历年分红率长期在 60% 以上, 没玩花活的高送转、没有突然消失的关联交易, 这就是诚实的信号。福耀留下。

3. Afternoon: Fuyao Glass (600660). Wang knows this one too — the windshield on his own car is Fuyao, and 4S service centres install almost nothing else. Business in one sentence: auto glass for global OEMs, 30%+ world share. Moat? Scale: one auto-glass line costs more than Wang can mentally count; latecomers can't fund it. Customer lock-in: contracts with VW, Toyota, Tesla span entire model cycles, ten years plus, nobody switches midway. Process: founder Cao Dewang has run this for 40 years, equipment and yield are hard to match. Three boxes ticked. Wang remembers Munger praising Cao at the 2003 Berkshire AGM — a builder, not a financial engineer. Third filter: is management honest? Fuyao's payout ratio has sat above 60% for years, no flashy stock splits, no related-party transactions that disappear next year. Honest signal. Fuyao stays.

4. 傍晚, 老王在万华化学 (600309) 这只上停下来。 这家做 MDI (聚氨酯原料), 老王自己用过的乳胶床垫和冰箱保温层都是 MDI 做的, 也算看得懂。护城河?MDI 全球只有 6 家能做, 万华是其中之一, 烟台基地的规模在全球第一, ROIC 长期 20%+, 妥妥的高资本回报。但第四把尺子 (当前价格) 让他犹豫: 万华 P/E 已经 18 倍, 比历史中位数 12 高出 50%。他翻 Ch.05 的 DCF 笔记, 粗算一下 — 假设未来 10 年自由现金流增长 8%, 折现率 10%, 内在价值大概对应 P/E 14 左右。当前价格 P/E 18, 明显贵于内在价值。第四把尺子说: "价格显著低于内在价值才买"。现在价格高于内在价值, 老王放弃, 把它放进观察清单, 等下次大跌再看。

4. Dusk: Wanhua Chemical (600309). They make MDI (the polyurethane backbone in Wang's own latex mattress and refrigerator insulation), so it's inside the circle. Moat? Globally only six firms can produce MDI; Wanhua's Yantai base is #1 by scale; ROIC has held 20%+ for years — a textbook high-return franchise. But the fourth filter (price) gives him pause: P/E is 18, half above the 12 historical median. He re-opens his Ch.05 DCF notes, runs a back-of-envelope: assume FCF grows 8% for ten years, discount at 10%, intrinsic value lands around P/E 14. Current 18 is plainly above intrinsic. Filter four says: only buy when price is significantly below intrinsic value. It isn't. Wang shelves Wanhua into a watch list — wait for the next sell-off.

5. 晚上, 老王最后一只看的是宁德时代 (300750)。 业务老王能描述: 做动力电池, 装在电动车上, 主要客户特斯拉、比亚迪、蔚来、理想都用它。护城河?规模 + 技术专利 + 客户嵌入, 看着像样。管理层?创始人曾毓群是技术出身, 多次回购增持。问题来到第四把尺子: 当前 P/E 22, 但 2021 高点曾到 P/E 130 倍 — 这只票的价格波动大到老王心里发毛。他给自己定一条规矩: 波动率超过我能扛住的程度的, 即使估值合理也少买 — 这是 Buffett 没明说但默认遵守的"心理能力圈"。宁德他买半仓 (4 万), 给自己留出"跌 30% 也能睡得着"的缓冲。

5. Evening: CATL (300750). Wang can describe the business — EV batteries; main customers Tesla, BYD, Nio, Li Auto. Moat? Scale plus patents plus customer integration; checks out. Management? Founder Zeng Yuqun has a research background and has bought stock multiple times. Filter four: current P/E 22, but in 2021 it touched P/E 130 — the price swings make him queasy. Personal rule: even at fair valuation, downsize when volatility exceeds my tolerance — Buffett's unspoken "psychological circle of competence." Wang takes a half position (¥40k), leaves himself a "−30% I can still sleep" buffer.

傍晚总结。 老王今天用四把尺子筛了 5 只: 基因测序公司 (看不懂, 出局) → 茅台 (四把全过, 买 2 万) → 福耀 (四把全过, 买 2 万) → 万华 (三过, 一不过, 进观察清单) → 宁德 (四过, 但波动太大, 买 4 万但减半仓)。8 万块全配完, 剩 4 万现金当 Buffett 说的"子弹" — 等下一次 Mr. Market 抑郁时再开火。这个流程, 用 Buffett 的话说是: "NO 比 YES 重要" — 一年看 100 家, 99 家都说 NO, 这才是价值投资。Layer 2 把这四把尺子背后的代数 (内在价值估算、安全边际公式、护城河量化) 写出来。

Wrap-up. Wang's day, five names, four filters: genomics firm (incomprehensible, out) → Moutai (clears all four, ¥20k) → Fuyao (clears all four, ¥20k) → Wanhua (three of four, watch list) → CATL (four pass but volatility too high, half position ¥40k). The ¥80k is deployed; ¥40k stays in cash as Buffett's "bullets" — wait for Mr. Market's next depression. The flow is Buffett's "NO matters more than YES": look at 100 names a year, say no to 99, that's value investing. ① 内在价值 (intrinsic value, IV)

① Intrinsic Value (IV)

Buffett 对内在价值的定义 (1996 Owner's Manual, Berkshire Hathaway):

Buffett's definition (1996 Owner's Manual):

内在价值Intrinsic value
$$ \mathrm{IV}_0 \;=\; \sum_{t=1}^{\infty} \frac{\mathrm{OE}_t}{(1+r)^t} $$(7.1)

公司在剩余生命期内可分配给所有者的现金流量 (Owner Earnings, OE) 的折现总和。$r$ 通常取长期国债收益率 + 适当风险溢价 (Buffett 实务中约 10%)。

PV of cash distributable to owners over the remaining life of the business. Owner earnings (OE) defined below. $r$ ≈ long-bond yield + risk premium (~10% in Buffett's practice).

Buffett 1996 Owner's Manual; Berkshire Hathaway 2014 letter (re-stated)

Owner earnings 是 Buffett 在 1986 年股东信里造的词 — 比 GAAP 净利润、比 EBITDA、比 FCF 都更接近"股东能拿到的真钱":

"Owner earnings," Buffett's 1986-letter coinage, sits between GAAP NI, EBITDA, and FCF — closest to "real cash distributable to owners":

Owner EarningsOwner Earnings
$$ \mathrm{OE} \;=\; \mathrm{NI} \;+\; D\&A \;+\; \mathrm{NonCash}_{\text{other}} \;-\; \mathrm{CapEx}_{\text{maintenance}} \;-\; \Delta\mathrm{WC} $$(7.2)

NI = 净利润; D&A = 折旧与摊销; CapExmaintenance = 仅用于维持现有产能的资本支出 (而非扩张资本支出); ΔWC = 营运资金净增加。关键: 扩张性 CapEx 不扣 — 因为它在"为未来的 OE 服务"。

NI + D&A + other non-cash items − maintenance CapEx − increase in working capital. Growth CapEx is not deducted: it serves future OE.

Buffett, 1986 letter to shareholders

OE vs FCF 的关键差别: 标准 FCF (M2 Ch.04) 减的是全部 CapEx; OE 只减维持性 CapEx。对于一家高增长公司, OE > FCF, 因为它在为扩张烧钱 — 这是 Buffett 给"成长股"的辩护。

OE vs FCF: textbook FCF subtracts all CapEx; OE subtracts only maintenance CapEx. For growth firms, OE > FCF — Buffett's algebraic defence of high-growth franchises.

② 安全边际 (margin of safety, MOS, Graham 1934)

② Margin of Safety (Graham 1934)

安全边际MOS
$$ \mathrm{MOS} \;=\; 1 \;-\; \frac{P}{\mathrm{IV}} $$(7.3)

买入条件: $\mathrm{MOS} \geq \mathrm{MOS}^*$, 其中 $\mathrm{MOS}^*$ 是阈值 (Graham 经典阈值 50%; 现代实务 30%; 高确定性公司可松到 20%)。

Buy iff $\mathrm{MOS} \geq \mathrm{MOS}^*$ — Graham classical 50%; modern practice 30%; reduce to 20% for very high certainty.

Graham & Dodd, Security Analysis (1934), Ch. 51 · Graham, The Intelligent Investor (1949), Ch. 20

为什么是 30%? Graham 自己说: "因为我估错的可能性 ≥ 30%"。这是工程冗余 (engineering redundancy): 桥梁建造时安全系数取 2-3 倍, 不是因为预期载荷会到 2-3 倍, 而是因为预期载荷的估计本身有误差。同理, IV 的估计误差 ±30% 是常见的 — 折现率从 9% 改到 11%, IV 可以差 25%; 增长率从 6% 改到 8%, IV 又差 20%。MOS 是对这些估计误差的统一缓冲。

Why 30%? Graham: "because my estimate of IV is itself wrong by 30%." This is engineering redundancy. A bridge's 2–3× safety factor isn't because design load will triple — it's because the load estimate has error. Same logic: change $r$ from 9% to 11% and IV swings 25%; change $g$ from 6% to 8% and IV swings 20%. MOS is the unified buffer.

Greenblatt magic formula (1997, The Little Book That Beats the Market): 把美国市场所有股票按两个指标排序 — (1) 资本回报率 ROC = EBIT / (净营运资本 + 净固定资产); (2) 收益率 EBIT/EV。两个排名相加, 取前 30 名等权持有, 每年再平衡。1988-2004 年测试在美国市场年化超额收益约 13%。在 A 股上同样有效但需调整 — 关联占款、商誉减值等会扭曲 ROC, 需先筛掉。

Greenblatt magic formula (1997): rank all stocks by (1) return on capital = EBIT / (net WC + net PPE) and (2) earnings yield EBIT/EV. Sum both ranks, hold top 30 equal-weight, rebalance annually. 1988–2004 US test: ~13% annual alpha. Works on A‑shares too, after filtering out related-party extractions and goodwill distortions that contaminate ROC.

③ 能力圈 (circle of competence)

③ Circle of Competence

Buffett 1996 Owner's Manual: "能力圈的边界清晰比它的大小重要。" 即: 一个只懂 5 个行业但严守边界的投资者, 期望收益 > 自以为懂 50 个行业的投资者。

Buffett 1996 Owner's Manual: "What matters is not the size of the circle but knowing its boundary." An investor who knows only five industries but stays inside > an investor who thinks she knows fifty.

能力圈的可操作判定: 你能不能给一个十岁孩子在 3 分钟内讲清楚这家公司怎么赚钱? 能否估计它三年后会更赚钱还是更不赚钱? 不能 → 出能力圈。

Operational test: can you explain how the firm makes money to a 10-year-old in 3 minutes? Can you say whether it'll earn more or less three years out? If not, you're outside the circle.

护城河的可持续性 (durability)是另一个维度。Buffett 多次强调"护城河必须每年加宽"。判断方法 (Dorsey 2008):

Moat durability is a separate axis. Buffett: "the moat must widen every year." Dorsey's diagnostics:

  1. ROIC 长期序列: 连续 10 年 ROIC > 15% 是强护城河的统计标志
  2. 毛利率的方向: 上升 → 护城河加宽; 下滑 → 护城河被侵蚀
  3. 行业份额: 稳定上升 + 行业集中度提高
  4. 新进入者出现频率: 一年新进 0-1 家 → 护城河保护; 一年新进 5+ → 护城河失效
  1. 10-year ROIC > 15% — statistical mark of a wide moat.
  2. Direction of gross margin: rising = widening; falling = eroding.
  3. Industry share: stable-rising + concentration up.
  4. New-entrant frequency: 0–1 per year = protected; 5+ = breached.

⑤ Mr. Market (Graham 1949, Ch. 8)

⑤ Mr. Market (Graham 1949)

Graham 笔下的"市场先生"是一个情绪化的合伙人, 每天来到你门口报一个价。你可以跟他交易, 也可以不交易; 他的报价是机会, 不是你公司价值的指引。Buffett 著名总结 (1987): "市场短期是投票机, 长期是称重机。"

Graham's "Mr. Market" is an emotional partner who shows up daily quoting a price. You may trade with him; you needn't. His quote is an opportunity, not a verdict on your firm's value. Buffett (1987): "In the short run the market is a voting machine, in the long run a weighing machine."

行为金融桥: Mr. Market 的情绪并非随机噪声, 而是系统性偏差 — Kahneman & Tversky 1979 prospect theory 表明, 投资者对损失的痛感 ≈ 同等收益快感的 2-2.5 倍; Shiller 1981 论文显示股价波动远超基本面波动 (excess volatility puzzle)。这两条机制保证了 Mr. Market 在恐慌时一定过度抛售, 在亢奋时一定过度追高 — 给价值投资者留下结构性机会。

Behavioral bridge: Mr. Market's mood isn't random noise — it's systematically biased. Kahneman & Tversky (1979) prospect theory: loss aversion ratio ~2–2.5×. Shiller (1981): excess volatility puzzle — prices swing far more than fundamentals. Together these mechanics guarantee panic over-selling and euphoria over-buying, leaving structural opportunity for value investors.

EMH 的张力: 半强式有效市场假说 (Fama 1970) 主张公开信息已反映在价格里, 价值投资似乎无效。但 Fama-French 1992 三因子模型自己也发现"小盘 + 价值 (低 P/B) 因子"的长期超额收益 — 即 value premium 客观存在 (1926-2020 美国, 约 4-5%/年)。学界一种和解: EMH 在个股短期大致成立, 但在因子层面长期存在系统性溢价。价值投资本质是采集这种溢价。

EMH tension: semi-strong EMH (Fama 1970) suggests value investing shouldn't work. Yet Fama-French (1992) themselves found a long-run value premium (low P/B factor) ~4–5% per year, 1926–2020 US. The compromise reading: EMH holds for individual short-run pricing but factor-level long-run premia persist. Value investing is the harvesting of that premium.

价值投资的 first-principles 总结: 公司有内在价值, 价格围绕内在价值波动且偏差时常 ≥ 30%, 而价值投资者通过四个过滤器(能力圈、护城河、管理层、价格)等到偏差出现时下手。这个流程的代数形式是条件期望: $E[\text{return} \mid \text{4 filters all pass}] \gg E[\text{return} \mid \text{random pick}]$。Layer 3 把这个流程落到具体 A 股标的上。

First-principles summary: firms have intrinsic value, price wobbles around it with deviations often ≥ 30%, and value investors wait, via four filters, for the deviation to appear. Algebraically: $E[\text{return} \mid \text{4 filters pass}] \gg E[\text{return} \mid \text{random pick}]$. Layer 3 maps this onto specific A‑share names.

  1. Q1. 估算公司内在价值 100 元, MOS 要求 30%. 多少元以下买入? 70 至 100 之间的"中性区"是不是浪费机会?Q1. IV ¥100, require 30% MOS. Buy below? Is the ¥70–100 "neutral zone" a wasted opportunity?

    由 (7.3): $\mathrm{MOS} \geq 0.3 \Rightarrow P/IV \leq 0.7 \Rightarrow P \leq 70$. 即 70 元以下才买入。

    中性区不是浪费, 而是 MOS 设计的必然: 70-100 这 30% 是给"我估错了 IV"的缓冲。若我估的 IV 实际是 80 (而非 100), 那么 70 仍然便宜, 但 95 就贵了 — 中性区允许我犯 30% 以内的估值错误而不亏钱。Buffett 1996: "Margin of safety is what's left of risk after you've done all your homework."

    三种处理"中性区"的方式: (a) 完全不动, 严守 30% MOS — Graham 派; (b) 用小仓位 (10-20% 试探), MOS 大时加仓 — Klarman 派; (c) 高确定性公司 (茅台、长江电力) 把 MOS 阈值放宽到 15-20% — Buffett 派。三种都自洽, 但同一标的不应"混用"。

    By (7.3): buy below ¥70. The ¥70–100 zone isn't waste — it's the buffer for my own IV estimation error. If my "100" is actually 80, ¥70 still cheap, ¥95 already expensive. Three styles for the neutral zone: (a) strict 30% MOS (Graham); (b) toe-hold position, add on deeper MOS (Klarman); (c) relax MOS to 15–20% for highest-certainty firms (Buffett). All consistent; never mix on the same name.

  2. Q2. "P/E = 8 的银行 vs P/E = 80 的科技, 哪个估值便宜?" 用价值投资框架回答, 并说明为什么"低 P/E = 便宜"是一个常见错误。Q2. "P/E 8 bank vs P/E 80 tech — which is cheap?" Answer via value framework, explain why "low P/E = cheap" is a common error.

    不直接比较。"便宜"的定义是价格 < 内在价值, 不是低 P/E。两家公司若 IV 都用 (7.1) 推导, 它们对 P/E 的隐含约束完全不同:

    • 银行: ROE 12%, 资本约束严格, 增长 5%/年 → 合理 P/E 约 8-10 (匹配)。P/E 8 → MOS 接近 0。
    • 科技: 若 ROE 25%, 增长 25%/年, 现金流 5 年后可见 → 合理 P/E 可到 40-60。P/E 80 → 隐含未来 5 年增长 > 35%/年, 高度脆弱。

    用 P/E/g (PEG) 略好但仍不充分。正确做法: 各自做 OE DCF 估算 IV, 再比 MOS。

    Buffett 的实际答案: 看哪一个在你能力圈内。不在能力圈的, P/E 多低都不投 — 这就是"NO 比 YES 重要"。Buffett 1990s 错过 Microsoft 不是错误, 是纪律。2016 后买 Apple 是因为 Apple 此时已转型为消费品 + 服务型公司, 回到 Buffett 能力圈。

    Don't compare directly. "Cheap" = price < IV, not low P/E. Banks: ROE 12%, capital-constrained, 5% growth → fair P/E ~8–10 (matched). P/E 8 → MOS ≈ 0. Tech: ROE 25%, growth 25%, 5y horizon → fair P/E 40–60 possible. P/E 80 implies >35% growth for 5y — fragile. Use PEG as rough check; properly do separate IV via OE DCF, compare MOS.

    Buffett's real answer: which is in your circle? Outside it, low P/E doesn't matter. Missing Microsoft in the 1990s was discipline, not error. Buying Apple post-2016 worked because Apple by then had become a consumer + services franchise — back inside the circle.

  3. Q3. 一家 ROIC 长期 25% 的公司, 当前 MOS 仅 10% (即价格 ≈ IV × 0.9)。Graham 严苛派会说"不买", Buffett 派会说"买"。用复利代数说明 Buffett 派为何可能正确。Q3. A firm with sustained 25% ROIC has MOS only 10% (price ≈ IV × 0.9). Strict Graham → don't buy; Buffett → buy. Use compounding algebra to defend Buffett.

    假设 ROIC = 25%, payout = 40% (即 60% 留存再投资), 则 IV 复利增长率 ≈ ROIC × (1 − payout) = 25% × 60% = 15%/年。

    当前 MOS = 10% → 价格 = IV × 0.9。一年后, IV 增长到 1.15 × IV, 若价格保持不变, MOS = 1 − 0.9 / 1.15 ≈ 22%。两年后 MOS ≈ 32%。三年后 MOS ≈ 41%。

    即: 持有时间本身就是一种安全边际。Buffett 1989 致股东信原话: "Time is the friend of the wonderful business, the enemy of the mediocre." 高 ROIC 公司的 IV 增长足以补偿入场时的较小 MOS — 这就是 Buffett 派愿意"用合理价格买极好公司"的代数基础。

    但 Graham 派的反驳: 复利只在ROIC 持续 25%的前提下成立。若公司护城河衰退, ROIC 5 年后跌到 12%, 那么 IV 增速也跟着塌, 原本 10% 的 MOS 远不够缓冲。所以这个分歧的本质是: 你对护城河可持续性的信心有多高?

    实务: 对高 ROIC 公司 (茅台、海康、招行), Buffett 派松一档 (MOS 15-20%); 对 ROIC 平庸或下滑的公司 (大部分周期股、传统制造), 严守 30%+。

    ROIC 25%, payout 40% → IV growth ≈ ROIC × (1 − payout) = 15% p.a. Start MOS 10% (P = 0.9 IV). Year 1: IV grows to 1.15 IV, P constant → MOS = 1 − 0.9/1.15 ≈ 22%. Year 2 ≈ 32%, Year 3 ≈ 41%. Holding period itself is a margin of safety.

    Buffett 1989: "Time is the friend of the wonderful business, the enemy of the mediocre." High-ROIC IV growth absorbs the smaller entry MOS — algebraic basis for "fair price, great company."

    Graham counter: compounding assumes ROIC stays 25%. If moat erodes and ROIC falls to 12% in 5 years, the IV growth collapses and 10% MOS is too thin. So the disagreement reduces to: how confident are you in moat durability? In practice: relax to 15–20% for high-ROIC franchises; hold strict 30% for cyclicals and decliners.

  4. Q4. Greenblatt magic formula 在美国 1988-2004 测试年化超额 ~13%。同样的规则放到 A 股, 直接复制可能效果减半 — 至少列出三个"水土不服"的 A 股特殊点, 并提出修正方案。Q4. Greenblatt magic formula yields ~13% annual alpha in 1988–2004 US tests. Copied to A‑shares, alpha often halves. Name three A‑share specifics that break the rule and propose fixes.

    原始 Magic formula: 排序 ROC = EBIT/(净 WC + 净 PPE) + 收益率 EBIT/EV, 取前 30 名等权。在 A 股有以下扭曲点:

    1. 关联方占款扭曲 ROC: A 股大量公司有"其他应收"(关联方借走的钱) 挂在表内, EBIT 真实表现但 WC 被关联方占用 → ROC 失真。修正: 把"其他应收 − 应收账款"剔除出 WC, 或筛掉"其他应收 / 总资产 > 10%"的公司。
    2. 商誉减值波动 EBIT: 2018-2019 A 股商誉减值集中爆发 (Ch.05 案例: 天神娱乐), 单年 EBIT 大幅波动, Magic formula 选中"减值前的高 ROC, 减值年塌方"型陷阱。修正: 用 3-5 年 EBIT 平均代替单年; 或筛掉"商誉/净资产 > 30%"的公司。
    3. 停牌与流动性问题: A 股长期停牌、重大重组、限售解禁等导致等权再平衡的执行成本高。修正: 选取流通市值 > 50 亿的"可操作池", 排除 ST 与近期重大重组。
    4. 财务造假特殊样本: 康得新、康美等高 ROC 公司事后被证实造假, magic formula 在事前无法识别。修正: 叠加 Beneish M-score < −2.22 + Altman Z > 2.99 筛选 (Ch.08 详述), 排除高造假风险样本。
    5. 政策周期: 集采、双减、互联网监管等 A 股特有的政策事件能在一夜之间消灭 ROC。修正: 行业内做 magic formula (而非全市场), 避免被某一个被政策"抽屉化"的板块拖累。

    实务上, 经"质量过滤 + 行业内 + 流动性筛选"后的修正版 magic formula, A 股 2010-2020 测试年化超额约 5-8% — 仍正, 但显著低于美国, 反映 A 股相对低效但摩擦也大。

    Three (plus more) A‑share distortions: (1) related-party "other receivables" inflate ROC — exclude or filter ROR>10%; (2) goodwill cliffs jolt EBIT — use 3–5y avg or filter goodwill/equity >30%; (3) liquidity and suspensions — restrict to free-float >¥5bn, exclude ST and reorgs; (4) accounting fraud — overlay Beneish + Altman; (5) policy shocks — run within-industry, not market-wide. Adjusted A‑share alpha 2010–20 ~5–8%, half of US but still positive.

  5. Q5. 一家 P/B = 0.6 的银行, Graham number 显示"严重低估", 但你认为可能是价值陷阱。列出至少四个验证它是否价值陷阱的诊断步骤。Q5. A bank trades at P/B 0.6; Graham number says "deeply undervalued." You suspect a value trap. List four diagnostics to verify.

    "价值陷阱"定义: 表面便宜, 但内在价值正在持续下降, 价格其实没有偏离 IV — 只是 IV 自己在塌。诊断步骤:

    1. 不良率与拨备覆盖率趋势: 银行的"账面净资产" (B) 取决于拨备的充分性。若 NPL 上升 + 拨备覆盖率下降 + 关注类贷款占比上升 → "B" 本身被虚高, 真实 P/B 可能远高于 0.6。看 5 年趋势, 不看单年。
    2. 地方政府融资平台 (LGFV) 与房地产风险敞口: 2024 年 A 股大量银行 P/B < 1 的核心原因是"LGFV + 地产开发贷"两块隐藏不良。看附注分行业贷款 + 五级分类迁徙率, 估算潜在损失。若潜在损失 > 当前净资产 × 30%, "便宜"立刻消失。
    3. ROE 趋势: ROE 是否连续 5 年下降? 银行 ROE 从 18% 跌到 8% 不是"周期", 是"结构"。结构性下降的银行, IV 在持续下行, 当前 P/B 0.6 三年后可能仍是 0.6 (因为分母 B 自己在塌)。
    4. 资产负债表外: 表外理财、银证保通道、信托收益权 — 一旦表外"出表回不来", 立刻吞噬资本。看附注"或有事项"与"资本充足率敏感性"。
    5. 管理层与监管反馈: 是否有大规模管理层变动、监管处罚、被点名? 监管的关注是"账面 B 不真实"的最高频信号。
    6. 对比逻辑: 把它和同体量银行 (招行 600036, ROE ~16%, P/B ~1.2) 比 — 为什么差 50%? 若答案是"NPL 高、行业 / 区域风险敞口大", 那是合理折价, 不是机会。

    诊断目标: 区分市场过度悲观(机会) vs 市场理性反映 IV 下降(陷阱)。前者 P/B 反弹回 1.0+, 后者 P/B 三年后仍 0.6, 因为 B 自身在缩水。Buffett 多次拒绝"廉价银行"原因都在这里 — Wells Fargo 早期他敢买, 因可以亲自看到资产质量; 大部分中国地方城商行他不会碰。

    "Value trap" = price looks cheap because IV is silently falling, not because price wandered away from IV. Diagnostics: (1) NPL and provisioning coverage trend — book equity B depends on provisioning sufficiency; rising NPL + falling coverage + rising "special mention" loans → B is overstated; (2) LGFV and real-estate exposure — hidden NPL pockets; estimate latent loss / equity ratio; (3) ROE trend — 5-year decline from 18% to 8% is structural, not cyclical; IV deflates with it; (4) off-BS items — wealth products, trust beneficiary rights — once forced back on, capital evaporates; (5) management changes and regulator attention; (6) peer-relative — vs CMB ROE 16% P/B 1.2: why 50% gap? Goal: distinguish over-pessimism (opportunity) from rational discounting of falling IV (trap). Buffett historically passed on cheap regional banks for exactly this reason — he could verify Wells Fargo's loan book personally; he can't verify a Chinese city bank's.
