现代组合理论 MPTModern Portfolio Theory

Markowitz (1952): 投资者关心组合 σ 而非单一资产 σ。通过组合构建, 在相同期望收益下可以显著降低风险。"不把鸡蛋放一个篮子"的数学化, 也是诺贝尔奖背后那条最简洁的公式。Markowitz (1952): investors care about portfolio σ. Combining assets with imperfect correlation lowers risk at given return. The math behind "don't put all eggs in one basket," and the cleanest equation behind a Nobel.

学习目标Objectives
  • 写出两资产 / N 资产组合的均值与方差公式
  • 用拉格朗日推导最小方差组合 (minimum variance portfolio)
  • 理解有效前沿 / 切线组合 / Sharpe 比率三者的几何关系
  • 能口头解释为什么 ρ < 1 就能"免费降风险", 以及为什么这个免费午餐在危机中失效
  • Two‑asset and N‑asset variance; minimum variance portfolio via Lagrangian; efficient frontier, tangent portfolio, Sharpe; why ρ < 1 gives a "free lunch" and why crises eat it.

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · 老王想分散 10 万元, 一步步发现"协方差才是关键" · Old Wang splits ¥100k and discovers covariance is the lever

M1 和 M2 结束后, 老王把店里的事交给伙计打理, 自己手上攒了 10 万元闲钱, 准备走进 A 股市场。他在证券公司开了户, 但马上撞上第一个问题: 钱怎么分? 这一节我们陪老王从"一只股票"走到"五只股票 + 一个国债", 每一步都问一个问题, 让"组合的风险"这件事从直觉里长出来。

After M1 and M2, Old Wang hands the noodle shop to his cook and takes ¥100,000 of free capital into A‑shares. He opens a brokerage account and immediately hits the first question: how to split it? We walk with him from one stock to five stocks plus a bond ETF, asking one question at each step so portfolio risk grows out of intuition.

1. 全押一只 — 茅台 600519, 价格 ¥1,500, 老王买 60 股, 用掉 ¥90,000, 留 ¥10,000 现金。 过去三年, 茅台月度涨跌大致 ±5% 到 ±10%, 年化波动率 σ ≈ 26%。老王第一个月就赶上一根 −12% 的 K 线, 一晚上账户少了 ¥10,800。他问伙计: "我是不是该买点别的, 分散一下?" 伙计反问: "你说说看, 多买一只就一定降风险吗?" 老王愣了 — 他直觉觉得"加一只", 风险就少了一半, 但说不出为什么。

1. All in one — Moutai 600519 at ¥1,500, 60 shares = ¥90,000, ¥10,000 in cash. Over the past three years, Moutai's monthly returns swing roughly ±5%–10%, annualised σ ≈ 26%. The first month delivers a −12% candle, and ¥10,800 evaporates overnight. Wang asks the cook: "Should I buy something else to diversify?" Cook fires back: "But why would adding one cut the risk?" Wang's gut says "halve it" — but he can't justify it.

2. 加一只同行业的 — 五粮液 000858, 卖 30 股茅台买 30 股五粮液, 等权两只白酒。 但是接下来三个月发生的事让老王傻眼: 两只酒一起涨一起跌。当国家发了限酒令, 茅台跌 10%, 五粮液跌 9%; 当中秋促销, 两只一起涨 6%。老王问伙计: "我现在不是有两只了吗, 怎么没分散?" 伙计答: "因为它们同涨同跌。" 同涨同跌这件事, 数学上叫 正相关 (positive correlation) — 用一个数 ρ 衡量, ρ 介于 −1 到 +1, 茅台和五粮液大约 ρ ≈ 0.75。如果两只资产几乎完全同涨同跌, 组合在一起跟一只没区别。这是老王走出的第一步: 想分散, 不能只看"有几只", 要看"它们之间什么关系"。

2. Add a same‑industry name — Wuliangye 000858. Sell 30 Moutai shares, buy 30 Wuliangye. Equal‑weight two liquors. The next three months floor him: they move together. Distribution restriction hits — Moutai −10%, Wuliangye −9%. Mid‑Autumn promo — both up 6%. Wang asks: "I have two now, why no diversification?" Cook: "They co‑move." Co‑movement has a name: positive correlation, ρ ∈ [−1, +1]. Moutai‑Wuliangye ρ ≈ 0.75. Near‑perfectly correlated assets are basically one asset. First lesson: diversification isn't about count, it's about relationship.

3. 换成不同行业的 — 卖掉五粮液, 买招商银行 600036 (银行) 和 比亚迪 002594 (新能源车)。 老王现在持有: 茅台 (白酒) ¥30,000 + 招行 (银行) ¥30,000 + 比亚迪 (新能源车) ¥30,000 + 现金 ¥10,000。这三家公司利润来源完全不同: 茅台靠经销商打款, 招行靠存贷利差, 比亚迪靠电车销量。它们的 ρ 互相大约: 茅台-招行 ≈ 0.35, 茅台-比亚迪 ≈ 0.30, 招行-比亚迪 ≈ 0.45。下一个月发生的事很关键: 当国家发布"消费税从严"传闻, 茅台跌 7%, 而招行和比亚迪几乎没动。组合整体只跌了 2.3%。老王第一次体感到了分散化: 跌的时候, 别人在帮我抵消一些。等式还成立吗? 组合波动率 σ_p ≈ 16-18%, 而单只 σ 都在 25%+ — 风险被实实在在压低了 ~30%。

3. Switch to different industries — drop Wuliangye, buy CMB 600036 (bank) and BYD 002594 (EV). Wang now holds: Moutai ¥30k + CMB ¥30k + BYD ¥30k + cash ¥10k. Three completely different profit engines — distributor remittances, deposit‑lending spread, EV unit sales. Pairwise ρ roughly 0.30–0.45. Next month a "stricter consumption tax" rumour hits: Moutai −7%, CMB and BYD barely move. The portfolio drops just 2.3%. Wang feels diversification for the first time. σ_p ≈ 16–18%, well below the ~25%+ of each single stock — risk cut by ~30%.

4. 加一只跟股市基本无关的 — 国债 ETF 511010, 用现金 ¥10,000 全买进。 国债 ETF 跟踪 5 年期国债, 年化波动率 σ ≈ 4-5%, 关键是它和股票的相关系数 ρ ≈ 0 甚至略负 (股市跌 → 资金避险买债 → 债涨)。老王把现金换成国债 ETF, 组合变成: 茅台 30% + 招行 30% + 比亚迪 30% + 国债 ETF 10%。这一步看似不大 (只换了 10% 现金), 但组合波动率再降了 2-3 个百分点。这就是 "低相关资产是免费午餐" 的真正体感。一旦市场出现剧烈下跌 (比如 2020 年 3 月全球流动性冲击), 这 10% 国债的贡献会更明显。

4. Add an asset basically uncorrelated with stocks — bond ETF 511010, ¥10,000 of cash all in. 511010 tracks 5y T‑bonds, σ ≈ 4–5%; crucially ρ with equities ≈ 0, sometimes slightly negative (stock crash → flight to bonds → bond up). Wang's portfolio becomes Moutai 30% + CMB 30% + BYD 30% + bond ETF 10%. Looks like a small swap, but σ_p drops another 2–3 pp. The actual taste of "low‑correlation = free lunch." During a sharp crash (think March 2020), that 10% bonds matters even more.

5. 改权重 — 老王问: 我能不能多放点国债, 比如 40%? 风险会怎么变? 伙计在纸上画了一条曲线: 横轴是国债 ETF 占比 (0% 到 100%), 纵轴是组合波动率 σ_p。当国债占比从 0 升到 30-40% 左右, σ_p 一直在下降 (因为债股低相关, 加债等于"借便宜的负相关"); 但继续加债到 60%+, σ_p 反而开始上升 (因为这时组合主要是国债自己的波动了, 边际效益递减)。这条 U 型曲线的最低点叫 最小方差组合 (minimum variance portfolio)。Layer 2 会用拉格朗日给出闭式解。

5. Reweight — Wang asks: what if I put 40% in bonds? What happens to risk? Cook sketches a curve: x is bond weight (0–100%), y is portfolio σ_p. As bond weight rises from 0 to ~30–40%, σ_p falls (cheap negative‑ish correlation buys risk reduction). Beyond ~60% bond, σ_p starts rising again (bond's own σ dominates). The U‑curve's bottom is the minimum variance portfolio. Layer 2 gives its closed‑form Lagrangian solution.

6. 进一步问: 在所有可能的组合中, 哪个 "每承担一份风险换最多回报"? 老王注意到一件事: 沿着 U 型曲线右上方 (国债占比逐渐降低, 股权占比升高), 期望回报在上升, 但波动率也在上升。如果他给定一个无风险利率 $r_f$ ≈ 2.5% (假设一年定期), 那么"每多一份 σ 换多少 μ"就是衡量"性价比"的关键。在所有组合里, 有一个特别的组合能让 $(\mu_p - r_f)/\sigma_p$ 达到最大 — 这就是 切线组合 (tangent portfolio)。从 $r_f$ 这一点画一条直线, 与有效前沿相切, 切点就是它。Layer 2 给出 $\mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$ 的矩阵公式。一旦找到这个切线组合, 老王只需要决定分多少比例给切线组合, 多少给无风险资产 — 不需要再去想"具体哪几只股票" — 这就是 Tobin 1958 的两基金分离定理 (two‑fund separation): 所有理性投资者的最优组合都是 "无风险资产 + 同一个切线组合" 的某种比例混合。

6. Push it: which portfolio gives the most return per unit of risk? Along the U‑curve's right‑upper arc, expected return rises with σ. Given a risk‑free rate $r_f$ ≈ 2.5%, the question becomes "how much μ per unit σ?" One special portfolio maximises $(\mu_p - r_f)/\sigma_p$ — the tangent portfolio. Drawing a line from $r_f$ tangent to the efficient frontier, the touch point is it. Layer 2 gives the closed‑form weight vector. Once found, Wang only chooses how much to allocate between tangent portfolio and risk‑free — never "which 5 stocks." That is Tobin's 1958 two‑fund separation theorem.

7. 老王画完那条曲线, 长舒一口气。 五步走过来, 他从"全押茅台"变成"4 只资产 + 国债 ETF, 每只权重经过计算"。重要的不是具体那几只股票, 而是: 风险不是各资产风险的简单加权, 而是受相关性 ρ 强烈影响; 加入低相关资产能"免费"降风险; 把切线组合 + 无风险资产线性组合, 就能拿到任意位置的风险/回报点。这就是 Markowitz 1952 在六页论文里说清楚的事。Layer 2 把它形式化, Layer 3 用真实的 沪深 300 ETF / 中证 500 ETF / 国债 ETF 三资产 ρ-Σ 数据跑一遍。

7. Wang closes the notebook with a long exhale. Five steps in, he's gone from "all‑in Moutai" to "4 assets + bond ETF, each weight computed." The lesson isn't the tickers — it's: risk is not a weighted sum but a quadratic form in ρ; low‑correlation assets give free risk reduction; combining tangent portfolio with $r_f$ reaches every point on the capital allocation line. That's what Markowitz 1952 said in six pages. Layer 2 formalises it; Layer 3 runs the real CSI 300 / CSI 500 / bond‑ETF Σ.

回看老王的五步: 单押 → 加同行业 → 加不同行业 → 加低相关资产 → 调权重找最优。这条路线就是 Markowitz 给所有投资者的"五步法"。下一节我们把这套语言形式化, 然后用真实的 沪深 300 ETF / 中证 500 ETF / 国债 ETF 数据跑一次完整优化。

Recap: single name → same‑industry add → different‑industry add → low‑correlation add → reweight to optimum. Markowitz's five‑step walk for any investor. Layer 2 formalises it; Layer 3 runs a real CSI 300 / CSI 500 / bond‑ETF optimisation.

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

Layer 1 用老王从单押茅台到 4 资产组合的五步走, 把"分散化的核心是协方差"这件事讲清楚了。现在我们把同一套规则用矩阵语言形式化, 并补一个工程类比 — 组合波动率是一个二次型, 数学上和"机械系统的转动惯量"是同一个数学对象。

Layer 1 used Wang's five steps to motivate "covariance is the lever." We now formalise it in matrix form and add one engineering bridge: portfolio variance is a quadratic form, mathematically identical to a rotational moment of inertia tensor.

① 组合的均值与方差

① Portfolio Mean and Variance

设 N 个资产, 权重向量 $\mathbf{w} = (w_1, ..., w_N)^\top$ 满足 $\mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} = 1$ (权重和为 1), 期望收益向量 $\boldsymbol{\mu}$, 协方差矩阵 $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}$ (N×N, 对称半正定)。则:

N assets, weights $\mathbf{w}$ with $\mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} = 1$, expected return vector $\boldsymbol{\mu}$, covariance matrix $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}$ (N×N symmetric PSD). Then:

组合的期望与方差 Portfolio mean and variance
$$ \mu_p \;=\; \mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\mu}, \quad \sigma_p^2 \;=\; \mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w} \;=\; \sum_{i,j} w_i w_j \sigma_{ij} $$(1.1)

展开看清楚: $\sigma_p^2 = \sum_i w_i^2 \sigma_i^2 + 2 \sum_{i < j} w_i w_j \rho_{ij} \sigma_i \sigma_j$。第一项是"各资产自身方差", 第二项是"协方差贡献"。当 $\rho_{ij} < 1$, 协方差项小于"完全相关情况", 这就是分散化的数学源头。

Expanded: $\sigma_p^2 = \sum_i w_i^2 \sigma_i^2 + 2 \sum_{i < j} w_i w_j \rho_{ij} \sigma_i \sigma_j$. First term: own variances. Second: covariance contribution. With $\rho_{ij} < 1$, the cross term is below the perfectly‑correlated case — that's where diversification mathematically originates.

Markowitz (1952), "Portfolio Selection," Journal of Finance 7(1): 77–91 · Brealey, Myers & Allen, Principles of Corporate Finance, Ch. 8

两资产特例 (老王 Layer 1 中"茅台 + 五粮液" vs "茅台 + 招行"):

Two‑asset case (Wang's "Moutai + Wuliangye" vs "Moutai + CMB"):

$$ \sigma_p^2 \;=\; w_1^2 \sigma_1^2 + w_2^2 \sigma_2^2 + 2 w_1 w_2 \rho_{12} \sigma_1 \sigma_2 $$(1.2)

数字验证 Layer 1 第 2 步与第 3 步:

Numerical check of Layer 1 steps 2 vs 3:

  • 茅台 + 五粮液 (等权), σ₁ = σ₂ = 26%, ρ = 0.75: $\sigma_p^2 = 0.25 \cdot 0.0676 \cdot 2 + 2 \cdot 0.25 \cdot 0.75 \cdot 0.0676 \approx 0.0592$, $\sigma_p \approx 24.3\%$ — 几乎没降。Moutai + Wuliangye equal‑weight, σ = 26% each, ρ = 0.75: σ_p ≈ 24.3% — barely down.
  • 茅台 + 招行 (等权), σ₁ = 26%, σ₂ = 25%, ρ = 0.35: $\sigma_p \approx 21.0\%$ — 降了约 5 个百分点。Moutai + CMB equal‑weight, σ = 26%/25%, ρ = 0.35: σ_p ≈ 21.0% — ~5 pp lower.

工程类比: 组合方差 $\mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w}$ 是二次型, 与机械系统转动惯量 $\mathbf{r}^\top \mathbf{I} \mathbf{r}$ 同一种数学对象。$\boldsymbol{\Sigma}$ 的特征向量给出主轴 (principal axes), 对应的特征值是该主轴上的"独立风险源"。这就是主成分分析 PCA 在金融里的版本 — 你可以把整个 A 股市场分解成几个"独立风险因子" (M3 Ch.02 因子模型的几何起点)。

Engineering analogy: portfolio variance $\mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w}$ is a quadratic form — same mathematical object as moment of inertia $\mathbf{r}^\top \mathbf{I} \mathbf{r}$. Eigenvectors of $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}$ are principal axes (independent risk sources); their eigenvalues are variances along those axes — PCA of the market (geometric starting point of Ch.02 factor models).

② 最小方差组合 — 拉格朗日推导

② Minimum Variance Portfolio — Lagrangian Derivation

最小方差组合解决: 给定权重和为 1 约束, 哪个 $\mathbf{w}$ 让 $\sigma_p^2$ 最小? 这是一个等式约束的凸优化问题。构造拉格朗日量:

Minimum variance: given $\mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} = 1$, which $\mathbf{w}$ minimises $\sigma_p^2$? Equality‑constrained convex QP. Lagrangian:

$$ \mathcal{L}(\mathbf{w}, \lambda) \;=\; \mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w} \;-\; \lambda \left( \mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} - 1 \right) $$(1.3)

对 $\mathbf{w}$ 求一阶条件 $\partial \mathcal{L} / \partial \mathbf{w} = 2 \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w} - \lambda \mathbf{1} = 0$, 解出 $\mathbf{w} = (\lambda/2) \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}$。代入约束 $\mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} = 1$ 求 $\lambda$, 最终:

FOC $\partial \mathcal{L} / \partial \mathbf{w} = 2 \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w} - \lambda \mathbf{1} = 0$, so $\mathbf{w} = (\lambda/2) \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}$. Pin $\lambda$ via $\mathbf{1}^\top \mathbf{w} = 1$:

最小方差组合 Minimum variance portfolio
$$ \mathbf{w}_{\min} \;=\; \frac{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}}{\mathbf{1}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}} $$(1.4)

直观: 协方差矩阵的逆"乘上 1 向量"得到一个"原始风险权重", 再归一化。$\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}$ 把高方差 / 高相关资产的权重压低, 让低方差 / 低相关资产更突出。这就是为什么 Layer 1 第 4 步引入国债 ETF 后, 最小方差点出现在"国债占 30-40%"附近 — 它的低 σ + 低 ρ 让 $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}$ 自动给它高权重。

Intuition: $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}$ produces "raw risk weights"; normalisation finishes. $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}$ downweights high‑σ / high‑ρ assets and amplifies low‑σ / low‑ρ ones. That's why Layer 1 step 4's bond ETF gets ~30–40% — its low σ & low ρ are exactly what $\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}$ rewards.

Markowitz (1952) · Merton (1972) "An Analytic Derivation of the Efficient Portfolio Frontier"

③ 切线组合与 Sharpe 比率

③ Tangent Portfolio and Sharpe Ratio

引入无风险资产 (国债 / 银行存款) 后, 投资者可以借入或借出 $r_f$ 利率的资金。新的优化问题: 在所有风险资产组合中, 哪一个的夏普比率最大?

Introducing risk‑free borrowing/lending at $r_f$: which risky portfolio maximises Sharpe ratio?

$$ \mathrm{Sharpe}(\mathbf{w}) \;=\; \frac{\mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f}{\sqrt{\mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w}}} $$(1.5)

用同样的拉格朗日法 (这次约束是 $\mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f$ = 常数, 最小化 $\sigma_p$), 解出:

Same Lagrangian (now constrain numerator, minimise σ_p):

切线组合 (最大 Sharpe) Tangent portfolio (max Sharpe)
$$ \mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}} \;=\; \frac{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} (\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1})}{\mathbf{1}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} (\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1})} $$(1.6)

和 (1.4) 比较: 把 $\mathbf{1}$ 替换成 $(\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1})$ 就得到切线组合。前者只关心"降风险", 后者同时关心"换回报"。$\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1}$ 是 超额预期收益向量 (excess expected returns) — Layer 1 第 6 步老王问的"每多一份 σ 换多少 μ", 就由这个向量回答。

Compare to (1.4): replace $\mathbf{1}$ with $\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1}$. (1.4) only minimises risk; (1.6) also targets return. $\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1}$ is the excess‑expected‑return vector — answers Wang's "how much μ per unit σ?"

Sharpe (1964) "Capital Asset Prices" · Tobin (1958) "Liquidity Preference as Behavior Towards Risk" — two‑fund separation

④ 有效前沿与两基金分离

④ Efficient Frontier & Two‑Fund Separation

对每一个目标期望收益 $\mu$, 解出 $\sigma_{\min}(\mu)$, 在 $(\sigma, \mu)$ 平面上画出 有效前沿 (efficient frontier) — 一条凸曲线。引入 $r_f$ 后, 通过 $r_f$ 点向有效前沿画切线 (CAL, capital allocation line), 切点就是切线组合 $\mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$。所有"无风险资产 + 切线组合"的混合都落在这条 CAL 上, 且 Sharpe 与切线组合相同。

For each target μ, solve $\sigma_{\min}(\mu)$ and plot the efficient frontier in $(\sigma, \mu)$ space. With $r_f$, the line from $(0, r_f)$ tangent to the frontier touches at $\mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$. All combinations of risk‑free + tangent lie on this capital allocation line with the same Sharpe.

两基金分离定理 (Tobin 1958) Two‑fund separation (Tobin 1958)

在均值-方差最优框架下, 所有投资者的最优组合都可写成: $\mathbf{w}^* = \alpha \cdot \mathbf{0} + (1-\alpha) \cdot \mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$。$\alpha$ 由投资者风险偏好决定 ($\alpha \to 1$ 全无风险, $\alpha \to 0$ 全切线组合, $\alpha < 0$ 加杠杆), 但具体哪些股票 / 多少比例完全由 $\mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$ 决定, 与投资者偏好无关。

In MV optimisation, every investor's optimum is $\mathbf{w}^* = \alpha \mathbf{0} + (1-\alpha) \mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$. α reflects risk preference; which risky assets and weights live in $\mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}}$ alone, independent of preferences.

Tobin (1958), Review of Economic Studies 25(2): 65–86

这条定理的实践含义巨大: 所有理性投资者应该持有同一个风险资产组合 (切线组合), 只是按风险偏好调节它和无风险资产的比例。这是 1960s 后期共同基金 / 指数基金 / ETF 大规模兴起的理论基础 — Bogle 在 Vanguard 推沪深 300 类宽基指数的逻辑根。

Big practical takeaway: every rational investor should hold the same risky portfolio (the tangent), tilting only between it and the risk‑free. Theoretical basis for the rise of mutual funds / index funds / ETFs from the late 1960s — the logic behind Bogle's broad‑market index push.

实务警告 — Layer 2 没有讲的三件事:

Practical caveats — three things Layer 2 didn't tell you:

  1. μ 估计噪声极大: 用 3 年月度数据估 μ, 标准误约 σ/√36 ≈ 4-5%, 几乎和 μ 本身一个量级。所以 Markowitz 优化结果对 μ 极其敏感。Black-Litterman (1992) 用 "先验 μ + 投资者观点" 缓解这个问题。
  2. Σ 在危机时突变: 2008 全球金融危机、2015 A 股股灾、2020 新冠冲击, 期间股票之间 ρ 全部跳到 0.9+ — 分散化在最需要它的时候失效。所以专业机构会叠加 "tail hedge" (例如 VIX 期权 / 长债 / 黄金)。
  3. A 股卖空限制: 公式 (1.6) 解出来若有负权重, 在 A 股散户层面无法执行 (没有融券渠道 / 融资门槛高)。约束下的最优解 (no short, $w_i \ge 0$) 需要数值二次规划求解 (Python CVXPY 或 Excel Solver)。

(1) μ estimates are extremely noisy — Black‑Litterman softens this with priors + views. (2) Σ regime‑shifts in crises (2008, 2015, 2020 — pairwise ρ → 0.9+); diversification fails when it's most needed; pros overlay tail hedges (VIX, long bonds, gold). (3) A‑share retail can't short → impose $w_i \ge 0$ constraint, solve numerically.

  2. Q2. 在 ρ = −1 (完全负相关) 时, 两资产组合能否达到 σ = 0? 给出权重和数学论证。Q2. At ρ = −1, can two‑asset σ_p reach 0? Give weights and proof.

  3. Q3. Markowitz 优化输出的切线组合在 2008 / 2015 / 2020 三次危机中均显著回撤。这是否意味着 MPT 是个"晴天理论"?Q3. Markowitz tangent drew down sharply in 2008 / 2015 / 2020. Is MPT a "fair‑weather theory"?
