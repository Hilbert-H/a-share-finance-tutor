现代组合理论 MPTModern Portfolio Theory

Markowitz (1952): 投资者关心组合 σ 而非单一资产 σ. 通过组合构建, 在相同期望收益下可以显著降低风险. "不把鸡蛋放一个篮子"的数学化。Markowitz (1952): investors care about portfolio σ. Combining assets with imperfect correlation lowers risk at given return. The math of "don't put all eggs in one basket."

Objectives
  • 写出两资产 / N 资产组合的方差公式
  • 用拉格朗日推导 minimum variance portfolio
  • 理解有效前沿 / 切线组合 / Sharpe ratio
  • Two‑asset and N‑asset variance; min‑variance portfolio; efficient frontier; tangent portfolio; Sharpe

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition

单看一只股票, 你只看到它的"波动"$\sigma$. Markowitz 革命: 组合中, 单只股票的相关性与其他股票决定了它对组合的贡献。两只波动率 30% 但相关系数 $\rho = 0$ 的股票, 等权组合 σ = $30/\sqrt{2} \approx 21\%$ — 风险降 30%, 期望收益不变。"免费午餐"。

Within a portfolio, an asset's correlation, not its standalone σ, determines its risk contribution. Two 30%‑σ assets with ρ=0, equal weight → portfolio σ ≈ 21% — 30% lower risk, same expected return. Free lunch.

Layer 2 · 公式Formulas

组合期望与方差:

$$ \mu_p = \mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\mu}, \quad \sigma_p^2 = \mathbf{w}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma} \mathbf{w} $$(1.1)

最小方差组合 (无约束, 无短卖限制):

$$ \mathbf{w}_{\min} = \frac{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}}{\mathbf{1}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} \mathbf{1}} $$(1.2)

切线组合 (最大 Sharpe, 引入无风险利率 $r_f$):

$$ \mathbf{w}_{\text{tan}} = \frac{\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} (\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1})}{\mathbf{1}^\top \boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1} (\boldsymbol{\mu} - r_f \mathbf{1})} $$(1.3)

Sharpe Ratio:

$$ \mathrm{Sharpe} = \frac{\mu_p - r_f}{\sigma_p} $$(1.4)

有效前沿: 给定每个期望收益 $\mu$, 找到对应最小 $\sigma$. 集合构成一条凸曲线。切线组合 + $r_f$ 直线 (Capital Allocation Line, CAL) 主导所有风险资产组合 (两基金分离).

Efficient frontier: for each $\mu$ find min $\sigma$. With $r_f$, the tangent portfolio + risk‑free line (CAL) dominates all pure‑risky portfolios (two‑fund separation).

理解检验题Trap Questions

  1. Q1. 为什么"60/40 股债组合"长期 Sharpe 高于 100% 股票?Q1. Why does 60/40 stock‑bond beat 100% stocks on Sharpe long‑run?

    股债低相关 (有时负相关). 60/40 组合 σ ≈ 0.6 × 18% ≈ 11% (近似), 但期望收益 ≈ 0.6 × 8% + 0.4 × 3.5% ≈ 6.2%. Sharpe = (6.2 − 2.5) / 11 ≈ 0.34, 高于 100% 股 (8 − 2.5)/18 ≈ 0.31. 危机时国债避险, 进一步降组合 σ. 这是 Bogle / 桥水"全天候组合"的数学根。

    Stocks‑bonds have low (sometimes negative) correlation. 60/40 σ ≈ 11% with return ≈ 6.2%; Sharpe ~0.34 vs 100% stocks ~0.31. Bonds hedge in crises. Math foundation of Bogle 60/40 and Bridgewater All Weather.

  2. Q2. 在 ρ = −1 时, 两资产组合能否达到 σ = 0?Q2. At ρ=−1, can two assets achieve σ=0?

    能. ρ=−1 时, $\sigma_p^2 = w_1^2 \sigma_1^2 + w_2^2 \sigma_2^2 - 2 w_1 w_2 \sigma_1 \sigma_2 = (w_1 \sigma_1 - w_2 \sigma_2)^2$. 令 $w_1 \sigma_1 = w_2 \sigma_2$ → σ_p = 0. 即按反比配权重. 现实意义: 完美对冲 (期权 + 标的) 的基础. 大部分实际资产 ρ > 0, 但接近这个极限的对冲是衍生品定价的核心。

    Yes. With ρ=−1, σ²_p = (w_1 σ_1 − w_2 σ_2)². Setting weights inversely proportional to σ gives σ_p=0 — perfect hedge. Most real assets ρ>0; near‑hedge is the basis of derivative pricing.

  3. Q3. MPT 在 A 股市场实务中的主要局限?Q3. MPT's main limits in A‑share?
    • $\mu$ 估计极不稳定 — 历史均值噪声大, 1 年误差远超均值本身
    • $\Sigma$ 在危机时突变 — 相关系数趋向 1, 分散化失效 (2008, 2020 等)
    • 政策冲击突变 (双减、教培、地产), MPT 静态分布假设失效
    • 卖空限制 → 实际不能持有 (1.3) 解出的负权重
    • A 股个股 ρ 普遍 0.3-0.6, 行业内 ρ 接近 0.8 — 分散化空间不如美国 (0.2-0.4 平均 ρ)

    μ estimates noisy; Σ regime‑shifts in crises (correlations → 1); A‑share policy shocks; short‑sale constraints; higher intra‑industry correlations than US. MPT remains useful as a baseline but requires robust estimation (Black‑Litterman, shrinkage covariance).
