会计恒等式与复式记账 The Accounting Identity & Double-Entry

所有会计学结论都从 A = L + E 这一条恒等式机械推出。借贷规则不是约定俗成的, 而是这条恒等式的代数对偶。 All accounting follows mechanically from one identity, A = L + E. Debit and credit aren't conventions — they are the algebraic dual of that identity.

学习目标 Objectives
  • 能用 $A = L + E$ 验证任意一份 A 股资产负债表的内在一致性 (实战通常通过 "资产总计 = 负债合计 + 所有者权益合计" 这一行)。
  • 能把任意一笔经济事件 (买设备、借款、赊销、计提折旧、回购股票) 表达为符合恒等式的借贷分录。
  • 理解为何借贷符号不能在不影响恒等式的前提下任意改变, 它们是 $dA = dL + dE$ 的两侧标记。
  • 理解为什么"扣应付账款 → 增短期借款"是结构变化, 不是改善。
  • Use $A = L + E$ to consistency‑check any A‑share balance sheet (in practice via the three "totals" rows).
  • Encode any transaction (purchase, borrow, credit sale, depreciation, buyback) as a balanced double‑entry.
  • See why debit/credit signs are determined by the identity, not chosen by convention.
  • Recognize that swapping payables for short‑term debt is a structural change, not an improvement.

Layer 1 · 直觉Intuition · Building the identity from zero · 从零搭建这条恒等式

把一家公司当作一个封闭系统。任何价值进入这个系统, 必有来源; 任何价值留在系统里, 必有形态。来源和形态的总和恒相等 — 这就是会计恒等式。和能量守恒、动量守恒同一种性质: 把"价值"作为守恒量。

Treat a company as a closed system. Every dollar inside must have both a source (who has a claim on it) and a form (what it currently is). Sources sum to forms — that is the accounting identity. It's a conservation law, just with "value" as the conserved quantity instead of energy or momentum.

想象时间从 $t_0$ 开始:

Imagine time starting at $t_0$:

  1. 公司还不存在: $A = L = E = 0$。
  2. 创始人投入 100 万元自有资金: 银行账户上多出 100, 由创始人 (= 股东) 享有 → $A = 100$, $E = 100$, $L = 0$。等式仍成立。
  3. 银行给公司借款 200 万元: 银行账户再 +200, 但这部分钱由银行享有索取权 → $A = 300$, $L = 200$, $E = 100$。
  4. 花 50 万买设备: 现金变设备, 形态变了但总价值没变 → $A$ 内部重排, 总和仍为 300。
  5. 不管后续做什么, 这条恒等式必须始终成立。如果不成立, 一定有错。
  6. There's no company yet: $A = L = E = 0$.
  7. Founder injects ¥1m of personal cash: bank balance +¥1m, claim held by the founder (= shareholder) → $A = 1$m, $E = 1$m, $L = 0$.
  8. Bank lends ¥2m: bank balance +¥2m more, but this part is claimed by the lender → $A = 3$m, $L = 2$m, $E = 1$m.
  9. Spend ¥500k on equipment: cash converts to equipment — the form of value changes but the total doesn't → $A$ rearranges internally; total stays ¥3m.
  10. No matter what happens later, the identity must hold. If it doesn't, something is wrong.

这个恒等式从未被"证明"。它是定义 — 会计学定义"所有者权益 = 资产 − 负债"。剩下所有规则都是为了让这条等式在每一笔交易后都保持成立。借贷规则本身就是为此而设计的代数语言。

The identity is not derived. It is definitional — equity is defined as assets minus liabilities. Every other rule exists so that the identity stays true after every transaction. Debit/credit is just the algebraic language built around it.

电路类比: 资产端是节点上的电荷分布, 负债 + 权益端是流入节点的电流方向标签 (谁送来的)。基尔霍夫电流定律 (KCL) 说: 流入 = 流出。恒等式说: 形态 = 索取权。

Circuit analogy: the asset side is the charge distribution at a node; the L + E side labels who supplied it. KCL says inflow = outflow; the identity says form = claim.

Layer 2 · 定义与公式Definitions & Formulas

恒等式 Identity
$$ A \;=\; L \;+\; E $$ (1.1)

资产 (Assets) = 负债 (Liabilities) + 所有者权益 (Equity)。等价写法 $E = A - L$ 即"净资产"。

Assets = Liabilities + Equity. Equivalently $E = A - L$ defines "net assets" / book equity.

CAS 基本准则 §16 · IFRS Conceptual Framework §4.3–4.7 · Brealey, Myers & Allen, Principles of Corporate Finance, Ch. 4

把权益展开到下一期 — 这就是利润表如何嵌入恒等式:

Expand equity into period $t$ — this is how the income statement plugs into the identity:

权益的时间演化 Equity dynamics
$$ E_t \;=\; E_{t-1} \;+\; \mathrm{NI}_t \;-\; D_t $$ (1.2)

期末权益 = 期初权益 + 当期净利润 (Net Income) − 当期股利 (Dividends)。

Ending equity = beginning equity + net income − dividends paid.

代入 (1.1) 得到完整状态方程:

Substituting into (1.1):

$$ A_t \;=\; L_t \;+\; E_{t-1} \;+\; (\mathrm{Rev}_t - \mathrm{Exp}_t) \;-\; D_t $$ (1.3)

这就是把资产负债表 (state vector) 和利润表 (state transition) 串起来的桥梁。Ch.03 会再加上现金流量表, 把整个系统闭环。

This is the bridge between balance sheet (state vector) and income statement (state transition). Ch.03 adds the cash flow statement and closes the loop.

借贷规则不是约定, 是 $dA = dL + dE$ 的代数对偶

Debit/credit isn't a convention — it's the algebraic dual of $dA = dL + dE$

对 (1.1) 取微分 / 取增量:

Take the differential of (1.1):

$$ dA \;=\; dL \;+\; dE $$ (1.4)

现在选择记号约定: "借 (debit, Dr)" 标记资产增加方向 (左侧增加), "贷 (credit, Cr)" 标记右侧增加方向 (负债或权益增加)。把所有项分配后, 任何分录必有:

Now choose a convention: "debit (Dr)" marks the asset‑increase direction (the left‑hand side), "credit (Cr)" marks the right‑hand‑side direction (liability or equity increases). Every entry then satisfies:

$$ \sum \mathrm{Dr} \;=\; \sum \mathrm{Cr} $$ (1.5)

For each account type:

理解检验题Trap Questions

先自己想清楚再展开答案。题目不长, 但每一题都设了至少一个反直觉点。 Think before unfolding. Every prompt has at least one counterintuitive twist.

  1. Q1. 一家公司从银行借入 1 亿元用于偿还应付账款。这笔操作对 $A$、$L$、$E$ 各有何影响? 进一步: 这是公司的结构改善还是结构恶化? Q1. A firm borrows ¥100m from a bank to pay down accounts payable. What happens to $A$, $L$, and $E$? And: is this structural improvement or deterioration?

    借款时: $\Delta A = +100$ (银行存款), $\Delta L = +100$ (短期借款), $\Delta E = 0$。
    还应付时: $\Delta A = -100$ (银行存款), $\Delta L = -100$ (应付账款), $\Delta E = 0$。
    净: $\Delta A = 0, \Delta L = 0, \Delta E = 0$。

    结构上是恶化: 应付账款是无息商业信用 (供应商给的免费短期资金), 短期借款是付息债。把无息债换成付息债, 资金成本上升, 净利润减少。看年报负债内部结构 (应付 vs 短期借款) 变化, 比看 D/A 总数更重要。

    On borrowing: $\Delta A = +100$ (cash), $\Delta L = +100$ (ST debt), $\Delta E = 0$.
    On paying down AP: $\Delta A = -100$ (cash), $\Delta L = -100$ (AP), $\Delta E = 0$.
    Net: nothing moves at the aggregate level.

    Structurally a deterioration: AP is a free, zero‑coupon supplier credit; ST debt is interest‑bearing. Swapping the former for the latter raises cost of funds and depresses earnings. The shift inside L matters more than the unchanged D/A.

  2. Q2. 公司用 5,000 万元自有资金回购并注销股份。怎么记账? 对每股净资产 (BVPS) 是上升还是下降? Q2. A firm buys back and cancels ¥50m of its own shares. How is it recorded? Does book value per share go up or down?

    分录 (简化): 借 库存股 / 减少实收资本与盈余 5,000   贷 银行存款 5,000。
    $\Delta A = -5{,}000$, $\Delta L = 0$, $\Delta E = -5{,}000$。等式保持。

    BVPS 上升 or 下降, 取决于回购价 vs 每股账面净资产: 假设回购前 $E = 10$ 亿, 总股本 1 亿股 → BVPS = 10 元。
    若用 5,000 万买回 1,000 万股 (回购价 5 元 < BVPS), 注销后 $E = 9.5$ 亿, 股本 0.9 亿 → BVPS ≈ 10.56 元, 上升。
    若回购价 = 15 元 > BVPS, 注销后 BVPS 反而下降。
    关键: 回购本身不创造价值, 但能以"低于账面价"买回时, 留下来的股东账面权益被动放大。

    Entry (simplified): Dr Treasury stock / Reduce share capital & retained earnings 50m   Cr Cash 50m. $\Delta A = -50, \Delta L = 0, \Delta E = -50$ — identity holds.

    BVPS direction depends on buyback price vs current BVPS. If $E=¥1$bn over 100m shares (BVPS = ¥10), buying 10m shares at ¥5 each (below BVPS) leaves $E = ¥950$m over 90m shares → BVPS ≈ ¥10.56, up. Buying at ¥15 (above BVPS) leaves BVPS down. Buyback creates no value; below‑book repurchases mechanically lift remaining shareholders' book equity.

  3. Q3. 公司收到客户预付款 200 万元 (合同尚未履约)。用 $A = L + E$ 推导: 为什么这 200 万元不能立即确认为收入? Q3. A firm receives a ¥2m customer prepayment (contract not yet performed). Use $A = L + E$ to show why it cannot be booked as revenue.

    正确分录: 借 银行存款 200   贷 合同负债 200。$\Delta A=+200, \Delta L=+200, \Delta E=0$。

    若直接确认收入: 借 银行存款 200   贷 主营业务收入 200, 则 $\Delta E = +200$。但合同尚未履约 — 这意味着公司还欠客户一次未来的服务 — 那就有未承担的负债, 不应该体现为净资产增加。

    极端测试: 第二天客户取消订单要求退款, 已"确认"的 200 万必须冲回, 净利润前后失真。这违反 accrual 与 matching: 收入必须在履约义务被满足时才确认, 而不是收钱时 (Ch.05 收入确认五步法详述)。

    Correct entry: Dr Cash 200   Cr Contract liability 200. $\Delta A=+200, \Delta L=+200, \Delta E=0$.

    If you booked it as revenue: Dr Cash 200   Cr Revenue 200, then $\Delta E = +200$. But the contract is unperformed — the firm owes a future service to the customer — so there's an unrecognized obligation; net assets shouldn't rise.

    Stress test: if the customer cancels next day, the "recognized" 200 must reverse — distorting net income both periods. This violates accrual + matching: revenue is recognized on satisfaction of the performance obligation, not on cash receipt (Ch.05 develops the 5‑step model).

  4. Q4. 折旧不引起任何现金流出, 为何还要在利润表上确认 (因而拉低 net income)? 用 $A = L + E$ 推导这一处理的必要性。 Q4. Depreciation involves no cash outflow. Why must it still hit the income statement (reducing net income)? Derive the necessity from $A = L + E$.

    假设买 100 万设备, 使用 5 年, 残值 0:

    • 买入: 借 固定资产 100 贷 银行存款 100 → $\Delta A = 0$。
    • 第 1 年末若不计折旧: 资产端"固定资产"仍记 100, 但实际经济价值已是约 80。$A$ 被高估 20。等式右边 ($L + E$) 不变 → 矛盾。要么 $A$ 也下降, 要么 $E$ 上升 — 都不符合事实。
    • 正确处理: 借 折旧费用 20 → 当期净利 -20 → $E$ -20; 贷 累计折旧 20 → 固定资产账面 -20 → $A$ -20。$dA = -20 = dE$, 恒等式重新平衡。

    结论: 折旧不是为了"现金", 是为了在 matching 原则下把"使用资源"翻译成"费用", 防止 $A$ 与 $E$ 同时被系统性高估。Ch.09 现金流量表深度解释: 在间接法 OCF 里折旧被加回, 不是因为它"创造现金", 而是因为它本就不消耗现金, 但已经在净利里扣过, 需要还回去。

    Buy ¥1m of equipment, 5‑year life, zero salvage:

    • Purchase: Dr PPE 100 Cr Cash 100 → $\Delta A = 0$.
    • If you don't depreciate at year 1 end: PPE still records 100 while real economic value is ~80. $A$ is overstated by 20. But the RHS ($L + E$) hasn't moved → contradiction with reality.
    • Correct: Dr Depr. exp. 20 → NI ↓ 20 → $E$ ↓ 20; Cr Accum. depr. 20 → PPE book value ↓ 20 → $A$ ↓ 20. $dA = -20 = dE$ — identity rebalances.

    Bottom line: depreciation isn't about cash; it's the matching‑principle translation of "resource consumed" into "expense," preventing systematic overstatement of both $A$ and $E$. Ch.09 deepens: depreciation is added back in indirect‑method OCF not because it "creates cash" but because it never consumed cash, yet net income already subtracted it.

  5. Q5. 永续债 (perpetual bond) 可能被分类为负债或权益, 取决于条款。同样一笔 100 亿融资, 两种分类对初始 $A = L + E$ 等式有无影响? 对后续 ROE 和 D/A 比率呢? Q5. A perpetual bond can be classified as liability or equity depending on terms. For the same ¥10bn issuance, does the initial $A = L + E$ change? What about ROE and D/A afterwards?

    初始恒等式: 两种分类下 $\Delta A = +100$ 亿都成立, 等式右边分别配 $\Delta L = +100$ 或 $\Delta E = +100$ — 恒等式都满足。从 $A=L+E$ 看不出差别。

    后续影响:

    • D/A = L/A: 计入权益时 D/A 不变; 计入负债时 D/A 上升 (设原 $A = 2{,}820, L = 484$, 加 100 亿则 D/A 从 17.2% → 20%)。账面杠杆差异 ~3 个百分点。
    • ROE = NI / E: 计入权益时分母 $E$ 变大 → ROE 拉低; 计入负债时分母不变, 但利息费用 (假设 4%) 每年扣减 $4$ 亿税前利润 → 净利下降 → ROE 同样下降, 渠道是分子。
    • 债务利息可税前扣除, 股息不能: 计入负债的版本有税盾 (M2 Ch.03 MM 定理含税版本展开)。所以"计入权益更好看"是表象, 实际上放弃了税盾。

    实际意义: 在中国大量基建/电力公司 (如中国电建 601669) 用永续债压低账面 D/A。分析师做"调整后杠杆"时, 通常把永续债从权益移回负债。Ch.07 详述判断标准 (强制赎回、利率重置、 step‑up 等)。

    Initial identity: either way $\Delta A = +10$ bn matches $\Delta L = +10$ or $\Delta E = +10$ — the identity is silent on the choice.

    Downstream:

    • D/A: unchanged under equity classification; rises under debt (here from 17.2% to ~20%).
    • ROE = NI / E: equity classification inflates the denominator → ROE down; debt classification keeps $E$ but adds ~¥400m interest expense → ROE down through the numerator. Same direction, different channel.
    • Tax shield: interest is tax‑deductible, dividend‑style coupons are not. Equity classification gives up the tax shield (developed in M2 Ch.03 with taxes).

    In practice, many Chinese infrastructure / utility issuers (e.g. Sinohydro 601669) use perpetuals to depress reported leverage. Analysts often reclassify them back to debt for an "adjusted D/A." Ch.07 develops the classification criteria (mandatory redemption, step‑ups, coupon deferrability).
