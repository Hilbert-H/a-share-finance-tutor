会计学 — 模块导览 Accounting — Module Overview

财报是一套描述企业状态与转移的语言。学完本模块, 你能把任意 A 股年报看成系统的状态向量。 Financial statements are a language for describing a firm's state and its transitions. After this module, an A‑share annual report reads as a system's state vector.

学习目标 Objectives
  • 用 $A = L + E$ 一致性检查任何一份 A 股资产负债表。
  • 读懂三大报表的勾稽: 净利→OCF→期末现金, 经营/投资/筹资三大活动如何拼接出现金净变动。
  • 把权责发生制 (accrual)、配比 (matching)、实质重于形式 (substance over form) 三原则套到具体科目上。
  • 分辨 CAS / IFRS / US GAAP 在商誉、R&D、租赁、收入确认上的关键差异。
  • 在附注里定位关联交易、或有事项、期后事项三类高风险披露。
  • Use $A = L + E$ to consistency‑check any A‑share balance sheet.
  • Articulate how net income flows into operating cash flow, and how the three activities reconstruct net change in cash.
  • Apply accrual, matching, and substance‑over‑form to concrete line items.
  • Distinguish CAS / IFRS / US GAAP on goodwill, R&D, leases, and revenue.
  • Locate related‑party transactions, contingencies, and subsequent events in the notes.

First-Principles 思维模型 First‑Principles Mental Models

本模块所有结论都可由这九条 mental models 推出。把它们装进脑子, 比记 200 条会计科目更省力。 All conclusions in this module follow from these nine mental models. Loading them into your head is cheaper than memorizing 200 GL accounts.

i

复式记账对称性 (Double‑Entry Symmetry)Double‑Entry Symmetry

任何经济事件都有借贷两侧, 总和为零。类比物理守恒律: 能量、动量、电荷皆守恒, 会计中价值也守恒。

Every event has a debit and a credit, summing to zero. Analogy: conservation of energy / momentum / charge — value, too, is conserved.

ii

权责发生制 vs 收付实现制 (Accrual vs Cash)Accrual vs Cash Basis

两种描述时间维度的方式。Accrual 把"经济意义已发生但现金未动"也记下来; Cash 只看现金。类比物理: 一个是基于事件的描述, 一个是基于物质流动的描述。

Two timelines. Accrual records economic events whose cash hasn't yet moved; cash basis records only cash. Like event‑based vs flow‑based descriptions in physics.

iii

实质重于形式 (Substance over Form)Substance over Form

看 control 与 risk‑reward 的真实分布, 不看合同文字。售后回租, 名义上"出售再租回", 实质仍是融资 — 计入负债, 不出表。

Look at the real distribution of control and risk‑reward, not the contract text. Sale‑and‑leaseback may legally transfer ownership but economically is still financing — keep it on the balance sheet.

iv

配比原则 (Matching)Matching

收入与产生它的费用必须同期确认。否则就出现"卖得很猛, 但 inventory 永远跟不上销量"的反常曲线。

Revenues match the expenses that generated them. Without matching you get anomalies like soaring revenue with permanently lagging inventory write‑offs.

v

谨慎性 (Conservatism)Conservatism

坏消息要立即承认 (减值、坏账), 好消息要等到实现才确认 (不预期未实现利得)。一个有偏的估计器, 但稳定。

Recognize losses early (impairments, bad debt), recognize gains only on realization. A biased but stable estimator.

vi

历史成本 vs 公允价值 (Historical Cost vs Fair Value)Historical Cost vs Fair Value

客观可验证 vs 与决策相关 — 永恒的张力。2008 金融危机后这场争论从未结束。

Verifiable vs decision‑relevant — a permanent tension. The debate hasn't settled since 2008.

vii

控制 (Control)Control

合并范围的核心标尺。不看股权比例, 看可变回报 + 重大影响 + 协议安排的综合实质。

The criterion for consolidation. Not equity %, but the combined reality of variable returns, significant influence, and contractual arrangements.

viii

信号 (Signaling)Signaling

自愿披露的博弈视角。公司选择披露什么、藏什么本身就是信号。

A game‑theoretic view of voluntary disclosure. What a firm chooses to disclose — and to hide — is itself the signal.

ix

信息含量 (Information Content)Information Content

报表为何被披露? 给资本市场提供风险定价所需的边际信息。

Why disclose at all? To supply the capital market with the marginal information needed for risk pricing.

专家最激烈的分歧 Where Experts Disagree

分歧Dispute 商誉: 减值测试 vs 摊销 Goodwill: Impairment Testing vs Amortization
IFRS / CAS — Impairment only
不定期摊销, 仅做减值测试No amortization; impairment only

商誉无法分摊期间, 强行摊销是任意。每年做减值测试更能反映真实价值。

There's no natural period to amortize over; forced amortization is arbitrary. Annual impairment better reflects current value.

FASB (pre‑2001) / 当下重启讨论
定期摊销 (10‑40 年)Periodic amortization (10–40 years)

减值测试主观性极强, 管理层有动机推迟确认。摊销规则更难操纵。A 股 2018‑19 商誉雷潮就是这种主观性的代价。

Impairment is highly subjective and management can defer. Amortization is harder to manipulate. The 2018–19 A‑share goodwill cliff was the cost of subjectivity.

现状State IFRS/CAS 仍走减值测试; FASB 2014 简化后讨论是否对私营公司恢复摊销; A 股目前的实践被多次批评制造"业绩洗大澡"机会。 IFRS/CAS still impairment‑only; FASB since 2014 has discussed restoring amortization for private companies; A‑share practice has been repeatedly criticized for enabling "big bath" maneuvers.
分歧Dispute 公允价值 vs 历史成本 Fair Value vs Historical Cost
Fair value (FAS 157, IFRS 13)
与决策相关 (decision relevance)Decision relevance

市场价格才是投资者关心的。把按市价反应的衍生品损益写出来, 让风险无处藏。

Market price is what investors care about. Marking derivatives to market exposes risk.

Historical cost — pro‑cyclicality critique
客观可验证, 不放大顺周期Verifiable; doesn't amplify procyclicality

2008 危机时, mark‑to‑market 把恐慌的市场价直接搬到资产负债表上, 触发更多卖盘 — 放大下跌。

In 2008, mark‑to‑market dragged panicked prices straight into balance sheets, triggering forced sales — amplifying the crash.

现状State 混合模型 (mixed‑attribute) 成为主流: 交易性资产用公允, 持有至到期/应收用摊余成本, 自用资产用历史成本。 Mixed‑attribute models dominate: trading assets at fair value, HTM/loans at amortized cost, owner‑occupied at historical cost.
分歧Dispute 研发支出: 资本化 vs 费用化 R&D: Capitalize vs Expense
IFRS / CAS 6 — 分阶段
研究全费用, 开发可资本化Research expensed; development capitalized

能可靠计量、有技术可行性、有未来经济利益时资本化。理论上更贴合资产定义。

Capitalize once technical feasibility and future economic benefit are established. Closer to the asset definition.

US GAAP — 一律费用化
研发全部费用化 (软件少数例外)All R&D expensed (with narrow software exceptions)

未来经济利益太难验证, 资本化等于给管理层调利润的工具。一刀切更稳健。

Future benefits are too speculative; capitalization gives management a lever. Blanket expensing is more conservative.

现状State A 股大量公司有研发资本化的诱因 (拉高短期净利、做高净资产)。看附注中"开发支出"科目余额变化与利润率是否同步异常, 是基本检验。 A‑share filers face strong incentives to capitalize (boost near‑term net income & book equity). Check whether the "development expenditure" balance moves together with margin anomalies.
分歧Dispute 永续债: 负债还是权益? Perpetual Debt: Liability or Equity?
分类为权益 (中国大量实务)
无强制赎回, 计入权益No mandatory redemption → equity

条款允许发行人无限期递延付息, 没有合同义务; 形式上与永久股本相似。

Issuer can perpetually defer coupons; no contractual obligation; formally resembles permanent equity.

分类为负债 (经济实质派)
实质仍是融资工具Substantively still debt

利率重置条款 (step‑up) 让递延付息变成自残行为, 实际上有"软"赎回义务。把这种工具记入权益拉低账面 D/E, 是会计套利。

Coupon step‑ups make deferral self‑destructive — there's a "soft" obligation. Classifying these as equity to depress book D/E is accounting arbitrage.

现状State 中国电建 601669、中国铁建 601186 等大量基建公司用永续债拉低账面资产负债率。第 06 章会展开。 Sinohydro 601669, China Railway Construction 601186 and many infrastructure issuers use perpetuals to depress reported leverage. Chapter 06 expands on this.
